About me



Ragnar Bøe Equipment Rental is a specialized service dedicated to supporting film and media productions by offering professional-grade equipment at flexible rates. Our goal is to ensure that independent filmmakers, small production teams, and other creative professionals have access to high-quality film gear, regardless of budget constraints. We believe that every project deserves to be shot with the best tools available, and our unique pay-what-you-can model allows productions to access top-tier equipment while working within their financial limitations. Whether you're working on a small indie film or a creative project with limited resources, we're here to make sure you have the tools you need to bring your vision to life.
