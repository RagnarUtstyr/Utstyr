Avenger Triple Riser Combo Stand



Inventory

3 Available



The Avenger Triple Riser Combo Stand is a heavy-duty support stand built to accommodate large lighting fixtures and other equipment on set. With its three riser sections, the stand can be extended to impressive heights, offering maximum flexibility for rigging lights, modifiers, and other gear in professional film, television, and photography production.

The Combo Stand is constructed from durable chrome-plated steel, providing exceptional strength and stability. It comes equipped with a junior 1-1/8" (28mm) receiver and baby 5/8" (16mm) spigot, allowing for compatibility with a wide range of lighting fixtures and accessories. The triple risers offer smooth adjustments and can be locked in place securely, making this stand an ideal choice for large setups.

With a maximum height of 4.4 meters (14.5 feet) and a sturdy wide base, the Avenger Triple Riser Combo Stand is capable of supporting equipment weighing up to 30 kg (66 lbs). Despite its large size, it is designed for easy transport and fast setup, with foldable legs that make it practical for location shoots.



Specifications

  • Model: Avenger Triple Riser Combo Stand
  • Material: Chrome-plated steel
  • Height Range: 1.65 meters to 4.4 meters (5.4 feet to 14.5 feet)
  • Spigot: 1-1/8" (28mm) junior receiver and 5/8" (16mm) baby pin
  • Weight Capacity: 30 kg (66 lbs)
  • Base Diameter: 1.28 meters (4.2 feet)
  • Weight: 11 kg (24.3 lbs)
  • Risers: 3 risers for adjustable height


Key Features

  • Triple riser design for flexible height adjustments
  • Durable chrome-plated steel construction
  • Supports up to 30 kg (66 lbs) of equipment
  • Compatible with 1-1/8" junior receiver and 5/8" baby pin spigot
  • Wide base for increased stability
  • Foldable legs for easy transport and setup
  • Perfect for large lighting setups and heavy equipment
