The DZOfilm Catta Ace 70-135mm T2.9 is a professional-grade cinema zoom lens designed for high-end filmmaking and videography. It offers a 70-135mm focal length, providing a telephoto zoom range with a constant T2.9 aperture, perfect for capturing stunning close-ups, portraits, and detailed shots with beautiful background separation. The lens is compatible with both PL and EF mounts (with an adapter), making it versatile for a wide range of camera systems.

Designed for Super 35mm sensors, the Catta Ace 70-135mm offers high-resolution images with excellent sharpness and color rendering. Its parfocal design ensures that the focus remains intact when zooming, while minimal focus breathing allows for smooth transitions and an overall cinematic feel. The 11-blade iris creates a soft and smooth bokeh, ideal for selective focus and artistic depth of field control.

The lens is built with cinema-standard gears for focus, iris, and zoom, allowing for easy integration with follow focus systems and lens control accessories. With its lightweight and durable construction, the Catta Ace 70-135mm is suitable for both handheld shooting and use with gimbals or drones, providing flexibility for any production environment.
