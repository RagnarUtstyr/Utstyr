BNC 90 Degree Angle



The BNC 90 Degree Angle Adapter allows for secure BNC connections in tight spaces where a straight connector would be impractical. Perfect for cable routing behind equipment racks, field monitors, or in camera rigs where space is limited.

This right-angle adapter preserves signal integrity and helps prevent cable strain or damage caused by sharp bends. It’s ideal for SDI video, timecode, or RF signal paths on professional broadcast or cinema gear.

Built with a durable metal housing and gold-plated contacts, the adapter provides a solid and reliable connection in studio or location setups.



Specifications

  • Connector Type: BNC Male to BNC Female
  • Angle: 90 Degrees
  • Impedance: 75 Ohm (SDI compatible)
  • Use Case: SDI, Timecode, RF, Video
  • Build: Nickel-plated or anodized metal body
  • Contact: Gold-plated center pin


Key Features

  • 90-degree adapter for BNC signal routing in tight spaces
  • Ideal for SDI video and timecode connections
  • Reduces cable stress and improves rig cable management
  • Durable all-metal construction for field use
  • Maintains high signal integrity for video/audio transmission
