Behringer P2 Ultra-Compact Personal In-Ear Monitor Amplifier



Inventory

1 Available



The Behringer P2 is an ultra-compact and durable personal in-ear monitor amplifier designed for performers and recording artists who need precise, high-quality monitoring on stage or in the studio. The P2 offers XLR/TRS input compatibility, providing flexible options for connecting to both balanced and unbalanced sources. With its high-power headphone output and volume control, the P2 allows you to monitor your mix with clarity and detail.

Featuring mono/stereo operation, the Behringer P2 can be easily switched to suit your monitoring needs. It is equipped with a locking XLR/TRS input to prevent accidental disconnection during performances. The belt clip allows for easy, hands-free use, making it a perfect solution for musicians, audio engineers, and presenters who require mobility on stage or in the studio.

The Behringer P2 runs on two standard AAA batteries, providing up to 12 hours of continuous use. Its rugged design and lightweight construction make it ideal for portable use in demanding environments, offering reliable performance for long sessions and live gigs.



Specifications

  • Model: Behringer P2 Ultra-Compact Personal In-Ear Monitor Amplifier
  • Input: XLR/TRS combo jack (balanced or unbalanced)
  • Output: 3.5mm headphone jack
  • Power: 2 x AAA batteries (up to 12 hours of use)
  • Operation: Mono/Stereo switchable
  • Controls: Volume control knob
  • Belt Clip: Yes
  • Dimensions: 92 x 74 x 42 mm
  • Weight: 0.18 kg


Key Features

  • Ultra-compact personal in-ear monitor amplifier
  • Compatible with XLR/TRS inputs (balanced or unbalanced)
  • Mono/Stereo operation for versatile monitoring
  • Powered by 2 AAA batteries with up to 12 hours of continuous use
  • High-power headphone output with volume control
  • Locking XLR/TRS input to prevent accidental disconnection
  • Belt clip for hands-free, portable use
  • Durable and lightweight design, ideal for live and studio use
