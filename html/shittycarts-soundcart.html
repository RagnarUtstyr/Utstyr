Soundcart handmade by Ragnar Bøe.



The cart is made with a top and middle shelf for storing a sound bag, along with a foldout table on the right-hand side for extra work surface and a bottom shelf for larger boxes. Designed with mobility and efficiency in mind, it provides ample storage and convenient access to essential audio gear.

It also features 19" rack mounting equipped with 2x 6.3" jack outputs and 6x XLR inputs, allowing quick connections for microphones and other audio devices. A rackmounted drawer and an additional rackmounted shelf offer secure, easily accessible storage for smaller accessories.

Two cheeseplates offer mounting points for monitors or tablets, and there is a boom holder as well as a telescopic stand for antennas, accompanied by two 5m BNC cables. The cart also includes a V-mount battery plate that powers integrated lights on the middle shelf and inside the rack drawer, each with its own separate switch. Additionally, there are 3x USB 5V outputs for charging tablets or phones.



Specifications

  • Shelves: Top and middle shelf for sound bag; bottom shelf for larger boxes
  • Foldout Table: Right-hand side extension for extra workspace
  • Rack Mounting: 19" rack setup with quick connections
  • Connectivity: 2x 6.3" jack outputs and 6x XLR inputs
  • Storage Solutions: Rackmounted drawer and additional shelf
  • Mounting Options: Two cheeseplates for monitors or tablets
  • Boom & Antennas: Boom holder and telescopic stand with 2x 5m BNC cables
  • Power: V-mount battery plate with separate light switches for shelf and drawer
  • USB Outputs: 3x USB 5V ports for device charging


Key Features

  • Top, middle, and bottom shelves to accommodate various audio gear and larger boxes
  • Foldout side table for added workspace on location
  • 19" rack mounting with multiple input/output options for quick microphone connections
  • Rackmounted drawer and extra shelf for organized accessory storage
  • Two cheeseplates for versatile monitor or tablet mounting
  • Boom holder and telescopic antenna stand for extended audio capabilities
  • Built-in V-mount battery plate powering dedicated lights on shelves and in the drawer
  • Three USB outputs (5V) for convenient mobile device charging
