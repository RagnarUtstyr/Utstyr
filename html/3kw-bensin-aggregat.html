3Kw Gasoline Generator



Inventory

1 Available



Single-cylinder, four-stroke gasoline-powered generator with synchronous generator. It features a pull-start and can power electronic equipment via a 230V outlet, as well as charge batteries through a 12V outlet. This generator is recommended for large single-phase machines but is not suitable for sensitive electronics, such as computers.

It runs best on four-stroke gasoline, but can also be operated with 95-octane fuel.
