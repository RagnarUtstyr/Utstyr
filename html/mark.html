The Nucleus-M Marking Disk is an essential accessory for the Nucleus-M follow focus system. Designed to provide precise and clear marking of focus points, this disk allows operators to accurately mark focus distances, ensuring smooth and consistent focus pulls. Whether you're working on set or in a studio, the marking disk offers a practical solution for visualizing focus marks on the lens, helping the camera operator and focus puller work more efficiently and effectively.

Crafted from durable materials, the Nucleus-M Marking Disk is built to withstand the rigors of professional filmmaking while maintaining its clarity and precision. Its universal design allows it to fit various lenses and follow focus setups, making it compatible with a wide range of camera equipment.

The disk features clear and easily visible markers, providing accurate reference points for focus pulls. With a simple and effective design, the Nucleus-M Marking Disk makes it easier for filmmakers to achieve precise focus control during fast-paced shooting scenarios.
