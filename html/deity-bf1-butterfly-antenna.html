Deity BF-1 Butterfly Antenna



Inventory

1 Available



The Deity BF-1 Butterfly Antenna is a specialized directional antenna designed for use with wireless audio systems, providing enhanced signal reception and increased range for professional sound recording. Featuring a foldable butterfly design, the BF-1 is compact, portable, and easy to set up, making it ideal for use on location or in studios.

The BF-1 operates in a wide 470-700 MHz frequency range, allowing it to capture strong and stable signals across a variety of wireless devices. Its directional design ensures that it focuses on signals from a specific direction, helping to reduce interference from unwanted sources and improving overall sound quality.

Built with durability in mind, the Deity BF-1 features a rugged yet lightweight construction, ensuring long-lasting performance in any production environment. Its BNC connector allows easy integration with wireless receivers and transmitters, providing a reliable signal feed for professional sound recording setups.



Specifications

  • Model: Deity BF-1 Butterfly Antenna
  • Frequency Range: 470-700 MHz
  • Type: Directional antenna
  • Connector: BNC
  • Dimensions (Folded): 33 x 33 cm (13 x 13 inches)
  • Dimensions (Unfolded): 46 x 46 cm (18 x 18 inches)
  • Weight: 0.8 kg (1.7 lbs)


Key Features

  • Directional antenna for improved signal reception and range
  • Wide frequency range (470-700 MHz) for compatibility with multiple wireless systems
  • Foldable butterfly design for easy transport and storage
  • BNC connector for simple integration with wireless receivers and transmitters
  • Rugged, lightweight construction for durability and portability
  • Ideal for location sound recording or studio use
