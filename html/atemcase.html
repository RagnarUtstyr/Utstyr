Blackmagic ATEM Mini Pro Case



Inventory

This Blackmagic ATEM Mini Pro Case is a rugged Pelicase build that brings portable live production to any location. A 7″ monitor is mounted inside the lid for clear and immediate viewing of program output or multiview, and all essential connectors (HDMI, LAN, USB, audio) are built into the exterior for quick and easy setup.

Ideal for compact broadcast setups, on-location streaming events, or mobile productions, this case protects your ATEM Mini Pro while offering a self-contained solution. The result is minimal fuss in rigging, efficient cable management, and rapid deployment so you can focus on delivering a professional live production.
