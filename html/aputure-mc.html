Aputure MC LED Light



Inventory

4 Available



The Aputure MC is a compact, RGBWW LED light that offers full-color control, making it a versatile and portable lighting solution for filmmakers and photographers. It features HSL color control and CCT control with a 3200K to 6500K adjustable bi-color temperature range. Despite its small size, the MC delivers accurate and powerful light output with CRI and TLCI ratings of 96+, ensuring exceptional color fidelity in any situation.

The Aputure MC is fully compatible with the Sidus Link App, allowing wireless control over brightness, color, and lighting effects. It also includes nine built-in lighting effects, such as Paparazzi, Fireworks, Lightning, Faulty Bulb, TV, Pulse, Fire, Party, and Cop Car, giving you a variety of creative options for different shooting scenarios. The MC's magnetic back allows for easy mounting on metal surfaces, adding to its portability and convenience.

The Aputure MC features built-in battery power with a runtime of up to 120 minutes at full brightness, making it ideal for quick, on-the-go lighting setups. It charges via USB-C and is also Qi wireless charging compatible. This makes the MC a highly portable and flexible light, perfect for use in tight spaces or as part of a larger lighting setup.
