20.000mAh Powerbank

This 20.000mAh powerbank is a high-capacity portable power solution for keeping your cameras, phones, tablets, or USB-powered accessories running all day. It's perfect for location shoots, travel kits, or emergency backup power in the field.

With dual USB-A outputs and one USB-C PD port, it supports simultaneous charging for multiple devices. The USB-C Power Delivery (PD) output offers fast charging for compatible devices, including modern mirrorless cameras, mobile phones, and recorders.

Durable and slim, this powerbank fits easily in a camera bag or pouch and includes LED indicators for real-time battery status monitoring. Recharge it overnight and you’re ready to go for a full day on set or location.



Specifications

  • Capacity: 20.000mAh / 74Wh
  • Output Ports: 2x USB-A, 1x USB-C PD
  • USB-C Output: 5V/3A, 9V/2A, 12V/1.5A (PD up to 18W)
  • USB-A Output: 5V/2.4A (shared)
  • Input: USB-C (5V/2A, 9V/2A)
  • Recharging Time: ~6 hours via USB-C PD
  • Dimensions: Approx. 14 x 7 x 2.5 cm
  • Weight: Approx. 360g


Key Features

  • High-capacity 20.000mAh battery
  • USB-C PD fast charging output
  • Dual USB-A ports for multi-device charging
  • Compact and travel-friendly design
  • LED indicators for battery level
  • Essential for location shoots, travel, and on-set use
