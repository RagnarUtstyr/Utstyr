Kupo KS-048 Junior Wall Plate with Junior Receiver



Inventory

3 Available



The Kupo KS-048 Junior Wall Plate is a versatile mounting solution that allows for secure attachment of large lighting fixtures or grip equipment to walls, ceilings, or other flat surfaces. Equipped with a built-in Junior 1-1/8" receiver, this wall plate is ideal for supporting heavy-duty lights and rigging gear used in film, television, or photography studios.

The Junior receiver accommodates 1-1/8" pins, making it compatible with a wide range of C-stands, light stands, and other grip equipment. Its heavy-duty steel construction ensures stability and durability, while the pre-drilled mounting holes make it easy to install securely on various surfaces.

The Kupo KS-048 Junior Wall Plate is a must-have for professional lighting setups, allowing you to mount fixtures in unique positions and maximize space on set or in the studio.



Specifications

  • Model: Kupo KS-048 Junior Wall Plate
  • Receiver: 1-1/8" (28mm) Junior Receiver
  • Material: Heavy-duty steel
  • Dimensions: 13 x 13 x 12 cm (5.1 x 5.1 x 4.7 inches)
  • Weight Capacity: 100 kg (220 lbs)
  • Mounting Holes: Pre-drilled for easy installation
  • Weight: 1.8 kg (4 lbs)


Key Features

  • Junior 1-1/8" receiver for large lighting fixtures and grip equipment
  • Heavy-duty steel construction for durability and stability
  • Pre-drilled holes for secure mounting on walls, ceilings, or flat surfaces
  • Supports up to 100 kg (220 lbs) of weight
  • Compatible with a wide range of stands and rigging gear
  • Ideal for maximizing space in studios or on set
