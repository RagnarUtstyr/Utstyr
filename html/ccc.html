The Camera Comfort Cushion is a padded shoulder accessory designed to enhance comfort during handheld shooting. Whether you're using a cinema camera, ENG-style rig, or DSLR shoulder setup, this cushion reduces strain and fatigue during long shooting days.

It easily attaches to most camera rigs or shoulder mounts via Velcro or straps, providing soft but stable padding between your gear and body. It’s ideal for documentary filmmakers, handheld camera operators, or camera assistants who carry equipment for extended periods.

Built with breathable, high-density foam and covered with rugged, washable fabric, the Camera Comfort Cushion is a lightweight yet durable addition to your kit.



Specifications

  • Type: Shoulder/hip pad for handheld camera support
  • Material: High-density foam with breathable outer fabric
  • Attachment: Hook-and-loop (Velcro) or strap system
  • Compatibility: Most shoulder rigs, camera braces, and body-worn mounts
  • Dimensions: Approx. 25 x 10 x 3 cm
  • Weight: ~150g


Key Features

  • Improves comfort for handheld or shoulder-mounted camera work
  • Breathable and padded design reduces fatigue
  • Universal fit for most shoulder rigs and mounts
  • Lightweight and easily packable
  • Durable construction with washable materials
