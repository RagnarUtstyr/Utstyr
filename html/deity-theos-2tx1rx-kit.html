Deity Theos 2TX1RX Kit



Inventory

3 Available



The Deity Theos 2TX1RX Kit is a professional wireless audio system that includes two transmitters (TX) and one receiver (RX), making it perfect for dual-channel audio setups. It’s designed to deliver broadcast-quality audio for filmmakers, content creators, and sound engineers. The system operates on the UHF spectrum with auto-scan and frequency hopping technology, providing a stable and interference-free signal, even in challenging environments.

Each transmitter (TX) comes equipped with a high-quality lavalier microphone and locking 3.5mm input, ensuring secure connections while recording. The receiver (RX) is compact and can be mounted directly to a camera or sound recorder via a cold shoe mount. The system is designed for simplicity and ease of use, featuring OLED displays on both the transmitters and receiver for real-time monitoring of audio levels, battery life, and signal strength.

With a range of up to 100 meters (328 feet), the Deity Theos is perfect for location shoots, interviews, and dynamic filming environments. The kit is powered by rechargeable lithium-ion batteries, offering up to 10 hours of continuous operation, making it ideal for long shooting days. The system also includes USB-C ports for firmware updates, ensuring the kit stays current with the latest features and improvements.



Specifications

  • Model: Deity Theos 2TX1RX Kit
  • Frequency Range: UHF (470-614 MHz)
  • Transmission Range: Up to 100 meters (328 feet)
  • Transmitter Input: Locking 3.5mm TRS
  • Receiver Output: 3.5mm TRS headphone output
  • Battery Life: Up to 10 hours (rechargeable lithium-ion)
  • Display: OLED screens on TX and RX
  • Dimensions: 10 x 5 x 2.5 cm (3.9 x 2 x 1 inches)
  • Weight: 250g (8.8 oz) per unit
