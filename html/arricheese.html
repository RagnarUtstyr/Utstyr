Cheeseplate for Arri WCU-4



Inventory

The Cheeseplate for Arri WCU-4 is a versatile and essential rigging accessory designed for securing and mounting various camera components in professional film production setups. This cheeseplate is specifically designed to integrate seamlessly with the Arri WCU-4 wireless camera control system, providing mounting points for attaching additional accessories, such as monitors, wireless receivers, and more.

Constructed from durable, high-quality aluminum, the cheeseplate is built to withstand the demands of a professional production environment. Its multiple threaded holes in a grid pattern offer flexibility for various configurations, making it easy to adapt your setup to meet specific requirements. The cheeseplate also features lightweight and compact design, ensuring that it doesn't add unnecessary weight to your rig while maintaining its structural integrity.

Whether you’re on set or location shooting, the Cheeseplate for Arri WCU-4 provides a solid foundation for your equipment, keeping everything organized and secure. This accessory is an ideal solution for filmmakers and camera operators who need flexibility and reliability in their rigging system.
