IDX DUO-C 300 V-Mount Battery



Inventory

2 Available



The Hähnel Procube2 for Canon is a compact and versatile dual-battery charger designed for photographers and videographers who need efficient and reliable power management for Canon DSLR and mirrorless camera batteries. This charger supports simultaneous charging of two Canon LP-E6, LP-E8, or LP-E17 batteries, making it an essential tool for professionals who rely on Canon cameras for continuous shooting and recording sessions.

Featuring a sleek, compact design, the Procube2 is easy to carry, making it ideal for travel and on-location shoots. Its rapid charging technology ensures batteries are ready for use in a short amount of time, while the included LCD display provides real-time feedback on battery charge levels, voltage, and capacity. In addition, the Procube2 supports AA battery charging and offers a USB charging port for additional devices, adding to its versatility.

The Procube2 is a robust and reliable power solution for professionals who use Canon cameras. With its intelligent charging system, the charger optimizes battery performance and ensures long-term battery health, making it an invaluable tool for anyone working in photography or videography.



Specifications

  • Model: Hähnel Procube2 Canon
  • Battery Compatibility: Canon LP-E6, LP-E8, LP-E17
  • Charging Bays: 2 simultaneous charging bays
  • Input Voltage: 100-240V AC, 50/60Hz
  • Charging Current: 1000mA for 1 battery, 500mA for 2 batteries
  • AA Battery Charging: Supports 4 AA batteries
  • USB Output: 2.4A USB port for charging additional devices
  • LCD Display: Provides real-time charging status and battery info
  • Dimensions: 100 x 100 x 75mm
  • Weight: 280g (9.88 oz)

  • Simultaneous charging of two Canon LP-E6, LP-E8, or LP-E17 batteries

  • Compact and portable design, ideal for travel
