White China Silk 12x12



Inventory

1 Available



The White China Silk 12x12 is a diffusion fabric that is especially effective at softening light without significantly reducing its intensity. With only about 1 stop of light loss, it provides excellent diffusion, making it ideal for positioning between lights and talent during filming or photography sessions.



Specifications

  • Material: China Silk
  • Dimensions: 360 cm x 360 cm (12x12 feet)
  • Light Loss: Approximately 1 stop
  • Compatibility: Fits 12x12 Matthews Butterfly Frame (rented separately)
  • Purpose: Diffusion fabric for softening light
