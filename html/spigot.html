This Baby Pin Double-Ended Spigot provides two standard 5/8" (16 mm) mounting surfaces in one convenient adapter. Ideal for lights, flags, monitors, or any other accessory that uses the Baby pin format, this spigot is perfect for quick rigging solutions on set.

Designed to fit securely in grip heads, clamps, or stands, it offers a reliable, wobble-free connection. The double-ended design allows you to mount multiple accessories or quickly switch orientations without re-rigging.

Whether you’re running a compact lighting setup or need a versatile mounting point in a crowded grip environment, this Baby Pin Double-Ended Spigot helps streamline your workflow.
