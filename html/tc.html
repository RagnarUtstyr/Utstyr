The Deity TC-1 Timecode Generator is a professional-grade wireless timecode generator designed for film production, television, and broadcast environments. It is an essential tool for synchronizing audio and video across multiple devices such as cameras, audio recorders, and monitors, ensuring perfect sync throughout the production process.

The TC-1 features Bluetooth 5.0 connectivity, allowing it to wirelessly connect and be controlled via the Deity Sidus Audio App on iOS and Android devices. With a high-precision oscillator that provides accurate timecode generation, the Deity TC-1 ensures seamless syncing over extended shooting periods. Its small, lightweight design allows it to be easily mounted on cameras or audio devices.

The Deity TC-1 offers a wide range of timecode frame rates, supporting 23.98, 24, 25, 29.97, and 30fps, ensuring compatibility with industry-standard workflows. It is powered by an internal rechargeable battery that provides up to 28 hours of continuous operation, making it ideal for long production days. The unit also features an OLED display for easy monitoring of settings and timecode status.
