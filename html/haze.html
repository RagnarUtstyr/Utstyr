Antari HZ350 Hazer



Inventory

1 Available



The Antari HZ350 is a high-performance haze machine designed for professional use in film, theatre, live events, and studio environments. It combines efficiency, control, and portability to produce consistent fine haze that enhances lighting effects and atmosphere without overpowering a set.

With a built-in LCD display and intuitive control menu, the HZ350 allows for simple configuration and operation. It supports both manual operation and DMX control, making it suitable for both fixed installations and on-set flexibility. Its ultra-quiet operation and fast haze production ensure it can be used without disrupting audio recordings or live performances.

The Antari HZ350 uses oil-based haze fluid to create a long-lasting, fine mist that spreads evenly and maintains consistency across a large space. It features an internal compressor, eliminating the need for CO₂ or external air sources.



Specifications

  • Model: Antari HZ350
  • Control: LCD interface, DMX 512, Wireless remote (optional)
  • Fluid Type: Oil-based haze fluid
  • Warm-up Time: Approx. 4 minutes
  • Output: 1,200 cu.ft/min (approx.)
