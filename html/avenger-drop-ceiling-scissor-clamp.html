Avenger C1000 Drop Ceiling Scissor Clamp



Inventory

4 Available



The Avenger C1000 Drop Ceiling Scissor Clamp is a handy and compact solution for securely mounting lighting fixtures and other accessories to the metal grids found in drop ceilings. This lightweight and durable clamp allows for quick and easy attachment to drop ceilings, making it ideal for film, television, and commercial shoots, or any setup that requires overhead lighting.

Designed to fit into standard drop ceiling rails, the Avenger C1000 features a 5/8" baby pin (16mm spigot), which can support a variety of lighting and grip equipment. Its scissor mechanism ensures a tight and secure grip on the ceiling grid, making it suitable for hanging lights, flags, reflectors, or any accessory weighing up to 10kg.

The C1000 Drop Ceiling Scissor Clamp is built from high-quality materials, ensuring that it remains reliable during prolonged use. Its compact size makes it easy to transport and set up, allowing for flexibility on location. With this scissor clamp, you can turn any drop ceiling into a professional-grade lighting setup quickly and efficiently.



Specifications

  • Model: Avenger C1000 Drop Ceiling Scissor Clamp
  • Material: High-quality metal
  • Weight Capacity: 10 kg (22 lbs)
  • Spigot (Baby Pin): 16mm (5/8")
  • Mount Type: Scissor mechanism for drop ceiling rails
  • Weight: 0.15 kg

  • Supports up to 10 kg (22 lbs)

  • Secure and reliable grip on drop ceiling grids
  • Ideal for film, television, and commercial production lighting setups
