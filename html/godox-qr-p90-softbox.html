Godox QR-P90 Quick Release Parabolic Softbox



Inventory

2 Available



The Godox QR-P90 Quick Release Parabolic Softbox is a professional-grade light modifier designed to create soft, even lighting for both studio and location shoots. Its parabolic shape ensures that the light is focused and wraps naturally around the subject, making it ideal for portrait photography, fashion, product shoots, and video productions.

Featuring a 90 cm (35.4 inches) diameter, the QR-P90 provides a large light source that softens shadows and enhances the quality of light. The quick-release mechanism allows for fast setup and breakdown, making it perfect for shoots that require speed and efficiency.

The softbox is constructed with durable, high-quality materials and comes with two layers of diffusers, offering flexibility in light diffusion. It also includes a grid attachment to control light spill and provide more focused lighting when needed. The Bowens mount ensures compatibility with a wide range of lights, including popular LED lights, strobes, and continuous lights.



Specifications

  • Model: Godox QR-P90 Quick Release Parabolic Softbox
  • Diameter: 90 cm (35.4 inches)
  • Shape: Parabolic
  • Mount Type: Bowens mount
  • Diffusers: Two layers of diffusers for soft light
  • Grid: Included for controlling light spill
  • Material: Durable, high-quality fabric and frame
  • Weight: 1.5 kg (3.3 lbs)


Key Features

  • 90 cm (35.4 inches) diameter parabolic softbox for soft, even lighting
  • Quick-release mechanism for fast setup and breakdown
  • Bowens mount for compatibility with various lights
  • Two layers of diffusers for flexible light control
  • Included grid for controlling light spill and focused lighting
  • Durable, high-quality construction for professional use
