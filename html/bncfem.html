BNC Female–Female Adapter



The BNC Female–Female Adapter is a compact, reliable solution for connecting two male BNC cables. It’s commonly used in professional video setups, timecode chains, SDI routing, or RF systems where inline cable extension or loop-out feeds are needed.

Its all-metal construction ensures durability and excellent shielding, preserving signal integrity across SDI, analog video, or sync signals. A must-have for any video tech, broadcast engineer, or AC kit.



Specifications

  • Type: BNC Female to BNC Female
  • Impedance: 75 Ohm
  • Use Case: SDI video, timecode, RF signals
  • Construction: Metal housing with nickel or chrome plating
  • Signal Support: SD, HD, 3G-SDI, analog video


Key Features

  • Connects two male BNC cables together
  • Ideal for SDI patching or timecode loop-through
  • 75 Ohm impedance for broadcast/video standards
  • Compact, durable, and field-proven design
  • Essential adapter for camera and rack setups
