Wind Up Stand



Inventory

1 Available



The Wind Up Stand is a heavy-duty lighting stand designed to safely lift and support large fixtures, frames, or accessories. It features a geared crank handle that allows users to raise equipment smoothly and precisely, even when working alone or with heavy setups.

This stand is ideal for studio or location shoots that require elevation of high-output lights such as HMI, LED panels, or diffusion frames. Equipped with braked wheels and a wide footprint for stability, it’s built to handle the demands of professional grip and lighting departments.

The riser sections are reinforced and chrome-plated for durability, while the wind-up mechanism includes an auto-locking system for safety.



Specifications

  • Type: Crank-operated Wind Up Light Stand
  • Material: Steel and aluminum construction
  • Maximum Height: Approx. 3.7 m (12.1 ft)
  • Minimum Height: Approx. 1.5 m (4.9 ft)
  • Load Capacity: Up to 30 kg (66 lbs)
  • Mount: Combo head (Junior 1-1/8" + Baby 5/8")
  • Wheels: Locking casters included
  • Weight: Approx. 17 kg (37.5 lbs)


Key Features

  • Geared crank handle for safe, controlled elevation
  • Heavy-duty support for large lights and frames
  • Wheeled base with locking brakes for mobility and security
  • Compatible with both Junior and Baby pin receivers
  • Wide footprint and solid construction for maximum stability
