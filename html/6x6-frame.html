Lastolite Skylite Rapid Large Frame - 2x2 meters



Inventory

1 Available



The Lastolite Skylite Rapid Large Frame (2 x 2 meters) is a sturdy aluminum frame designed for demanding lighting control in outdoor environments. It is foldable and rust-resistant, making it ideal for location photographers who need reliable performance in even the toughest weather conditions.

The Skylite Rapid frame works with both Skylite and Skylite Rapid fabrics of the same size. Its lightweight build (only 2.3 kg) allows for easy transport, and the fabrics can be easily clipped on to offer maximum versatility. You can purchase the Skylite Rapid frames, fabrics, and accessories separately or as kits. Additionally, the frame is compatible with original Skylite fabrics as well as Skylite Rapid fabrics.



Specifications

  • Weight: 2.3 kg (2300 g)
  • Dimensions: 200 cm x 200 cm
  • Material: Aluminum (foldable and rust-resistant)
  • Compatible Fabrics: Skylite and Skylite Rapid fabrics
  • Shape: Square
  • Product Category: Lighting Control
  • Type: Accessory
  • Brand: Manfrotto
  • Manufacturer's Part Number: LL LR822
