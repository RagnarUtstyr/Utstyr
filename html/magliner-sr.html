Magliner SR



Inventory

1 Available



The Magliner SR is a heavy-duty, multi-purpose equipment cart, commonly used in the film and television industry for transporting gear such as cameras, lights, audio equipment, and production accessories. Renowned for its rugged durability and versatility, the Magliner SR is a standard choice for grips, camera assistants, and production crews working in fast-paced environments.

Built with a lightweight yet robust aluminum frame, the Magliner SR is designed to handle heavy loads while remaining easy to maneuver. Its modular design allows for customization with various accessories such as shelves, baskets, and racks, enabling users to tailor the cart to their specific needs. It can be used for transporting gear on set, organizing equipment, or even as a mobile workstation.

The Magliner SR is equipped with large pneumatic wheels, providing smooth transport over rough terrain, while the ergonomic handle makes it easy to push and steer, even when fully loaded. When not in use, the cart can be easily folded for compact storage or transport in a vehicle.



Specifications

  • Model: Magliner SR
  • Frame Material: Aluminum
  • Weight Capacity: 450 kg (1000 lbs)
  • Wheels: Pneumatic tires for smooth transport over various terrains
  • Dimensions (L x W x H): 127 x 53 x 96 cm (50 x 21 x 38 inches)
  • Foldable: Yes, for compact storage and transport
  • Weight: 32 kg (70 lbs)


Key Features

  • Heavy-duty aluminum construction for long-lasting durability
  • Supports up to 450 kg (1000 lbs) of equipment
  • Modular design allows for accessory customization (shelves, racks, baskets)

  • Foldable for compact storage and transport
