Aputure B7C LED Bulb



Inventory

8 Available



The Aputure B7C is a smart, battery-powered LED bulb designed for filmmakers and photographers who need versatile and high-quality lighting for their shoots. This 7W RGBWW bulb offers 2700K to 6500K adjustable color temperature, providing flexible lighting options from warm to cool tones. The bulb's high color accuracy is backed by CRI and TLCI ratings of 95+, ensuring your footage has excellent color fidelity.

The Aputure B7C integrates seamlessly with the Sidus Link App, giving users full wireless control over brightness, color, and even advanced effects. With nine built-in lighting effects, including Fireworks, Lightning, Faulty Bulb, TV, Paparazzi, and more, it provides creative flexibility for both practical and atmospheric lighting. Additionally, the B7C can be powered by AC or its internal battery, with a runtime of up to 70 minutes at maximum brightness, making it perfect for both stationary and portable setups.

This bulb features an E26/E27 standard socket, allowing it to fit into most common household and production light fixtures. The Aputure B7C also offers stepless dimming for precision control and flicker-free lighting performance across a variety of frame rates.
