Accsoon CineEye 5G Wireless HDMI Transmitter



Inventory

1 Available



The Accsoon CineEye 5G Wireless HDMI Transmitter is a pocket-sized HD transmitter that delivers high-definition video monitoring via WiFi to up to 4 iOS or Android devices simultaneously. It offers a 100-meter range and 4 hours of operation on a single charge, making it ideal for on-the-go setups. This transmitter supports HDMI input and streams live video to smartphones and tablets, transforming them into portable wireless monitors for your camera.

CineEye 5G supports multiple resolutions up to 1080p/60fps, with low-latency streaming and the ability to configure output resolutions via the Vuer app. With WiFi streaming at up to 10 Mbps, you get reliable performance for smooth monitoring. Built with a milled aluminum body, it ensures durability while remaining lightweight for easy portability.

The device offers advanced video processing with H.264 compression, built-in video scaling, and deinterlacing features. It supports Teradek streaming protocols for seamless integration with platforms like Live:Air. The OLED display with joystick navigation allows easy configuration, and its 2.4/5GHz dual-band WiFi ensures a stable wireless connection.
