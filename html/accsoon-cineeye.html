Accsoon CineEye Wireless Video Transmitter



Inventory

1 Available



The Accsoon CineEye transmits an HDMI signal to phones and tablets, treating them as video receivers and allowing you to use them as real-time video monitors. The CineEye accepts up to a 1080p60 HDMI signal via a full-size HDMI port, and it wirelessly transmits the signal to up to four separate iOS/Android mobile devices simultaneously. The signal is sent over 5 GHz Wi-Fi up to 328 feet line-of-sight with low latency of approximately 60ms.

The CineEye iOS/Android app offers features like false color, peaking, zebra, and histogram, as well as .cube format 3D LUT support, allowing your devices to function as professional video monitors. CineEye can be used for standard monitoring, remote focus pulls, and other on-set applications.

The CineEye is ultra-compact and measures about 3.5 x 2.5 inches. It has a 1/4"-20 mounting thread on the bottom and can be easily mounted on your camera. The aluminum housing ensures durability, and the two integrated antennas fold seamlessly to maintain its compact size. The built-in battery provides approximately four hours of runtime and is rechargeable via a standard 5V/2A USB charger (not included).

The 5 GHz Wi-Fi signal is more resistant to interference than standard Wi-Fi, ensuring optimal reliability. The system will automatically find a clean channel before transmission and lower the streaming bitrate if needed as distance increases to maintain real-time monitoring. Additionally, manual channel selection is available from nine 5 GHz transmission channels.
