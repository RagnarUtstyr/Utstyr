The NiSi Yellow Light Blocker 4x5.65 is a specialized filter designed to remove unwanted yellowish hues and enhance color accuracy in outdoor photography and cinematography. This filter helps balance the color temperature when shooting in environments with warm or artificial light, giving your images a more neutral and natural look. Ideal for outdoor shoots, portrait photography, and any situation where precise color reproduction is necessary.

The Yellow Light Blocker is made of high-quality optical glass that ensures excellent image sharpness and clarity, with no noticeable degradation of image quality. The filter is designed to fit into 4x5.65" matte boxes, making it suitable for use with a wide range of professional cinema cameras and lenses.

With its lightweight and durable construction, this filter is an essential tool for filmmakers and photographers who need to address color shifts caused by natural or artificial lighting. The NiSi Yellow Light Blocker 4x5.65 helps you achieve accurate, natural colors in your footage or stills, even in challenging lighting conditions.
