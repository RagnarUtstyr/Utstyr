Astera ART7 AsteraBox CRMX



Inventory

1 Available



The Astera ART7 AsteraBox CRMX is a wireless DMX interface designed to bridge the gap between lighting control applications and Astera’s wireless LED lights. This compact and powerful tool serves as both a CRMX transmitter and a wireless DMX controller, offering flexibility in controlling your lighting setups. Using the AsteraApp via Bluetooth, the ART7 AsteraBox can wirelessly control your fixtures with precision, making it ideal for professional lighting designers and filmmakers.

The Astera ART7 supports both wired and wireless DMX control, allowing users to connect traditional DMX controllers or use Astera’s app for wireless control. Its CRMX technology (Coded Radio Multiplex) ensures reliable and interference-free wireless transmission up to 300 meters in open areas. It also functions as a DMX node, allowing integration with larger DMX control systems for more complex setups.

With a built-in battery, the ART7 offers up to 20 hours of runtime, making it portable and ideal for on-location shoots or events. The device can be charged via USB-C or standard power supply, offering flexible power options to suit your production needs.



Specifications

  • Model: Astera ART7 AsteraBox CRMX
  • Wireless Protocol: CRMX (Coded Radio Multiplex)
  • DMX Control: Wired and Wireless DMX
  • Wireless Range: Up to 300 meters (in open areas)
  • Control App: AsteraApp via Bluetooth
  • Battery Runtime: Up to 20 hours
  • Charging Options: USB-C or standard power supply
  • Dimensions: 140 x 75 x 30 mm
  • Weight: 300g
