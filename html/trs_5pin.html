TRS → XLR Male 5 pin



This TRS to XLR Male 5-pin cable is designed for use with wireless transmitters, timecode boxes, or audio recorders that output a signal over 3.5mm TRS. It adapts the TRS connection to a standard 5-pin XLR male connector, making it suitable for gear that accepts dual-channel audio or timecode via XLR.

Commonly used in timecode sync setups or dual-mono audio routing, this cable ensures compatibility between portable audio sources and professional cameras or field mixers. Built with robust shielding and quality connectors for clean signal transfer.

Ideal for use with devices such as Tentacle Sync, Zoom F-Series, or external audio gear that requires 5-pin XLR inputs.



Specifications

  • Type: 3.5mm TRS to 5-pin XLR Male
  • Connector 1: 3.5mm TRS (mini-jack)
  • Connector 2: XLR Male 5-pin
  • Cable Length: Short (approx. 20–30 cm)
  • Shielding: Yes, for minimal interference
  • Applications: Timecode sync, stereo audio, dual-channel recorders


Key Features

  • Adapts TRS mini-jack output to XLR 5-pin input
  • Useful for timecode, stereo audio, or dual-channel inputs
  • Compact and durable design for field use
  • Compatible with Tentacle Sync, Zoom, and more
  • Clean signal transmission with minimal noise
