NiSi IRND SE 0.9 Filter



Inventory

1 Available



The NiSi IRND SE 0.9 Filter is a neutral density (ND) filter designed to reduce the amount of light entering the camera by 3 stops. With its soft edge (SE) design, the filter offers a gradual transition of light reduction from the clear area to the denser region, making it ideal for balancing bright skies with darker foregrounds in landscape photography and cinematic productions.

The built-in infrared (IR) technology ensures that color shifts caused by infrared light are minimized, providing accurate color reproduction and a more natural look in both photography and videography. Whether you're shooting in bright outdoor conditions or capturing complex cinematic scenes, the NiSi IRND SE 0.9 filter helps you maintain control over exposure and depth of field.

Constructed from high-quality optical glass, this filter ensures exceptional sharpness and clarity, reducing any potential loss of detail. The NiSi IRND SE 0.9 is designed to fit into 4x5.65" matte boxes, making it a versatile option for use with professional cinema cameras and lenses.



Specifications

  • Model: NiSi IRND SE 0.9
  • Filter Type: Neutral Density (ND) with Infrared protection
  • Optical Density: 0.9 (3 stops)
  • Design: Soft Edge (SE)
  • Size: 4x5.65 inches
  • Material: High-quality optical glass
  • Compatibility: Fits 4x5.65" matte boxes
  • Weight: 0.2 kg (0.44 lbs)


Key Features

  • Neutral density filter with 0.9 density (3-stop reduction)
  • Soft Edge (SE) design for gradual light reduction
  • IR technology to prevent color shifts caused by infrared light
  • Perfect for outdoor photography and cinematography
  • Allows for slower shutter speeds or wider apertures in bright conditions
  • High-quality optical glass for sharpness and clarity
  • Compatible with 4x5.65" matte boxes for professional setups
