Aputure F10 Fresnel



Inventory

1 Available



The Aputure F10 Fresnel is an advanced optical modifier designed to maximize the output of high-powered LED fixtures, such as the Aputure LS 600d Pro. It delivers a precise, adjustable beam angle ranging from 15° to 45°, allowing for versatile lighting control, from tight spotlights to broad washes. The large 10-inch Fresnel lens is designed to create a soft, even beam of light with minimal edge falloff, providing studio-grade lighting quality for filmmakers and photographers.

The dual-lens optical design ensures maximum light efficiency and output, while maintaining excellent color accuracy and quality. With its Bowens Mount compatibility, the F10 Fresnel can be easily attached to a wide variety of LED fixtures, making it a versatile tool for any professional lighting setup. The removable drop-in accessory slot allows for the addition of Aputure's F10 Barndoors for even more precise shaping of the light.

The F10 Fresnel’s industrial build quality is designed for professional use in both studio and field environments. Its rugged construction ensures durability, while its large diameter allows for greater control over the light output, making it an essential tool for any lighting professional.



Specifications

  • Model: Aputure F10 Fresnel
  • Lens Diameter: 10 inches
  • Beam Angle: Adjustable from 15° to 45°
  • Compatibility: Bowens Mount
  • Weight: 3.45 kg (7.6 lbs)
  • Dimensions: 340 mm x 250 mm
  • Max Power Output: 600W (with LS 600d Pro)
  • Accessory Slot: Drop-in for barndoors


Key Features

  • Large 10-inch Fresnel lens for high light output and soft beam quality
  • Adjustable beam angle from 15° to 45° for flexible lighting control
  • Compatible with Bowens Mount lighting fixtures, including Aputure LS 600d Pro
  • Dual-lens optical design for maximum efficiency and minimal edge falloff
  • Rugged construction for professional use in studio and field
  • Removable accessory slot for adding barndoors or other modifiers
