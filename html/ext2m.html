The **Manfrotto Autopole 2m Extension Tube** is an accessory designed to extend the length of the **Manfrotto Autopole**. It allows for greater flexibility when working in larger spaces or when you need to extend the reach of your existing Autopole setup. With a length of 2 meters (6.6 feet), it gives users more versatility in positioning lights, backdrops, and other equipment.

Made from high-quality, lightweight aluminum, the extension tube maintains the strength and durability of the original Autopole, while still being easy to transport. It features a secure locking mechanism to ensure a stable setup. Whether you're working in larger studio environments or on location, the Manfrotto Autopole Extension Tube is the perfect accessory for enhancing your setup.
