Avenger Combo Roller Base 12



Inventory

1 Available



The **Avenger Combo Roller Base 12** is a robust, mobile support solution designed for **heavy-duty lighting and grip equipment**. This roller base provides **exceptional stability** and mobility, making it perfect for on-set use in studios or large production environments where lights or other equipment need to be moved frequently. The **Combo Roller Base 12** features a **chrome-plated steel frame** with a **low-profile design**, ensuring both strength and durability.

Equipped with **braked wheels**, this roller base allows for easy positioning of heavy lighting fixtures or accessories, and the locking brakes ensure stability when the equipment is in place. The **Combo Roller Base** is compatible with **combo stands** and can support **industry-standard spigots and adapters**, allowing for versatile setups. Its **low center of gravity** provides additional stability, making it suitable for use with large modifiers, flags, or heavy lights.

The **Avenger Combo Roller Base 12** is capable of supporting loads up to **70 kg (154 lbs)**, making it a go-to choice for productions that require both flexibility and strength. Whether for lighting, grip accessories, or rigging, the roller base's smooth mobility and durable build ensure it performs reliably in demanding environments.
