Godox CS-65D Lantern



The Godox CS-65D Lantern Softbox is a versatile light modifier that creates soft and even illumination, perfect for lighting up larger areas while minimizing harsh shadows. With its 65cm diameter, the CS-65D spreads light in a 270-degree beam, making it ideal for use in interviews, film sets, or any scenario that requires a wide, diffused light source.

Thanks to its quick setup design, this lantern softbox can be assembled and disassembled in seconds, making it perfect for mobile productions or studios that need rapid flexibility. The Bowens mount adapter ensures compatibility with a wide range of continuous lights and flashes, expanding your creative options.

The included skirt allows for more control over the light spread, enabling users to direct and shape the light precisely where it is needed, reducing spill and enhancing the quality of your lighting setup.



Specifications

  • Model: Godox CS-65D Lantern Softbox
  • Diameter: 65cm (25.6 inches)
  • Light Spread: 270-degree beam angle
  • Mount Type: Bowens mount
  • Material: Durable nylon and aluminum rods
  • Skirt Included: Yes, for directional control
  • Setup Time: Quick setup and breakdown in seconds
  • Weight: 1.2 kg
