Paganini



Inventory

1 Set Available



The “Paganini” set is our Half Basso Block offering, designed for grip departments working on film, TV, or commercial sets. These wooden blocks are ideal for leveling and stabilizing dollies, track, or other heavy equipment on uneven terrain.

Made from durable, seasoned wood, each half block maintains consistent density and grain, ensuring reliable support and minimal splitting. Whether you’re building up a dolly platform, stabilizing stands, or adjusting camera angles on location, these blocks help you set up safely and efficiently.

The half-block design reduces excess weight and footprint, making them easy to carry and stack. From smooth studio floors to challenging outdoor surfaces, “Paganini” blocks provide a versatile and dependable foundation for your rigging and support needs.



Specifications

  • Product Name: “Paganini” Half Basso Block Set
  • Material: Seasoned hardwood (maple or equivalent)
  • Block Dimensions: Approx. 30 x 15 x 10 cm each (12" x 6" x 4")
  • Usage: Leveling dollies, track, stands, and grip setups
  • Design: Half-block for lighter weight and easy stacking
  • Number of Pieces: 2 half-blocks per set


Key Features

  • Sturdy, seasoned wood ideal for supporting heavy loads
  • Half-block design reduces bulk while remaining stable
  • Essential for leveling camera dollies or track on uneven surfaces
  • Compatible with standard grip practices for film and TV productions
  • Convenient size for quick stacking, transport, and repositioning
