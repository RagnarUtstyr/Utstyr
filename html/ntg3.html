Røde NTG3B Shotgun Microphone



Inventory

2 Available



The Røde NTG3B is a premium broadcast-grade shotgun microphone engineered for film, television, and location recording applications. Offering a superb signal-to-noise ratio and a rich, warm sound, this microphone is ideal for capturing dialogue and sound effects with pinpoint accuracy.

Constructed with RF-bias technology, the NTG3B is highly resistant to moisture and harsh environmental conditions, making it an excellent choice for shoots in demanding locations. Its ultra-lightweight build ensures comfortable handling on boom poles and camera rigs, while the included foam windscreen helps reduce unwanted noise from wind and movement.

With remarkable off-axis rejection and minimal self-noise, the NTG3B delivers professional-grade audio clarity, whether you’re on set, in a studio, or out in the field.



Specifications

  • Acoustic Principle: Pressure gradient with RF-bias
  • Frequency Range: 40 Hz – 20 kHz
  • Output Impedance: 25 Ω
  • Sensitivity: -30 dB re 1 Volt/Pascal (31.6 mV @ 94 dB SPL) ±2 dB at 1 kHz
  • Weight: 163 g (5.75 oz)
  • Dimensions (L × Ø): 255 mm × 19 mm
  • Color: Black (NTG3B variant)
  • Power: +48V phantom power required


Key Features

  • Professional-grade shotgun microphone for film and broadcast
  • RF-bias technology for superior moisture resistance
  • Ultra-low self-noise (13 dBA)
  • Exceptional off-axis rejection
  • Lightweight design for easy boom operation
  • Includes foam windscreen and protective storage case
