Sachtler FSB 10 Fluid Head



Inventory

1 Available



The Sachtler FSB 10 Fluid Head is a versatile and robust tripod head designed for professional broadcast and cinematography. This fluid head is ideal for cameras weighing between 4 to 12 kg (8.8 to 26.5 lbs), making it perfect for documentary filmmakers, ENG shooters, and studio productions. It provides smooth, accurate pan and tilt movements, ensuring precise camera control in dynamic shooting environments.

Featuring Sachtler’s SpeedBalance technology, the FSB 10 offers 10 steps of counterbalance, allowing users to balance the camera quickly and easily for perfect alignment. The head also includes 5+0 pan and tilt drag settings, offering a range of options for fine-tuned, fluid movements in any shooting scenario.

The FSB 10 fluid head is equipped with a 100mm bowl mount, providing secure mounting on a wide variety of professional tripods. The ergonomic tilt range of +90° to -70° allows you to capture a wide variety of shots, from high angles to low angles, while maintaining smooth, fluid motion. Additionally, the temperature-independent friction control ensures consistent performance, even in extreme weather conditions.



Specifications

  • Model: Sachtler FSB 10
  • Payload Capacity: 4 to 12 kg (8.8 to 26.5 lbs)
  • Counterbalance: 10-step counterbalance system
  • Pan & Tilt Drag: 5+0 steps for pan and tilt
  • Pan Range: 360°
  • Tilt Range: +90° to -70°
  • Mount Type: 100mm bowl
  • Weight: 2.7 kg (5.95 lbs)
  • Temperature Range: -40°C to +60°C (-40°F to +140°F)


Key Features

  • Supports cameras weighing between 4 to 12 kg (8.8 to 26.5 lbs)
  • 10-step counterbalance for precise camera balance
  • 5+0 pan and tilt drag settings for smooth movement
  • 360° pan range and +90° to -70° tilt range
  • Temperature-independent friction control for use in extreme environments
  • 100mm bowl mount for secure tripod mounting
  • Durable and lightweight, perfect for on-the-go productions
