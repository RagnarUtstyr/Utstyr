The **Deity DQC1 Smart Battery Charger** is designed for professionals needing efficient, reliable charging for their Deity wireless microphone systems and other compatible equipment. This compact charger can charge two batteries simultaneously, ensuring you are always ready to go on set or in the field.

It features an intelligent charging system that automatically detects the battery type and adjusts the charging current accordingly, ensuring the battery is charged safely and efficiently. With a clear LED status indicator, you can easily monitor the charging progress at a glance. The Deity DQC1 is compatible with all Deity rechargeable batteries, offering convenience and reliability for your wireless audio gear.
