Deity DQC1 Smart Battery Charger

The **Deity DQC1 Smart Battery Charger** is a compact, USB-C powered charger designed for Deity's S-95 smart battery and other 2054-style batteries compliant with SBS Specification Version 1.1. Its portability and versatility make it ideal for field recording, documentary, and reality TV productions.

With a maximum charging capacity of 45W, the DQC1 supports various USB-C input standards, ensuring compatibility with a wide range of power sources. It also features a 4-LED charging meter to monitor the battery's charge progress.



Specifications

  • Model: Deity DQC1 Smart Battery Charger
  • Input: USB-C (supports 5V/3A, 9V/3A, 12V/3A, 15V/3A, 20V/2.25A)
  • Output: USB-C (5V/2.5A, 9V/2.5A, 12V/2.5A, 15V/2.5A, 20V/2.25A)
  • Battery Cup Output: 16.8V @ 45W (2054-style 5-blade connector)
  • Dimensions: 53.5mm x 45mm x 16.5mm
  • Weight: 90g
  • Cable Length: 120mm
  • Material: Metal chassis
  • Charging Indicator: 4-LED meter

  • Compact and lightweight design for easy portability
  • Supports multiple USB-C input and output standards
  • High-wattage output capable of powering devices like laptops
