Atomos Ninja V 5" HDR Monitor/Recorder



Inventory

1 Available



The Atomos Ninja V is a 5-inch HDR monitor and external recorder designed to enhance your video production workflow. With its 1000-nit brightness, the Ninja V offers HDR (High Dynamic Range) support and exceptional daylight visibility, making it ideal for both indoor and outdoor shoots. The 1920x1080 touchscreen display provides accurate image monitoring, while the 10-bit recording capability ensures high-quality output for professional production.

The Ninja V features 4K HDMI input and output and supports ProRes and DNxHR recording formats, delivering visually lossless editing workflows. With support for up to 4Kp60 recording, the Ninja V can record directly to SSD drives via the AtomX SSDmini or standard SSDs. This allows filmmakers to bypass internal camera recording limits and achieve extended recording durations. Additionally, it supports HDR workflows with Log and PQ/HLG signal input and output for real-time HDR monitoring.

The Ninja V is built with a compact, lightweight design that’s both durable and portable, making it a perfect on-camera companion. Its AtomOS operating system is intuitive and offers features like focus peaking, false color, and waveform for precise monitoring. With dual battery slots and continuous power support, the Ninja V ensures uninterrupted operation during long shoots.



Specifications

  • Model: Atomos Ninja V
  • Display: 5" touchscreen, 1920x1080 resolution
  • Brightness: 1000 nits
  • HDR Support: Yes (Log, PQ, HLG)
  • Input/Output: HDMI 2.0 (up to 4Kp60)
  • Recording Formats: ProRes, DNxHR
  • Recording Media: AtomX SSDmini, Standard SSDs
  • Battery Slots: Dual Sony L-series batteries
  • Dimensions: 151 x 91.5 x 31 mm
  • Weight: 360g
