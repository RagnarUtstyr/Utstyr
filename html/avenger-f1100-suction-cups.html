Avenger F1100 Suction Cup with 16mm Spigot



Inventory

4 Available



The Avenger F1100 Suction Cup with 16mm Spigot is a versatile tool designed to securely mount lighting and camera accessories to non-porous surfaces like glass, metal, or plastic. This suction cup features a 16mm (5/8") baby pin spigot, which allows compatibility with a wide range of professional film and photography equipment, including lights, reflectors, and small cameras.

The F1100 is built for reliable, strong adhesion to smooth surfaces, making it ideal for mounting equipment on cars, windows, or any glass surface. It provides a stable and durable grip, giving filmmakers and photographers the flexibility to achieve creative angles and shots without the need for traditional stands or clamps. The 16mm spigot is perfect for standard baby pin mounts, enabling secure attachment of various lighting and grip equipment.

With its lightweight design, the Avenger F1100 is easy to transport and set up, making it a valuable tool for location shoots, especially in tight spaces or environments where conventional mounting options aren't practical. Its durable rubber construction ensures long-lasting performance, even with frequent use in demanding conditions.

  • Spigot Size: 16mm (5/8") baby pin
  • Material: Rubber suction cup with metal spigot
