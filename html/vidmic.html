Rode VideoMic Camera-Mount Shotgun Microphone



Inventory

The Rode VideoMic is a popular on-camera shotgun microphone specifically designed for DSLR and mirrorless setups, offering improved audio capture for interviews, vlogs, and general video production. Its highly directional supercardioid pickup pattern focuses on audio in front of the camera, minimizing surrounding noise and ambience.

An integrated shock mount isolates the microphone from vibrations and handling noise, delivering clearer and more professional sound. The microphone is powered by a standard 9V battery, providing an extended runtime for long shoots. It features a 3.5 mm TRS output jack for easy connection to your camera’s audio input.

Lightweight and durable, the Rode VideoMic can be quickly mounted on your camera’s shoe mount or a boom pole, making it a versatile and essential tool for content creators, documentary filmmakers, and event videographers alike.



Specifications

  • Model: Rode VideoMic
  • Type: On-camera shotgun microphone
  • Polar Pattern: Supercardioid
  • Frequency Range: 40 Hz – 20 kHz
  • Sensitivity: -38 dB ± 2 dB (1 V/Pa @ 1 kHz)
  • Output Connection: 3.5 mm TRS mini-jack
  • Power Source: 9V battery (up to 100 hours)
  • Weight: ~176 g (6.2 oz)
  • Dimensions: 25.0 x 6.5 x 10.0 cm (9.8 x 2.6 x 3.9 inches)

  • Supercardioid pickup pattern for focused audio capture
  • Integrated shock mount to reduce handling noise
  • Powered by a single 9V battery for extended operation
