Sound Devices 664



Inventory

1 Available



The Sound Devices 664 is a professional-grade 12-channel portable field mixer and recorder designed for location sound, film production, and broadcast environments. This flagship model from Sound Devices offers six high-performance XLR inputs with full mic/line switching, phantom power, limiters, and additional six line inputs via TA3 connectors, providing versatility for any production scenario.

The 664 has a built-in 16-track recorder that records directly to CompactFlash (CF) and SD cards, ensuring redundancy and reliability for critical audio capture. The 664 records polyphonic or monophonic broadcast WAV files at up to 24-bit/48 kHz. Dual media recording offers flexibility, allowing you to split tracks or record multiple backups in real time.

With intuitive controls and a comprehensive LED metering system, the Sound Devices 664 ensures precision audio recording in complex environments. The 664 also features multiple output options, including four balanced XLR outputs, two TA3 outputs, and AES digital outputs, making it compatible with a wide range of cameras, mixers, and other audio devices.



Specifications

  • Model: Sound Devices 664
  • Channels: 6 mic/line inputs + 6 additional line inputs
  • Recorder: 16-track recorder (polyphonic/monophonic WAV files)
  • Sample Rate: Up to 24-bit/48 kHz
  • Inputs: 6x XLR, 6x TA3 (line level)
  • Outputs: 4x XLR, 2x TA3, AES digital out
  • Media Slots: CompactFlash (CF) and SD card slots
  • Phantom Power: +48V phantom power for all inputs
  • Power Source: 5x AA batteries or external DC power
  • Weight: 2.25 kg (5 lbs)
  • Dimensions: 55mm x 260mm x 185mm
