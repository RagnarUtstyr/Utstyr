Tilta Nucleus Nano Wireless Follow Focus (1st Gen)



Inventory

1 Available



The Tilta Nucleus Nano is a compact, wireless follow focus system perfect for solo camera operators and small production crews. Designed for lightweight DSLR and mirrorless setups, the Nano provides precise focus control in a compact and user-friendly package.

The system includes a wireless focus hand wheel and a powerful motor that can be mounted directly to a 15mm rod. The hand wheel offers adjustable resistance and a responsive feel, mimicking traditional mechanical follow focus systems.

With a working range of up to 300 feet (91 meters) and long battery life via a 14500 rechargeable battery, the Nano is ideal for gimbal, handheld, and rig setups where space and weight are critical.



Specifications

  • Model: Tilta Nucleus Nano (1st Gen)
  • Range: Up to 300 feet (91 meters)
  • Control: Single-channel wireless focus control
  • Compatibility: DSLR and mirrorless cameras
  • Power: 14500 rechargeable battery (for hand wheel)
  • Motor: High-efficiency torque motor
  • Mounting: 15mm rod system compatible
  • Weight (Hand Wheel): Approx. 226g


Key Features

  • Wireless focus control up to 300 ft (91 m)
  • Compact and lightweight — perfect for gimbal and handheld use
  • Responsive hand wheel with adjustable tension
  • Quiet, high-torque motor compatible with most lenses
  • Easy mounting with standard 15mm rods
  • Ideal for solo shooters and small production teams
