4 pin Hirose → 4 pin Hirose



This 4 pin Hirose to 4 pin Hirose cable is commonly used to connect and power compatible audio equipment, wireless receivers, or battery distribution systems. It provides a secure and stable DC connection for devices using the standard 4-pin Hirose configuration.

Designed with durability and flexibility in mind, this cable features high-quality locking Hirose connectors on both ends and is ideal for sound bags, ENG setups, and compact location sound kits.

A perfect choice for linking between power distribution boxes and devices such as wireless receivers, timecode boxes, or other low-draw gear powered via Hirose connections.



Specifications

  • Type: Hirose 4 pin male to Hirose 4 pin male
  • Connector: Hirose HR10A-7P-4P (male both ends)
  • Use Case: Power distribution between compatible devices
  • Cable Length: Approx. 30–50 cm
  • Voltage: Supports standard 12V DC systems
  • Build: Flexible, shielded cable for production use
