The Zoom H6 is a versatile 6-track handheld field recorder ideal for interviews, sound design, location sound, and podcasting. Its modular input system, high-quality preamps, and wide compatibility with microphones and line-level sources make it a favorite among location sound professionals and creators alike.

The H6 features four XLR/TRS combo inputs with dedicated gain knobs, phantom power, and pad switches. With the addition of optional expansion modules, it can support up to six simultaneous inputs. It records directly to SD cards in WAV or MP3 format, and features a color LCD screen for easy menu navigation.

Whether you're capturing dialogue, field ambience, or multi-mic setups, the Zoom H6 offers flexible input and output options in a compact, battery-powered package.



Specifications

  • Model: Zoom H6
  • Tracks: Up to 6 simultaneous recording tracks
  • Inputs: 4x XLR/TRS combo jacks + 2 via interchangeable mic module
  • Outputs: Stereo line out, headphone out with volume control
  • Recording Format: WAV (up to 24-bit/96kHz), MP3
  • Media: SD, SDHC, SDXC cards up to 128GB
  • Power Source: 4x AA batteries or USB power
  • Phantom Power: +12V / +24V / +48V
  • USB Interface: 2-in/2-out or 6-in/2-out operation
  • Dimensions: 77.8mm x 152.8mm x 47.8mm
  • Weight: 280g (without batteries)
