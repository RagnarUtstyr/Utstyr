NiSi IRND 0.6 Filter



Inventory

1 Available



The NiSi IRND 0.6 Filter is a professional-grade neutral density (ND) filter that reduces the amount of light entering the camera by 2 stops. This allows filmmakers and photographers to shoot in bright lighting conditions while maintaining control over exposure and depth of field. The filter’s infrared (IR) technology prevents unwanted color shifts caused by IR light, ensuring true-to-life color accuracy in your footage or photos.

The 0.6 optical density makes this filter ideal for scenarios where you need to use a wider aperture or a slower shutter speed without overexposing the image. Whether shooting landscapes, long exposures, or outdoor scenes, the NiSi IRND 0.6 offers precise light control with minimal impact on image quality.

Made from high-quality optical glass, this filter ensures superior sharpness and clarity, while the multi-coating reduces reflections and flare. Designed to fit into 4x5.65" matte boxes, the NiSi IRND 0.6 is compatible with a wide range of professional cinema cameras and lenses.



Specifications

  • Model: NiSi IRND 0.6
  • Filter Type: Neutral Density (ND) with Infrared protection
  • Optical Density: 0.6 (2 stops)
  • Size: 4x5.65 inches
  • Material: High-quality optical glass
  • Compatibility: Fits 4x5.65" matte boxes
  • Weight: 0.2 kg (0.44 lbs)


Key Features

  • Neutral density filter with 0.6 density (2 stop reduction)
  • IR technology for minimizing color shifts caused by infrared light
  • Ideal for shooting in bright conditions while maintaining control of exposure
  • Allows for wider apertures and slower shutter speeds in daylight
  • High-quality optical glass for superior sharpness and clarity
  • Compatible with 4x5.65" matte boxes
