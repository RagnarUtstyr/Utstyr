Godox FL150S Flexible LED Light



Inventory

2 Available



The Godox FL150S Flexible LED Light is a versatile and lightweight flexible LED panel designed for professional filmmakers, videographers, and photographers. With its 150W output and bi-color capabilities, the FL150S allows users to adjust the color temperature between 3300K to 5600K, offering both warm and cool light to suit any shooting environment.

The flexible design of the FL150S enables it to be easily shaped and positioned in tight or unconventional spaces where traditional lights cannot reach. Its soft and even light output makes it ideal for portrait photography, interviews, or product shoots, providing natural and flattering lighting for subjects.

The FL150S comes with a wireless remote control and Bluetooth app support via the GodoxLight app, allowing users to adjust brightness and color temperature remotely, providing ultimate control on set. The panel can be powered by AC or V-mount batteries, making it convenient for both studio and location shoots.



Specifications

  • Model: Godox FL150S Flexible LED Light
  • Power Output: 150W
  • Color Temperature: 3300K to 5600K (bi-color)
  • CRI: 96+
  • TLCI: 98
  • Beam Angle: 210°
  • Power Options: AC power or V-mount battery (not included)
  • Dimensions: 60 x 40 cm (23.6 x 15.7 inches)
  • Weight: 1.5 kg (3.3 lbs)
  • Control Options: Wireless remote control and GodoxLight app (Bluetooth)


Key Features

  • Flexible LED panel for versatile positioning and lighting setups
  • 150W bi-color light with adjustable color temperature (3300K to 5600K)
  • High CRI of 96+ and TLCI of 98 for accurate color rendering
  • Wide beam angle of 210° for soft, even light spread
  • Wireless remote control and Bluetooth app support for easy adjustments
  • Powered by AC or V-mount batteries for studio or location shoots
  • Lightweight and easy to transport, ideal for professionals on the go
