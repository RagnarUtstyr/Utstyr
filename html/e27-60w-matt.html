E27 60W Matt Lightbulb



Inventory

100 Available



The E27 60W Matt Lightbulb is a high-output lighting solution, perfect for creating bright, yet soft and diffused lighting in various production environments. With a standard E27 screw base, this 60W bulb can be easily installed into most lighting fixtures, offering excellent versatility for both indoor and outdoor setups.

The matte finish on the bulb surface diffuses the light evenly, reducing glare and harsh shadows, which makes it ideal for use in film, photography, and other creative applications where consistent and balanced lighting is required. The E27 60W Matt Lightbulb is a reliable choice for key lighting, fill lighting, or background lighting.

Designed to be durable and long-lasting, this bulb offers approximately 1,000 hours of usage. Its compact size and lightweight design make it easy to handle, while its high brightness makes it suitable for lighting large areas or achieving higher intensity lighting setups.
