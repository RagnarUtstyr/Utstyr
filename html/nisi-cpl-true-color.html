NiSi Cine Filter Rotating CPL True Color 4x5.65



Inventory

1 Available



The NiSi Cine Filter Rotating CPL True Color 4x5.65 is a professional-grade circular polarizer designed for filmmakers and videographers. This CPL (Circular Polarizing Filter) effectively reduces reflections, glare, and haze from non-metallic surfaces, such as water and glass, while maintaining true color accuracy. The filter's ability to remove unwanted reflections and enhance saturation makes it essential for achieving rich contrast and clear, vibrant images in outdoor or bright environments.

The rotating design allows for precise control over the polarization effect, giving cinematographers the ability to adjust the filter depending on the angle of the light. Unlike many polarizing filters that can introduce color shifts, the NiSi True Color CPL ensures that the original colors of the scene are preserved, maintaining a neutral color balance.

Designed to fit into 4x5.65" matte boxes, the NiSi Cine Rotating CPL is compatible with a wide range of professional cinema cameras and lenses. Constructed from high-quality optical glass with a multi-layer coating, this filter minimizes flare and reflections, ensuring maximum clarity and sharpness. Whether you are shooting landscapes, water scenes, or reflective surfaces, the NiSi Rotating CPL enhances your footage with superior light control and vivid, natural colors.
