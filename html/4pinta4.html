4 pin Hirose → TA4



This 4 pin Hirose to TA4 cable is commonly used to power wireless transmitters, such as Lectrosonics, from external battery distribution systems. It provides a secure, reliable power connection between Hirose-compatible outputs and TA4 (mini XLR) power inputs.

Designed for use in professional audio setups, particularly sound bags, this cable is built with high-quality components and rugged connectors for dependable performance in demanding environments.

It’s an ideal solution for location sound mixers needing to power TA4 devices from 12V Hirose outputs, eliminating the need for internal batteries.



Specifications

  • Type: 4 pin Hirose (HR10A) to TA4F (mini XLR)
  • Application: Powering wireless transmitters from battery distribution systems
  • Voltage: Supports 12V DC systems
  • Cable Length: Approx. 30–50 cm
  • Build: Flexible, shielded cable with strain relief
  • Connector Orientation: Straight Hirose and TA4F (female)


Key Features

  • 4 pin Hirose to TA4 cable for powering wireless transmitters
  • Commonly used with Lectrosonics and other TA4-powered gear
  • Ideal for sound bag setups and compact field use
  • Durable and field-ready construction
  • Reduces need for onboard batteries in transmitters
