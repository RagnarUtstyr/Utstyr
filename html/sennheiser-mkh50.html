Sennheiser MKH50



Inventory

1 Available



The Sennheiser MKH50 is a professional-grade supercardioid condenser microphone renowned for its high directionality, making it ideal for film sound recording, broadcast, and studio applications. Known for its clarity, low self-noise, and precise sound capture, the MKH50 excels in capturing dialogue and isolated sounds in controlled environments, such as sound stages or indoor locations.

The MKH50 is specifically designed to provide greater off-axis rejection than a standard cardioid microphone, allowing users to capture focused sound while minimizing ambient noise and reverberation. Its frequency response is tailored for natural voice reproduction, while a switchable low-cut filter helps reduce low-frequency rumble and handling noise.

This microphone is phantom-powered, with a rugged metal body that ensures durability and longevity, even in demanding environments. The MKH50 is particularly valued for its ability to maintain excellent sound quality in close-miking situations, delivering rich and detailed audio across a variety of professional recording scenarios.



Specifications

  • Model: Sennheiser MKH50
  • Microphone Type: Supercardioid condenser
  • Frequency Response: 40Hz - 20kHz
  • Pickup Pattern: Supercardioid
  • Sensitivity: 25 mV/Pa
  • Equivalent Noise Level: 12 dB(A) (DIN IEC 651)
  • Max SPL: 134 dB
  • Power Source: 48V phantom power
  • Connector: XLR-3M
  • Dimensions: 153mm x 25mm (6.02" x 0.98")
  • Weight: 160g (5.64 oz)


Key Features

  • Highly directional supercardioid pattern for excellent off-axis rejection
  • Natural and detailed sound reproduction, ideal for dialogue and voice
  • Low self-noise for pristine audio recording
  • Switchable low-cut filter to reduce rumble and low-frequency noise
  • Phantom-powered, suitable for professional recording setups
  • Rugged metal construction for durability
  • Perfect for film sound, broadcast, and studio applications
