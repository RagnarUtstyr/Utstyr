The Zhiyun Smooth X is a compact, foldable smartphone gimbal designed for quick, on-the-go filming. Thanks to its innovative 2-axis stabilizing system, you can capture smooth, steady footage whether you’re vlogging, traveling, or recording casual family moments.

Its telescoping aluminum arm extends to help you achieve creative angles and perfect selfies, while intuitive onboard controls let you switch between portrait and landscape modes instantly. With a lightweight build and user-friendly design, the Zhiyun Smooth X is perfect for upgrading your smartphone videography and photography game.

The free ZY Cami app offers intelligent tracking features, cinematic video templates, and easy editing tools — giving you everything you need to create polished, professional-looking content straight from your phone.



Specifications

  • Model: Zhiyun Smooth X
  • Compatibility: Most iOS/Android smartphones up to 235g (approx. 0.52 lbs)
  • Stabilization: 2-axis gimbal (pan & roll)
  • Telescoping Arm Extension: Up to 260 mm (10.2 inches)
  • Battery Life: Up to 5.5 hours
  • App Integration: ZY Cami for gestures, tracking, and video editing
  • Weight: Approx. 246 g (0.54 lbs)
