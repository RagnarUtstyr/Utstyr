The DPA 4060 is a professional-grade omnidirectional lavalier microphone that offers pristine sound quality in a compact, discreet design. Known for its remarkable clarity and natural, full-range frequency response, this mic is ideal for broadcast, film, and theater applications where capturing accurate and detailed sound is paramount.

The 4060 is designed to capture high-quality audio with excellent rejection of unwanted noise, and it is highly resistant to sweat, moisture, and interference. This microphone is well-suited for use in demanding environments, providing consistent and reliable performance. Whether used for interviews, camera setups, or theater productions, the DPA 4060 is a trusted solution for high-end audio recording.

It is available with different accessories, including windshields and clips, and is compatible with a wide range of wireless transmitters through its miniature connector options.



Specifications

  • Model: DPA 4060
  • Type: Omnidirectional Condenser Microphone
  • Frequency Response: 20Hz – 20kHz
  • Pickup Pattern: Omnidirectional
  • Sensitivity: 35mV/Pa
  • Connector: MicroDot (adapter required for wireless systems)
  • Maximum SPL: 144 dB SPL
  • Impedance: <1000 ohm
  • Self Noise: 28 dB-A
  • Water Resistance: IP57 (water-resistant and sweat-proof)
  • Cable Length: 1.2 meters (3.9 ft)
  • Weight: 0.5 g
