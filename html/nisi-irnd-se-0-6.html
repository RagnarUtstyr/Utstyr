NiSi IRND SE 0.6 Filter



Inventory

1 Available



The NiSi IRND SE 0.6 Filter is a neutral density (ND) filter designed for professional filmmakers and photographers. This filter reduces the light entering the camera by 2 stops, making it ideal for outdoor shooting in bright lighting conditions. The SE (Soft Edge) design offers a gradual transition from the clear area to the neutral density, providing smoother exposure blending between bright skies and darker landscapes.

Equipped with infrared (IR) technology, the NiSi IRND SE 0.6 helps eliminate color shifts caused by infrared light, ensuring natural color rendition. This makes it perfect for scenes requiring balanced exposure, such as landscape photography, cinematic productions, and long-exposure photography.

Constructed from high-quality optical glass, this filter offers sharp, distortion-free images while reducing unwanted flare and ghosting. Designed to fit into 4x5.65" matte boxes, the NiSi IRND SE 0.6 filter integrates seamlessly into professional setups for cinema cameras and lenses, offering superior control over exposure and depth of field.



Specifications

  • Model: NiSi IRND SE 0.6
  • Filter Type: Neutral Density (ND) with Infrared protection
  • Optical Density: 0.6 (2 stops)
  • Design: Soft Edge (SE)
  • Size: 4x5.65 inches
  • Material: High-quality optical glass
  • Compatibility: Fits 4x5.65" matte boxes
  • Weight: 0.2 kg (0.44 lbs)


Key Features

  • 2-stop light reduction with a soft edge transition for balanced exposure
  • IR technology to prevent color shifts from infrared light
  • Perfect for outdoor photography and cinematic scenes with varied lighting
  • Ideal for blending bright skies with darker landscapes in landscapes and cinematography
  • High-quality optical glass for excellent sharpness and clarity
  • Compatible with 4x5.65" matte boxes for professional use
