NiSi IRND SE 0.3 Filter



Inventory

1 Available



The NiSi IRND SE 0.3 Filter is a neutral density (ND) filter designed to reduce light by 1 stop, making it ideal for controlling exposure in bright lighting conditions while maintaining natural colors and details in the shot. The filter is equipped with IR (Infrared) technology, which helps prevent color shifts caused by infrared light, ensuring true color reproduction in both photos and videos.

The "SE" (Soft Edge) design of this filter features a gradual transition of density from the edge of the filter, making it perfect for balancing bright skies with darker landscapes in landscape photography or cinematic outdoor scenes. By using the NiSi IRND SE 0.3, filmmakers and photographers can maintain control over depth of field and shutter speeds, even in challenging lighting environments.

Crafted from high-quality optical glass, the NiSi IRND SE 0.3 ensures excellent sharpness and minimal distortion while providing reliable light reduction. The filter is designed to fit into 4x5.65" matte boxes, making it compatible with a wide range of professional cinema cameras and lenses.



Specifications

  • Model: NiSi IRND SE 0.3
  • Filter Type: Neutral Density (ND) with Infrared protection
  • Optical Density: 0.3 (1 stop)
  • Design: Soft Edge (SE)
  • Size: 4x5.65 inches
  • Material: High-quality optical glass
  • Compatibility: Fits 4x5.65" matte boxes
  • Weight: 0.2 kg (0.44 lbs)


Key Features

  • 1-stop light reduction with a soft edge transition for balanced exposure
  • IR technology to prevent color shifts from infrared light
  • Ideal for outdoor and landscape photography or cinematic shoots
  • Perfect for balancing bright skies with darker foregrounds
  • High-quality optical glass construction for sharpness and clarity
  • Compatible with 4x5.65" matte boxes for professional setups
