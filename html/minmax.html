Easyrig Minimax



Inventory

1 Available



The Easyrig Minimax is a compact and lightweight camera support system designed to alleviate the strain on the camera operator's back and shoulders during long shoots. Ideal for documentaries, news production, and handheld shooting, the Minimax distributes the camera weight evenly, providing more comfort and stability when operating cameras ranging from 2 to 7 kg (4.4 to 15.4 lbs).

The Easyrig Minimax features a patented mechanical tension system that automatically adjusts to the camera's weight, offering a customized level of support for various setups. Its shock-absorbing support line ensures smooth, fluid camera movement while minimizing jerky motion, making it perfect for run-and-gun scenarios and dynamic shots.

Designed for portability and ease of use, the Minimax can be quickly packed and carried in its own travel case. The rig is compatible with a wide range of DSLRs, mirrorless cameras, and small cinema cameras, providing professionals with greater flexibility and comfort during extended shoots.



Specifications

  • Model: Easyrig Minimax
  • Weight Range: 2 to 7 kg (4.4 to 15.4 lbs)
  • Height: Adjustable 90 cm (35.4 inches)
  • Support Line: Shock-absorbing cord
  • Material: Durable, lightweight aluminum
  • Weight: 3.2 kg (7 lbs)
  • Carrying Case: Included

  • Evenly distributes camera weight to reduce operator strain
  • Mechanical tension system for automatic weight adjustment
  • Shock-absorbing support line for smooth, fluid movement

  • Comes with a travel case for easy transport
