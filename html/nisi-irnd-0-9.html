NiSi IRND 0.9 Filter



Inventory

1 Available



The NiSi IRND 0.9 Filter is a neutral density (ND) filter that provides a 3-stop reduction in light, allowing for precise exposure control in bright environments. This makes it ideal for outdoor shoots, landscape photography, or situations where you want to maintain a wide aperture or a slower shutter speed without overexposing the image. The built-in IR (Infrared) technology minimizes color shifts caused by infrared light, ensuring natural color reproduction even in challenging lighting conditions.

With its 0.9 optical density, the NiSi IRND 0.9 is perfect for capturing shots with beautifully blurred backgrounds (bokeh) or achieving motion blur effects in bright conditions. The filter allows photographers and cinematographers to manage their exposure without compromising image sharpness or color accuracy.

Crafted from high-quality optical glass with multi-layer coatings, this filter ensures excellent light transmission and minimal loss of detail, while reducing lens flare and ghosting. Designed to fit into 4x5.65" matte boxes, it integrates seamlessly with professional cinema cameras and lenses, providing maximum versatility in various shooting environments.



Specifications

  • Model: NiSi IRND 0.9
  • Filter Type: Neutral Density (ND) with Infrared protection
  • Optical Density: 0.9 (3 stops)
  • Size: 4x5.65 inches
  • Material: High-quality optical glass
  • Compatibility: Fits 4x5.65" matte boxes
  • Weight: 0.2 kg (0.44 lbs)


Key Features

  • Neutral density filter with 0.9 density (3-stop reduction)
  • IR technology to prevent color shifts caused by infrared light
  • Ideal for outdoor shoots, long exposures, and controlling depth of field
  • Allows for slower shutter speeds or wider apertures in bright conditions
  • High-quality optical glass with multi-layer coating for sharp, clear images
  • Compatible with 4x5.65" matte boxes for professional cinema setups
