The Godox VL300 is a powerful 300W LED video light, offering bright and consistent output at a daylight-balanced 5600K color temperature. Ideal for studio or on-location shooting, its high CRI and TLCI ratings ensure accurate color rendition. The fixture is lightweight, quiet, and easy to handle, making it an excellent choice for interviews, film sets, and commercial photography.

With a Bowens mount design, the VL300 is compatible with a wide range of light modifiers, such as softboxes, reflectors, and beauty dishes. Its external controller box allows for intuitive dimming from 0% to 100%, while optional wireless control provides added flexibility. Whether you need a powerful key light or a versatile fill light, the Godox VL300 can adapt to your production demands.



Specifications

  • Power Output: 300W
  • Color Temperature: 5600K ±200K
  • CRI: ≥96
  • TLCI: ≥95
  • Dimming Range: 0–100%
  • Mount: Bowens Mount
  • Power Supply: AC 100–240V
