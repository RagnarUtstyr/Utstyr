Hollyland Solidcom C1



Inventory

3 Available



The Hollyland Solidcom C1 is a professional full-duplex wireless intercom system designed for high-quality communication in production environments. This system is ideal for film sets, live events, or any situation where clear, uninterrupted communication is critical. With its advanced technology, the Solidcom C1 offers crystal-clear audio, providing smooth communication between crew members without the need for cables or bulky devices.

The Solidcom C1 operates on a 1.9 GHz frequency, which ensures a stable and interference-free signal in crowded wireless environments. It supports up to 8 users on the same network, making it highly scalable for a variety of production sizes. The system offers both short and long-range communication, with a maximum range of up to 500 meters, ensuring reliable connectivity even across large sets.

Designed for ease of use, the Solidcom C1 features lightweight headsets with ergonomic designs, ensuring comfort during long hours of use. The system is also equipped with rechargeable batteries, making it a portable and cost-effective solution for production teams.
