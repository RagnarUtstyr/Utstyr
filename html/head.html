The Standard 2.5" Grip Head is a core grip accessory used for mounting and securing flags, scrims, reflectors, and other light control tools. Its compact yet robust design makes it an essential part of any lighting or grip setup in film, photography, and studio environments.

Constructed from solid aluminum, this grip head provides strong clamping power while remaining lightweight. It fits onto standard 16mm (5/8") baby studs and can hold various rigging accessories with stability and ease. The head features multiple mounting holes to accommodate different grip arms and setups.

Whether you're flying a flag, holding a diffusion frame, or securing a background element, the 2.5" Grip Head offers reliable performance in any professional production scenario.
