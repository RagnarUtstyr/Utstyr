SmallHD 1703 P3X Monitor



Inventory

1 Available



The SmallHD 1703 P3X Monitor offers a stunning 17-inch Full HD display with a resolution of 1920x1080 and an impressive brightness of 900 nits. Known for its incredible color accuracy and daylight viewability, this monitor rivals OLED with its 100% DCI-P3 color gamut and 1400:1 contrast ratio. Built with a rugged anodized aluminum chassis and four-sided rubber impact protection, it is designed for durability, especially for on-location shoots.

The monitor comes equipped with dual SDI and HDMI inputs and outputs, along with multiple mounting options. The IPS LCD panel ensures 178-degree viewing angles both vertically and horizontally, providing clarity from almost any position. The 1703 P3X is powered via 12-34V DC input or AC power, with versatile power options including V-Mount plate.
