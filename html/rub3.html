The Rubber Box Power Distro 400V is a heavy-duty power distribution unit, designed for professional applications where high power is required, such as large film sets, live event productions, or industrial use. With a 63A input and 6x 32A outputs, this distro box allows for safe and reliable distribution of 400V power across multiple devices.

Its rugged, rubberized construction ensures durability, even in challenging environments, providing protection from physical impacts and harsh weather conditions. Equipped with circuit breakers for each output, this unit ensures safety and protection against overloads, making it ideal for powering large lighting rigs, high-powered sound equipment, or other high-demand applications.
