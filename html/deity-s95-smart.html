Deity S95 Smart Battery



Inventory

3 Available



The Deity S95 Smart Battery is a high-performance Li-Ion battery designed specifically for wireless audio transmitters and receivers. With a capacity of 95Wh, the S95 provides long-lasting power, making it an ideal choice for extended shoots and field recording sessions. The battery features smart technology that communicates with compatible devices, allowing for accurate battery monitoring and power management.

The S95 Smart Battery also features an integrated 5 LED indicator, which provides information on remaining battery life and charging status. Its advanced protection circuitry guards against overcharging, overheating, and short circuits, ensuring safe and reliable operation under demanding conditions.



Specifications

  • Model: Deity S95 Smart Battery
  • Capacity: 95Wh
  • Charging Input: 16.8V DC
  • Battery Type: Lithium-Ion
  • Display: OLED screen for battery status
  • Dimensions: 15 x 9 x 5 cm (5.9 x 3.5 x 2 inches)
  • Weight: 0.8 kg (1.76 lbs)


Key Features

  • High-capacity 95Wh Li-Ion battery for wireless audio devices
  • Smart technology for real-time battery monitoring
  • Integrated LED indicators showing battery life and charging status
  • Advanced protection circuitry for safe and reliable operation
  • Compact and lightweight design for easy transport
  • Durable build, ideal for professional production environments
