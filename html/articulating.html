Generic Articulating Arm



Inventory

2 Available



These Articulating Arms are versatile and sturdy mount solutions for cameras, monitors, lights, or other accessories. They offer precision adjustability for professional shoots. Its robust metal construction and secure locking mechanisms ensure stability, even under demanding conditions.

Ideal for film and photography use, the arm features multiple joints that allow for a wide range of angles, enabling you to position your camera or accessory exactly where you need it. With a 1/4"-20 screw on one or both ends, it is compatible with most standard mounting options, and the center locking knob makes it quick and easy to tighten or loosen the joints simultaneously.

Whether you're attaching a monitor for easy viewing or rigging a small LED light, this articulating arm is a must-have accessory for any camera operator or content creator looking for flexibility and reliability.



Specifications

  • Model: Articulating Arm
  • Material: Aluminum alloy
  • Mount Threads: 1/4"-20 screws on each end
  • Maximum Length: 7–11 inches (varies by model)
  • Locking Mechanism: Single-knob tightening for all joints
  • Weight Capacity: Supports up to 2–4 kg (depending on model)
  • Weight: Approximately 0.3–0.5 kg


Key Features

  • Fully articulating arm for versatile mounting
  • Aluminum alloy construction for lightweight durability
  • Compatible with standard 1/4"-20 mounts
  • Single-knob lock to secure multiple joints simultaneously
  • Ideal for on-camera monitors, small LED lights, or microphones
  • Perfect for professional film and photography setups
