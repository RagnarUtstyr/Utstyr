Smallrig 15mm Baseplate



Inventory

1 Available



The Smallrig 15mm Baseplate is a compact and reliable camera rig accessory that provides standardized rod support for mounting follow focus systems, matte boxes, lens supports, and other rig components. It’s an essential piece for building out your camera rig with precision and modularity.

Designed with a standard 15mm LWS (Lightweight Support) spacing, this baseplate is compatible with a wide range of camera bodies and accessories. The adjustable height feature ensures proper alignment with lens center height, while the quick-release plate system allows for fast camera swaps in the field.

Built from durable aluminum alloy, the Smallrig baseplate offers both stability and lightweight convenience. It includes rubber padding to prevent scratching and slipping, and is ideal for DSLR, mirrorless, or compact cinema camera setups.



Specifications

  • Model: Smallrig 15mm LWS Baseplate
  • Rod Standard: 15mm LWS (60mm spacing)
  • Material: Aluminum alloy
  • Camera Mount: 1/4"-20 and 3/8"-16 mounting screws
  • Rod Ports: 2x 15mm rod openings
  • Height Adjustment: Yes
  • Weight: Approx. 220g


Key Features

  • Standard 15mm LWS rod support system
  • Height adjustable for precise lens alignment
  • Quick-release camera plate for fast setup
  • Durable aluminum construction
  • Ideal for mirrorless, DSLR, or cinema rigs
  • Compatible with follow focus, matte box, lens support, and more
