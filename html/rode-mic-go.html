Røde Mic Go



Inventory

1 Available



The Røde VideoMic GO is an ultra-lightweight, on-camera shotgun microphone designed to provide high-quality audio capture for filmmakers, vloggers, and content creators. This compact microphone is a plug-and-play solution that requires no batteries or complicated settings, making it incredibly easy to use with any DSLR, mirrorless camera, or other audio recording devices.

The VideoMic GO features a supercardioid pickup pattern, which focuses the audio capture on the subject directly in front of the camera while reducing background noise from the sides and rear. This makes it ideal for recording dialogue, interviews, or ambient sound in noisy environments. The microphone is equipped with Rycote Lyre shock mounting, which isolates the microphone from handling noise and vibrations, ensuring clear, crisp audio.

With a durable yet lightweight construction, the Røde VideoMic GO is designed for portability, allowing creators to capture professional-quality audio anywhere. It comes with a standard 3.5mm TRS connector that is compatible with most cameras and recording devices.



Specifications

  • Model: Røde VideoMic GO
  • Microphone Type: On-camera shotgun microphone
  • Pickup Pattern: Supercardioid
  • Frequency Range: 100Hz - 16kHz
  • Connector: 3.5mm TRS jack
  • Power Source: Plug-in power (no batteries required)
  • Weight: 73g (2.5oz)
  • Dimensions: 167mm x 79mm x 70mm


Key Features

  • Ultra-lightweight design for easy portability
  • Supercardioid pickup pattern for focused audio capture
  • Requires no batteries – plug and play with any camera
  • Integrated Rycote Lyre shock mount for isolation from handling noise
  • Compatible with DSLR, mirrorless cameras, and other audio recording devices
