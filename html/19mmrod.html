19mm Camera Rods



Inventory

1 Set Available



These 19mm camera rods are a vital part of professional cinema rig configurations. Commonly used on high-end film production setups, they allow you to attach matte boxes, follow focus systems, handles, and other accessories with maximum stability and robustness.

Constructed from sturdy aluminum alloy, each rod measures 45 cm (18 inches) long, offering ample space for multiple accessories. The 19mm diameter ensures compatibility with heavy-duty rod-based support systems, providing a rock-solid foundation for your camera and accessories.

Easy to install and adjust, the rods feature smooth edges to prevent snagging or scratching your rig components. Whether you’re on a professional film set or a large-scale commercial shoot, these 19mm rods help keep your build flexible, secure, and dependable.



Specifications

  • Model: 19mm Aluminum Camera Rods
  • Diameter: 19 mm (high-end cinema standard)
  • Length: 45 cm (18 inches) each
  • Material: Durable aluminum alloy
  • Compatibility: Suited for 19mm rod-based camera rigs
  • Weight (per rod): Approx. 80 g (0.18 lbs)


Key Features

  • 19mm diameter rods for heavy-duty cinema rigs
  • 45 cm length provides plenty of space for multiple accessories
  • Crafted from sturdy aluminum alloy for long-term use
  • Smooth edges to prevent scratches during setup
  • Ensures solid mounting for matte boxes, follow focuses, and more
