DZOfilm Catta Ace 35-80mm T2.9 PL/EF Zoom Lens



Inventory

1 Available



The DZOfilm Catta Ace 35-80mm T2.9 is a professional-grade cinema zoom lens designed for high-quality filmmaking. This super35 lens offers a 35-80mm focal range with a constant T2.9 aperture, providing stunning image quality and exceptional low-light performance. The Catta Ace series is built with a PL mount, but it is also compatible with EF mount adapters, making it versatile for a wide range of cinema cameras.

This lens is engineered for professional production, featuring parfocal optics that maintain focus throughout the zoom range and minimal focus breathing for smooth, cinematic transitions. The 11-blade iris creates a beautifully smooth bokeh, giving filmmakers the ability to create soft, defocused backgrounds with a pleasing aesthetic. The Catta Ace 35-80mm lens offers sharp detail, high contrast, and excellent color rendering, making it suitable for both studio and on-location shooting.

The lens is constructed with high-precision mechanics, ensuring durability while remaining lightweight for handheld or gimbal operation. Its cinema-standard gear rings for focus, iris, and zoom allow seamless integration with follow focus systems and other lens control accessories, providing full control over the image.



Specifications

  • Model: DZOfilm Catta Ace 35-80mm T2.9 PL/EF
  • Focal Length: 35-80mm
  • Aperture: T2.9 constant throughout zoom range
  • Mount: PL mount (interchangeable to EF with included adapter)
  • Image Circle: Super 35
  • Iris Blades: 11
  • Minimum Focus Distance: 75 cm (29.5 inches)
  • Front Diameter: 95mm
  • Weight: 1.8 kg (3.97 lbs)
