4 pin Hirose → 3x USB-C Power Cable



This 4 pin Hirose to 3x USB-C splitter cable allows you to power up to three USB-C devices from a single 4 pin Hirose output. It's perfect for charging or running small accessories like wireless audio receivers, timecode boxes, mobile devices, or small monitors from your sound bag or camera rig's power distro.

Featuring a high-quality locking Hirose connector and regulated USB-C outputs, this cable simplifies cable management while providing stable power across multiple modern devices.

It’s ideal for streamlined rigs, mobile setups, and workflows where battery space and power ports are limited.



Specifications

  • Input: 4 pin Hirose (HR10A) male
  • Outputs: 3x USB-C (female)
  • Voltage Input: 12V DC (standard Hirose)
  • Output: 5V regulated per USB-C port
  • Total Output Current: Up to 3A combined
  • Cable Length: Approx. 30–40 cm
  • Build: Flexible rubberized cable with strain relief


Key Features

  • Converts 12V Hirose to 3x 5V USB-C outputs
  • Power multiple modern accessories from one source
  • Great for sound bags, camera rigs, and wireless setups
  • Compact and cable-managed alternative to multiple battery eliminators
  • Durable and lightweight for production environments
