These 15mm camera rods are essential components for building a robust, modular camera rig. They provide a secure and standardized way to attach matte boxes, follow focuses, handles, and other accessories to your camera setup, ensuring better stability and expandability.

Made from lightweight aluminum alloy, they balance durability with minimal added weight. Each rod measures 30 cm in length (approximately 12 inches), and the 15mm diameter is compatible with most industry-standard rod-based systems. Whether you’re a run-and-gun shooter or a studio cinematographer, these rods offer a flexible backbone for a variety of rig configurations.

Quick to install or remove, they feature smooth ends to facilitate easy mounting without scratching your gear. Pair them with rod clamps, baseplates, and support arms to unlock endless possibilities for mounting external monitors, follow focus units, battery plates, and more.
