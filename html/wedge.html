Wooden Wedge Set for Dolly Tracks



Inventory

1 Set Available



This Wooden Wedge Set provides a quick and efficient way to level and stabilize dolly tracks, camera platforms, or other equipment on set. Each wedge is cut from sturdy, seasoned wood to prevent splitting under heavy loads, ensuring reliable support in a variety of shooting environments.

Ideal for both interior studio floors and uneven outdoor locations, these wedges allow you to make precise height adjustments, maintain track alignment, and minimize unwanted vibrations. Light and easy to handle, they’re an essential item in any grip department’s arsenal.

Whether you’re setting up a complex dolly shot or simply need to compensate for slopes, this wedge set offers the versatility and durability you need to keep production running smoothly.



Specifications

  • Material: Seasoned hardwood (e.g., pine or maple)
  • Dimensions: Ranging from 15 cm to 25 cm (6" to 10") in length
  • Thickness Range: 0.5 cm to 3 cm (0.2" to 1.2") taper
  • Pieces per Set: 6 wedges
  • Weight: Approx. 1.2 kg (2.6 lbs) total


Key Features

  • Perfect for leveling dolly tracks, rigs, and platforms
  • Durable hardwood construction to support heavy loads
  • Lightweight and easy to transport on location
  • Multiple wedge sizes to accommodate varied surfaces
  • Reduces vibrations and ensures smooth camera moves
