Safety Wire



Inventory

1 Available



This Safety Wire is an essential piece of grip equipment, designed to prevent accidental drops of cameras, lighting fixtures, or other gear on set. The robust steel cable ensures reliable protection in case of mount failures, adding a vital layer of security during fast-paced shoots or overhead rigging.

Equipped with secure carabiner clips at both ends, the wire attaches easily to rigging points, light stands, or camera cages. Its lightweight, flexible design makes it simple to pack and deploy, whether you’re on location or in a studio setting.

Protecting crew, talent, and equipment is paramount in professional film or TV productions—and this Safety Wire helps you do just that, ensuring peace of mind on set.



Specifications

  • Material: Steel wire with protective coating
  • Length: 60 cm (23.6")
  • Diameter: 2.5 mm (approx. 0.1")
  • End Fittings: Dual locking carabiners
  • Load Capacity: Up to 20 kg (44 lbs)
  • Weight: Approx. 60 g (0.13 lbs)


Key Features

  • Prevents accidental drops and damage to equipment
  • Durable steel construction for reliable on-set use
  • Lightweight and easy to store in grip or camera bags
  • Locking carabiners enable quick, secure attachment
  • Suitable for rigging cameras, lights, or other gear overhead
