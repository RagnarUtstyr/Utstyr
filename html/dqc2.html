The **Deity DQC2 Smart Battery Charger** is a professional-grade charger designed to charge up to two batteries simultaneously. Ideal for film, broadcast, and theater production environments, the DQC2 ensures that your Deity wireless microphones and other compatible devices are always powered and ready for action.

Featuring intelligent charging technology, the DQC2 automatically detects the battery type and adjusts the charging current for optimal performance. The charger has clear LED indicators that allow you to easily monitor the charging process, ensuring a quick turnaround and minimal downtime.

With its compact design, the DQC2 is portable and perfect for use in the field, on set, or in a studio environment. It also includes overcharge protection and other safety features to ensure your batteries last longer and perform better.
