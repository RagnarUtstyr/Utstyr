Canon LP-E6 Battery



Inventory

16 Available



The Canon LP-E6 is a rechargeable lithium-ion battery pack designed for use with Canon's EOS DSLR cameras and various accessories. Known for its reliability and long battery life, the LP-E6 is an industry-standard power solution for photographers, videographers, and content creators alike. It is compatible with a range of Canon DSLR cameras, including the 5D Mark II, 5D Mark III, 6D, 7D, 70D, and 80D models.

With a capacity of 1865mAh, this battery provides ample power for extended shooting sessions, whether in the studio or on location. The LP-E6 can be recharged using Canon's LC-E6 battery charger, and it includes built-in protection against overcharging and overheating, ensuring safe and efficient use.

The Canon LP-E6 battery is lightweight, compact, and easy to carry as a spare, making it a convenient accessory for long shoots or travels. With reliable performance and a durable build, it’s the perfect backup power source for your Canon camera gear.



Specifications

  • Model: Canon LP-E6
  • Battery Type: Lithium-ion
  • Capacity: 1865mAh (7.2V)
  • Compatibility: Canon EOS 5D Mark II, 5D Mark III, 6D, 7D, 70D, 80D, and more
  • Charging Time: Approximately 2 hours with LC-E6 charger
  • Weight: 80 g (2.8 oz)
  • Dimensions: 3.8 x 2.1 x 5.6 cm (1.5 x 0.8 x 2.2 inches)


Key Features

  • 1865mAh capacity for long shooting sessions
