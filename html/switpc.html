SWIT PC-U130S Single Channel Charger



Inventory

1 Available



The SWIT PC-U130S is a compact, single-channel V-mount charger designed for efficient and rapid battery charging. With its universal AC input (100–240 V) and lightweight design, this charger is ideal for both on-location shoots and studio use.

Designed specifically for lithium-ion batteries, the PC-U130S delivers 16.8V/3.5A output, allowing you to quickly recharge V-mount batteries used by cinema cameras, broadcast camcorders, and other professional equipment. It features LED indicators for charging status, and its sturdy build ensures durability for everyday use in demanding environments.

Whether you’re shooting documentaries, commercial projects, or studio broadcasts, the SWIT PC-U130S offers the reliability and portability professionals need to keep their gear powered throughout the day.



Specifications

  • Model: SWIT PC-U130S
  • Type: Single-channel lithium-ion battery charger
  • Battery Mount: V-Mount
  • Input: AC 100–240 V, 50/60 Hz
  • Output: DC 16.8 V, 3.5 A
  • Charging Time: ~4 hours for a 130 Wh battery
  • Indicator: LED charge status
  • Weight: Approx. 0.9 kg
  • Dimensions: 16.0 x 9.0 x 5.8 cm

  • LED indicators for easy monitoring of charging progress
