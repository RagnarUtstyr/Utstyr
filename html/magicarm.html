Manfrotto Friction Arm



Inventory

5 Available



The Manfrotto Friction Arm is a versatile and reliable articulating arm designed for mounting cameras, monitors, lights, and other production gear in studio and on-location setups. This friction arm is known for its adjustability and sturdy construction, making it a favorite among professionals who require flexible mounting solutions for various production scenarios.

The arm features two pivoting joints with adjustable friction control, allowing users to securely position and lock equipment in the perfect spot. With a total arm length of 53 cm (21 inches), it provides ample reach and flexibility. The 3/8" and 1/4" threads on both ends make it compatible with a wide range of mounting options, clamps, and accessories.

Built from durable aluminum, the Manfrotto Friction Arm is designed to withstand heavy use in demanding production environments. It is commonly used for mounting monitors, LED panels, audio recorders, or even cameras in tight spaces, where traditional stands cannot be easily deployed. Its lightweight and compact form factor make it easy to transport and quick to set up.

  • Mounting Threads: 3/8" and 1/4" on both ends
