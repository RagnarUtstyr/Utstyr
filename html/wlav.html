The Deity W.Lav Pro is a professional-grade subminiature lavalier microphone designed for film, broadcast, and theater applications. With a 3mm capsule and ultra-low self-noise, it delivers clean, natural sound in a discreet profile that’s ideal for talent miking in both indoor and outdoor environments.

Built with a microdot connector and high compatibility with various wireless systems, the W.Lav Pro provides exceptional versatility. It is IP57 waterproof-rated, making it suitable for challenging shooting conditions including light rain or sweat-heavy scenarios.

The W.Lav Pro includes a foam windscreen, cable management clips, and multiple mounting options, making it a solid go-to lav mic for high-end location sound. It is well-suited for pairing with timecode recorders like the Tentacle Sync or wireless systems from brands such as Deity, Sennheiser, and Lectrosonics.



Specifications

  • Model: Deity W.Lav Pro
  • Capsule Size: 3mm subminiature condenser
  • Frequency Response: 20Hz – 20kHz
  • Pickup Pattern: Omnidirectional
  • Sensitivity: -38dB ±2dB (1V/Pa @ 1kHz)
  • Connector: Microdot (adapter required for wireless packs)
  • Self-Noise: 24dB(A)
  • IP Rating: IP57 (dust and water resistant)
  • Cable Length: 1.2m (3.9 ft)
  • Color: Black
