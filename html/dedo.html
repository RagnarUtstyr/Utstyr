Dedo 150W Light



Inventory

2 Available



The Dedo 150W light is a compact yet powerful tungsten spotlight, known for its precise beam control and exceptional focusing range. With superior optics and low power consumption, the Dedo 150W is a staple on film sets and photography studios alike. Whether you’re working with intricate tabletop setups or need a controlled key or accent light, this fixture delivers clean, consistent output and minimal spill.

Featuring a continuous focusing mechanism, you can easily adjust the beam angle to achieve anything from a tight spotlight to a broad flood. The included barn doors further enhance shaping capabilities, while the rugged construction ensures reliability and durability on location or in the studio.



Specifications

  • Lamp Type: 150W tungsten halogen
  • Color Temperature: 3200K (approx.)
  • Focusing Range: 4.5° to 45° (spot to flood)
  • CRI: ~90
  • Power Requirements: 100-240V AC (via included power supply)
  • Dimensions: ~ 25 x 12 x 12 cm
  • Weight: ~1.3 kg (fixture only)


Scope of Delivery

  • 1x Dedo 150W Light Fixture
  • 1x Power Supply
  • 1x Set of Barn Doors
  • 1x Standard Cable (AC)
  • 1x Protective Carrying Case (optional, if available)


Key Features

  • Compact and lightweight tungsten spotlight
  • Wide focusing range for precise beam control
  • High color rendering for accurate skin tones and colors
  • Low power draw with bright output
  • Durable construction for professional use
