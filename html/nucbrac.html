Nucleus-M Bracket



Inventory

1 Available



The Nucleus-M Bracket is a mounting solution designed specifically for the Tilta Nucleus-M wireless follow focus system. It allows secure and versatile attachment of the hand unit or controller module to a variety of rig components, including handles, rods, cages, and camera builds.

The bracket provides solid positioning for either handheld or mounted operation, and helps integrate the Nucleus-M seamlessly into your camera setup for optimal ergonomics and control.

Built from lightweight aluminum, it includes standard 1/4"-20 threads, ARRI-style anti-twist pins, or rod-mounting clamps depending on configuration, making it a versatile accessory for assistant camera operators and operators alike.



Specifications

  • Compatibility: Tilta Nucleus-M controller & hand unit
  • Mount Options: 1/4"-20 screw, ARRI anti-twist, rod clamp
  • Material: CNC-machined aluminum alloy
  • Weight: Approx. 150g
  • Adjustability: Tiltable or rotatable (model-dependent)


Key Features

  • Mount your Nucleus-M controller or hand unit securely to your rig
  • Supports various mounting standards including ARRI pins and rods
  • Perfect for gimbals, handheld rigs, and shoulder setups
  • Lightweight, low-profile, and durable
  • Essential accessory for wireless focus workflows
