C-Stand 20



Inventory

2 Available



The C-Stand 20 is a highly versatile and durable grip stand designed for use in film, television, and photography production. It is perfect for holding lights, modifiers, flags, reflectors, and other accessories securely in place during shoots. The 20-inch version of the classic Century Stand offers a low-profile height for precise positioning in a variety of environments.

Built from high-quality chrome-plated steel, the C-Stand 20 is incredibly sturdy and resistant to wear, making it ideal for both indoor and outdoor shoots. The stand comes equipped with a 2-section riser and detachable turtle base, allowing for easy transport and setup. The turtle base can fold flat for quick packing, while the grip head and arm enable precise adjustments for positioning flags, nets, and other modifiers exactly where you need them.

The C-Stand 20 is a staple of professional grip departments, providing exceptional strength, flexibility, and ease of use. Its steel construction and wide base ensure stability, even with heavier gear, and its compact size makes it ideal for use in tight spaces or locations where larger stands are impractical.

  • Material: Chrome-plated steel
  • Height Range: 0.87 m to 1.65 m (34.2 inches to 65 inches)
  • Risers: 2-section riser
  • Base Type: Detachable turtle base
  • Weight Capacity: 10 kg (22 lbs)
  • Weight: 4.2 kg (9.26 lbs)
  • Arm Length: 51 cm (20 inches)
