The Boom Holder for C-Stands is an essential accessory for securely mounting a sound boom pole on C-stands during film and video production. It provides a sturdy and reliable grip, allowing operators to maintain precise boom positioning for optimal sound capture without fatigue. Its adjustable design fits a variety of boom pole sizes and ensures quick setup and takedown.



Specifications

  • Compatible Stands: Fits all standard C-stands
  • Material: High-quality steel/aluminum construction
  • Adjustable Grip: Fits multiple boom pole sizes
  • Use Cases: Ideal for film, TV, and sound production environments
  • Weight Capacity: Supports standard and heavy-duty boom poles
