HDMI to SDI Converter



Inventory

1 Available



The HDMI to SDI Converter enables you to seamlessly convert an HDMI signal to a professional-grade SDI stream. This device is ideal for bridging consumer or prosumer gear (e.g., DSLRs, laptops, or media players) into broadcast or live production workflows that rely on SDI infrastructure.

Compact and robust, this converter offers support for common video resolutions and standards, providing stable and reliable signal output. Perfect for live events, studio setups, or on-the-go productions, it offers quick setup with minimal complexity.



Specifications

  • Input: 1x HDMI (up to 1080p60 or above, model-dependent)
  • Output: SDI (supports up to 3G-SDI, again model-dependent)
  • Audio: Embedded HDMI audio passed through to SDI output
  • Power Source: External power supply (included)
  • Weight: Approx. X g/lbs


Key Features

  • HDMI to SDI Conversion: Integrate consumer/prosumer devices into professional broadcast systems.
  • Stable Performance: Maintains a reliable signal with minimal latency.
  • Compact & Portable: Lightweight form factor simplifies transport and on-location use.
  • Easy Setup: Plug-and-play design; no additional drivers or complex configurations required.
  • Durable Construction: Built to withstand the demands of studio work and remote productions.
