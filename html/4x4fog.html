The 4x4 Fog Filter is a specialized filter designed to create a soft, diffused effect in your footage. It is used to add a sense of atmosphere, softness, or dreaminess to your shots, making it an essential tool for filmmakers working in genres that require a cinematic, ethereal look. This filter is ideal for creating haze, mist, or other atmospheric effects, providing a subtle touch to portraits, landscapes, and environmental shots.

Constructed from high-quality optical glass, the 4x4 Fog Filter is lightweight and durable, ensuring long-lasting performance without compromising on image quality. It fits into standard 4x4" matte boxes, allowing for easy integration with a wide range of camera setups. Whether you're working on a feature film, commercial, or music video, this filter can help you achieve the desired look with ease.

The fog filter creates a mild diffusion effect that softens the overall image, enhancing contrasts and adding a touch of mist or haze without losing detail in the scene. It is a great tool for filmmakers looking to create mood and atmosphere in their shots, helping to elevate the overall visual aesthetic.
