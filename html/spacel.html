The **Aputure Space Light** is an innovative and versatile lighting modifier that softens and diffuses light to create an even, wraparound glow, making it ideal for cinematographers and photographers. It is particularly effective for use with Aputure’s light sources like the **LS 600d Pro** and **LS 1200d Pro** lights, as well as other lighting systems with Bowens mounts.

Featuring a lightweight and collapsible design, the Space Light is easy to transport and set up. It provides a soft, high-quality light output without harsh shadows, making it perfect for creating high-key lighting, simulating daylight, or adding a dramatic soft look to your subject.

The Space Light's unique spherical design ensures an even distribution of light across the surface area, providing a 360-degree light coverage. Whether you’re working in the studio or on location, the Aputure Space Light is a must-have modifier for anyone seeking to achieve stunningly soft, flattering lighting.
