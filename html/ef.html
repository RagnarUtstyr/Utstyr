The Metabones EF-E Mount Adapter allows Canon EF and EF-S lenses to be mounted on Sony E-mount cameras. Designed for professional filmmaking and photography, this adapter preserves electronic communication between lens and camera, enabling aperture control, autofocus, and image stabilization.

Built with precision-machined brass and stainless steel mounts, the adapter ensures a secure, accurate fit while maintaining proper flange distance for infinity focus. It supports metadata transmission (EXIF data), making it ideal for hybrid shooters who rely on Canon glass with Sony cinema or mirrorless bodies.

The adapter features a detachable tripod foot for added support when using heavier lenses, reducing strain on the camera mount during professional production setups.
