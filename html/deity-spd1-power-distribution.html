Deity SPD-1 Power Distribution



Inventory

1 Available



The Deity SPD-1 Power Distribution is a professional-grade power distribution system designed for wireless audio setups, ensuring that all of your gear is efficiently powered from a single source. With multiple output ports, the SPD-1 can distribute power to several devices simultaneously, such as wireless receivers, transmitters, and other audio gear.

Built to support a wide range of professional equipment, the SPD-1 provides four Hirose 4-pin outputs and a two-pin LEMO DC output, ensuring compatibility with most industry-standard devices. The device also features voltage monitoring and overload protection, ensuring safe and stable operation even in demanding environments.

The SPD-1’s rugged metal construction ensures durability while remaining lightweight and compact, making it ideal for on-set use or location shoots. It includes a belt clip for easy portability, allowing sound mixers and audio professionals to move freely while maintaining reliable power distribution throughout their setup.



Specifications

  • Model: Deity SPD-1 Power Distribution
  • Output Ports: 4x Hirose 4-pin, 1x LEMO 2-pin DC
  • Input Voltage: 12-18V DC
  • Power Protection: Overload protection, short circuit protection
  • Dimensions: 12 x 8 x 3 cm (4.7 x 3.1 x 1.2 inches)
  • Weight: 0.4 kg (0.88 lbs)


Key Features

  • Distributes power to multiple wireless audio devices from a single source
  • 4x Hirose 4-pin and 1x LEMO 2-pin DC output ports
  • Overload and short circuit protection for reliable operation
  • Compact, lightweight, and durable design
  • Includes a belt clip for easy portability on set
  • Ideal for professional sound mixers and audio technicians
