The 77mm Variable ND Filter is a versatile tool designed for photographers and filmmakers to control exposure in bright lighting conditions without affecting the color balance. This filter allows you to adjust the amount of light entering the lens, providing the flexibility to shoot at wider apertures or slower shutter speeds even in bright environments.

With a variable design, this ND filter provides a range of light reduction from 2 to 8 stops, allowing for precise control over your exposure settings. Whether you're capturing cinematic footage or stills, this filter is ideal for outdoor shoots, especially when you're looking to achieve shallow depth of field effects in bright sunlight or long exposure shots.

Constructed from high-quality optical glass, the 77mm Variable ND Filter ensures excellent clarity and sharpness. The filter's slim design is compatible with most lenses with a 77mm filter thread, making it a perfect addition to your camera gear for both photography and videography.
