The Beyerdynamic DT 770 Pro is a professional-grade, closed-back headphone designed for critical listening in studio and professional environments. Known for its exceptional sound isolation, it ensures precise audio reproduction across all frequencies, making it ideal for monitoring, mixing, and casual listening. With a comfortable, over-ear design, these headphones allow long periods of use without causing fatigue.



Specifications

  • Design: Closed-back, over-ear design
  • Impedance: 80 Ohms
  • Frequency Response: 5 Hz to 35 kHz
  • Cable Length: 3-meter coiled cable
  • Use Cases: Ideal for studio monitoring, mixing, and professional audio applications
  • Comfort: Soft, replaceable ear pads for extended sessions
