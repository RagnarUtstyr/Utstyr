Astera Titan LED Tube



Inventory

2 Available



The Astera Titan Tube is a professional-grade RGB, mint, and amber LED light designed to provide versatile, high-quality lighting for film, photography, and events. With its precise color control and a wide color temperature range of 1750K to 20,000K, the Titan Tube delivers stunning lighting with exceptional accuracy, achieving CRI and TLCI ratings of 96+. This LED tube is known for its high output, ease of use, and portability, making it a go-to choice for both indoor and outdoor production.

The Astera Titan Tube features wireless control through the AsteraApp and can also be integrated into larger lighting setups via DMX control using the Astera ART7 CRMX transmitter. With its internal rechargeable battery, the Titan Tube can run for up to 20 hours on a single charge, providing flexible, reliable performance in any environment. The magnetic mounting options and IP65 rating for water and dust resistance ensure it can be used in various settings with ease.

Each Titan Tube can be programmed with a variety of lighting effects, including strobe, pulse, and color fade, making it ideal for creative lighting setups. Whether used as a practical light or for atmospheric effects, the Titan Tube's lightweight and durable construction ensures it can withstand the demands of any production.
