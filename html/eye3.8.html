This 3/8" Thread Eyebolt is an ideal solution for securing heavier camera rigs, accessories, or additional grip equipment. Commonly used for rigging, tethering, or suspending gear, it adds a stable mounting point that integrates seamlessly into most professional workflows.

Crafted from robust steel with a rust-resistant coating, the eyebolt ensures reliable fastening without damaging your accessories. The open ring design makes it easy to attach carabiners, safety cables, or rope, helping safeguard your gear from accidental drops or rough handling on set.

With a universal 3/8" thread, it’s compatible with many camera cages, grip plates, and support systems, providing a dependable anchor point wherever additional security is required.
