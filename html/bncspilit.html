BNC Splitter (T-Adapter)



The BNC Splitter, also known as a BNC T-Adapter, is a passive splitter that allows you to connect two BNC devices to a single BNC source. It's commonly used for timecode distribution, monitoring SDI signals, or duplicating a sync signal to multiple devices on set.

Designed with one male BNC connector and two female BNC outputs, this adapter maintains signal integrity while offering a convenient and compact form factor. It’s essential in broadcast, camera, or sound department kits.

While passive splitters reduce signal strength slightly, they’re highly effective when used with buffered outputs or short cable runs.



Specifications

  • Type: BNC T-Adapter (1 Male to 2 Female)
  • Impedance: 75 Ohm
  • Use Case: Timecode, SDI, sync signal duplication
  • Connector Material: Nickel-plated brass
  • Compatibility: Supports SD, HD, 3G-SDI signals


Key Features

  • Splits a BNC signal to two destinations
  • Compact and rugged T-adapter design
  • Works for SDI video, timecode, sync, and RF
  • Standard 75 Ohm broadcast signal compatibility
  • Essential for multi-device signal distribution
