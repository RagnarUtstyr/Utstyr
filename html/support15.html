Lens Support for 15mm Rods



Inventory

1 Available



This adjustable lens support is designed for 15mm rod-based camera rigs, providing extra stability and preventing lens droop or strain. Ideal for longer or heavier lenses, it helps maintain precise alignment for both manual and motorized focus systems, ensuring sharper imagery and reducing stress on your camera mount.

Constructed from durable aluminum alloy, the lens support features an adjustable center column with a cushioned cradle, accommodating a wide range of lens diameters. Once fastened to the dual 15mm rod system, it locks tightly into place to keep your lens stable during movements, panning, or tilt shots.

Whether you’re shooting on location or in the studio, this lens support minimizes the risk of lens mount damage and delivers added confidence for your setup.



Specifications

  • Model: 15mm Rod Lens Support
  • Rod System: Standard 15mm spacing
  • Material: Aluminum alloy
  • Height Adjustment: Up to 35 mm from rod center
  • Weight: Approx. 150 g (0.33 lbs)
  • Compatibility: Fits most lens diameters up to 114 mm


Key Features

  • Prevents lens sag and maintains perfect alignment
  • Quickly clamps onto standard 15mm rod systems
  • Cushioned cradle protects lens surfaces
  • Height-adjustable design to match various lens sizes
  • Sturdy aluminum construction for long-term reliability
