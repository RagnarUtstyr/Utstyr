Manbily MFL-06 Bi-Color LED



Inventory

2 Available



The Manbily MFL-06 Bi-Color LED is a compact and versatile video light designed for creators on the go. Offering an adjustable color temperature range from 3200K (warm) to 5600K (daylight), this LED panel makes it easy to achieve the perfect look for interviews, product demos, or vlogging. With its built-in battery and sleek design, the MFL-06 is equally at home in a studio setup or out on location.

Featuring a high CRI rating (95+) for accurate color rendering, it ensures that skin tones and scene details appear natural and vibrant. The intuitive controls allow you to dial in brightness and color temperature quickly, and the built-in display keeps your settings clearly visible. This versatile light also comes with a cold shoe mount for easy attachment to your camera or stand.



Specifications

  • Power Output: 6W (approx.)
  • Color Temperature Range: 3200K – 5600K
  • CRI: ≥95
  • Brightness Control: 0–100% Dimmable
  • Power Source: Built-in lithium battery (USB rechargeable)
  • Runtime: ~1.5 hours at full brightness (varies by settings)
  • Dimensions: ~ 110 x 70 x 15 mm
  • Weight: ~150 g


Scope of Delivery

  • 1x Manbily MFL-06 Bi-Color LED Light
  • 1x USB Charging Cable
  • 1x Cold Shoe Adapter
  • 1x Protective Carrying Pouch


Key Features

  • Bi-color adjustability for versatile lighting setups
  • High CRI for accurate color reproduction
  • Lightweight, portable design with built-in battery
  • Easy brightness and color temperature control
  • Includes cold shoe adapter for mounting
