Dynacore D-TS V-Mount Hot Swap Plate/Dual Battery "Sharkfin" Adaptor



Inventory

1 Available



The Dynacore D-TS V-Mount Hot Swap Plate, often referred to as a "Sharkfin" adaptor, is a dual-battery mounting solution designed to keep your camera or equipment powered continuously. By allowing two V-Mount batteries to be mounted at the same time, it ensures you can replace one depleted battery with a fresh one—without ever losing power on set.

Built with durable materials and featuring a streamlined design, the D-TS Hot Swap Plate is compact and lightweight, making it ideal for on-the-go productions. Its secure locking mechanism and universal V-Mount connection guarantee a reliable fit, whether you're using DSLR rigs, cinema cameras, or other broadcast equipment.

LED indicators and convenient D-Tap outputs (depending on model) provide additional versatility, allowing you to power accessories such as monitors, lights, or wireless transmitters. Engineered for professional use, the Dynacore D-TS plate ensures smooth production workflows by minimizing downtime and guaranteeing continuous operation.



Specifications

  • Model: Dynacore D-TS V-Mount Hot Swap Plate
  • Battery Type: V-Mount (14.4V)
  • Max Load (approx.): 10A–15A (depending on battery)
  • Dimensions (approx.): 146 x 95 x 65 mm
  • Weight (approx.): 0.6 kg (1.3 lbs)
  • Outputs: Dual V-Mount plus optional D-Tap outputs
  • LED Indicators: Battery & Power Status


Key Features

  • Hot Swap functionality ensures uninterrupted power
  • Dual V-Mount battery support for extended runtime
  • Lightweight, durable design for professional shoots
  • Secure locking mechanism with ARRI-standard mounting points
  • LED status indicators and possible D-Tap outputs for accessories
