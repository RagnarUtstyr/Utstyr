Aputure LS 300x Bi-Color LED Light



Inventory

1 Available



The Aputure LS 300x is a powerful bi-color LED light that combines high output with variable color temperature, giving filmmakers and photographers the flexibility to adjust the lighting from 2700K to 6500K. With its 150W COB (Chip on Board) LED technology, the LS 300x delivers up to 40,000 lux of brightness at 1 meter with its included hyper reflector, making it ideal for a wide range of professional lighting applications.

The LS 300x features stepless dimming from 0% to 100%, allowing for precise control of brightness. Its bi-color control offers continuous color temperature adjustments between tungsten (warm) and daylight (cool), making it easy to match ambient light conditions or achieve creative lighting setups. The Bowens Mount compatibility ensures versatility, allowing the attachment of a wide range of light modifiers, such as softboxes and Fresnel lenses.

The LS 300x also supports Sidus Link App control, enabling wireless adjustments of brightness and color temperature. The fixture is equipped with 9 lighting effects, including Lightning, Paparazzi, TV, Strobe, and Fireworks, making it a powerful tool for both creative and practical lighting. With dual power options—AC or V-Mount/Gold Mount battery— the LS 300x is portable and ready for use in both studio and location shoots.



Specifications

  • Model: Aputure LS 300x
  • Power Output: 350W
  • Color Temperature Range: 2700K to 6500K
  • Brightness (with Hyper Reflector): 40,000 lux at 1 meter
  • CRI: 95+
  • TLCI: 95+
  • Beam Angle: 55° (with reflector)
  • Dimming: Stepless dimming (0%-100%)
  • Control: Sidus Link App (Wireless), Onboard controls
  • Special Effects: Lightning, Paparazzi, TV, Strobe, Fireworks, and more
  • Power Supply: AC or V-Mount/Gold Mount battery (optional)
  • Mount Type: Bowens Mount
  • Weight (Light Head): 5.5 kg
  • Dimensions (Light Head): 340 x 260 x 340 mm

  • High brightness with 40,000 lux output at 1 meter (with Hyper Reflector)
  • Stepless dimming from 0% to 100% for precise brightness control
