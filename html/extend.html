The Vocas Rosette Extender is a versatile and essential accessory for extending rosette-based systems in professional camera rigs. This extender allows users to adjust the position of rosette-mounted accessories, providing additional flexibility and customization. Ideal for camera operators who require precise and customizable rig setups, the Vocas Rosette Extender enables smoother adjustments for achieving optimal comfort and balance.

Made from high-grade aluminum, the Vocas Rosette Extender offers robust durability while maintaining a lightweight design. It is compatible with standard rosette systems and works seamlessly with a variety of camera and rig accessories, making it a must-have tool for filmmakers and camera operators seeking versatile rig setups.

Whether you're adjusting the position of a matte box, follow focus, or other rosette-mounted equipment, this extender helps you achieve the perfect setup. Its simple and secure installation makes it easy to implement in any environment.
