Tilta MB T-12 Matte Box



Inventory

1 Available



The Tilta MB T-12 Matte Box is a professional 4x5.65 clamp-on matte box designed for cinema cameras and large-sensor DSLR cameras. The MB T-12 provides efficient control of light, helping to block unwanted glare and flares while offering a sleek, lightweight design. This makes it an essential tool for cinematographers and videographers who need precise light control while keeping their rig lightweight.

The MB T-12 is compatible with 4x5.65 filters, allowing users to quickly change and stack filters to adjust exposure, contrast, and color balance without altering the camera's settings. It clamps directly onto lenses of various sizes using adapter rings to fit diameters ranging from 80mm to 114mm. The matte box's carbon fiber construction ensures durability while remaining lightweight, making it ideal for both handheld and gimbal setups.

The Tilta MB T-12 also includes a top flag to further reduce lens flare from overhead lighting and offers rotating filter stages for variable filter adjustment. Its design is simple and efficient, allowing for quick assembly and breakdown in fast-paced shooting environments.



Specifications

  • Type: Clamp-on matte box
  • Filter Size: 4x5.65 inch
  • Lens Compatibility: Fits lenses with 80mm to 114mm diameter
  • Construction: Carbon fiber
  • Included Accessories: Top flag, adapter rings
  • Weight: 300g (0.66 lbs)
