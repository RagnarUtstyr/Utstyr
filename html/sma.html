SMA → SMA Adapter



This SMA to SMA adapter is used to connect two SMA-terminated RF cables or devices. It's ideal for wireless antenna setups, timecode sync units, video transmitter systems, or any gear using standard SMA connections.

The adapter features male SMA connectors on both ends with a precision threaded interface, ensuring a secure and low-loss RF connection. Compact and durable, it's a must-have for RF kits, wireless audio systems, and production carts.



Specifications

  • Connector Type: SMA male to SMA male
  • Impedance: 50 Ohm
  • Frequency Range: DC – 6 GHz
  • Build: Gold-plated brass or stainless steel
  • Style: Straight in-line adapter
  • Use Case: RF, antennas, timecode, wireless video


Key Features

  • Connects two SMA-terminated cables or ports
  • Reliable and secure threaded RF connection
  • Low-loss signal transfer for high-frequency applications
  • Durable, compact, and field-friendly design
  • Perfect for wireless, antenna, and timecode setups
