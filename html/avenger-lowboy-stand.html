The Avenger Lowboy Stand is a durable and sturdy stand designed to support heavy lighting and grip equipment. With its low-profile design, the Lowboy is perfect for supporting larger fixtures at low heights, making it an essential tool for professional film and photography setups. The stand is made from chrome-plated steel, ensuring long-lasting durability and stability, even under heavy loads.

The Lowboy Stand features two risers and a 16mm (5/8") baby pin spigot, allowing for the secure mounting of lights, flags, and other accessories. Its wide base ensures that the stand remains stable, even when fully extended, while the compact design makes it ideal for use in tight spaces or when shooting in confined areas.

Built to withstand the demands of professional production, the Avenger Lowboy Stand can support equipment weighing up to 25 kg and reaches a maximum height of 1.23 meters (4 feet). The stand is perfect for use with heavier light fixtures, such as large LED panels, Fresnels, or strobes, offering both strength and versatility for any lighting setup.
