3.5mm to 6.3mm AUX Adapter



Inventory

10 Available



This 3.5mm to 6.3mm AUX adapter is the perfect solution for musicians, audio professionals, or anyone needing to connect devices with a 3.5mm output to equipment with a 6.3mm input. Whether you’re hooking up headphones to professional audio gear, plugging a smartphone into an amplifier, or connecting an instrument cable, this adapter delivers reliable performance.

Designed with a sturdy casing, it ensures durability for both studio and on-the-go use. Its compact design makes it easy to carry in your pocket or gear bag. A secure fit on both the 3.5mm and 6.3mm ends helps minimize noise and loss of signal, guaranteeing clear audio quality every time.

Thanks to universal compatibility, you can confidently use this adapter with headphones, mixers, audio interfaces, instruments, and much more. Experience enhanced audio fidelity and convenience without complicated setup or bulky equipment.



Specifications

  • Connector A: 3.5mm (1/8 inch) male stereo plug
  • Connector B: 6.3mm (1/4 inch) female stereo jack
  • Material: Sturdy metal construction with secure connectors
  • Dimensions: Approx. 45mm (length) x 10mm (diameter)
  • Weight: 0.01 kg (0.02 lbs)
  • Compatibility: Works with most 3.5mm and 6.3mm audio devices


Key Features

  • Converts 3.5mm male to 6.3mm female connections
  • Durable metal construction for long-lasting use
  • Compact and portable design for easy transport
  • Secure fit to prevent noise and signal loss
  • Compatible with headphones, mixers, amplifiers, and more
