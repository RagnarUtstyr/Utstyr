Sound Devices 302



Inventory

2 Available



The Sound Devices 302 is a compact and professional 3-channel field audio mixer designed for ENG (Electronic News Gathering), documentary production, and on-location audio recording. Known for its robust build, high-quality audio performance, and portability, the 302 is ideal for sound professionals who require reliability and high-performance sound mixing in the field.

This mixer features three XLR microphone/line inputs with high-quality preamps that deliver clean, low-noise performance, making it ideal for dialogue and natural sound capture. Each input has independent controls, including low-cut filters, limiters, and phantom power for condenser microphones. With its intuitive LED metering and clear layout, the Sound Devices 302 ensures that sound professionals can easily monitor and adjust levels on the fly, even in challenging recording environments.

The 302 offers dual balanced outputs via XLR connectors, making it easy to send a clean, high-quality audio signal to external recording devices or cameras. The rugged construction and aluminum chassis ensure durability in harsh environments, while the compact size makes it easy to carry in any production setup.



Specifications

  • Model: Sound Devices 302
  • Channels: 3-channel mixer
  • Inputs: 3x XLR (mic/line level)
  • Outputs: 2x XLR balanced outputs
  • Phantom Power: +48V phantom power (individually selectable)
  • Low-Cut Filters: Switchable high-pass filters (80 Hz and 160 Hz)
  • Limiter: Input and output limiters for overload protection
  • Power Source: 2x AA batteries or external DC (5-18V)
  • Weight: 0.9 kg (2 lbs)
  • Dimensions: 50mm x 200mm x 120mm


Key Features

  • Compact 3-channel field mixer for ENG, documentaries, and on-location recording
  • High-quality microphone preamps for low-noise, clean audio
  • Phantom power and low-cut filters for each input
  • Input and output limiters for audio overload protection
  • Dual balanced XLR outputs for connection to cameras and recorders
  • Robust aluminum chassis for durability in challenging environments
  • Powered by AA batteries or external DC input
