Sennheiser MKE600



Inventory

1 Available



The Sennheiser MKE600 is a professional-grade shotgun microphone designed for video production, film, broadcast, and field recording. This highly directional microphone excels in capturing clear and focused audio in noisy or uncontrolled environments, making it ideal for on-location shoots, interviews, and sound effects recording.

Featuring a supercardioid/lobar pickup pattern, the MKE600 effectively rejects off-axis noise, focusing on the sound source directly in front of the microphone. It includes a low-cut filter switch to eliminate low-frequency background noise like wind and rumble, ensuring pristine audio quality in a variety of recording conditions.

The Sennheiser MKE600 can be powered either by phantom power from professional cameras and recorders or by a single AA battery for up to 150 hours of continuous use. The durable metal construction ensures longevity and reliability, even in challenging environments.



Specifications

  • Model: Sennheiser MKE600
  • Microphone Type: Shotgun microphone
  • Pickup Pattern: Supercardioid/lobar
  • Frequency Response: 40Hz - 20kHz
  • Low-Cut Filter: 100Hz switchable filter
  • Power Source: Phantom power (48V) or 1x AA battery
  • Battery Life: Up to 150 hours on a single AA battery
  • Connector: XLR-3M
  • Dimensions: 256mm x 20mm (10.08" x 0.79")
  • Weight: 128g (4.52 oz)


Key Features

  • Highly directional shotgun microphone for clear, focused audio capture
  • Supercardioid/lobar pickup pattern for excellent off-axis noise rejection
  • Switchable low-cut filter to minimize low-frequency noise and rumble
  • Powered by either phantom power or a single AA battery
  • Up to 150 hours of battery life on a single AA battery
  • Rugged, lightweight metal construction for durability
  • Perfect for video production, film, broadcast, and field recording
