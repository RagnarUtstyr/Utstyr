Sennheiser G2 IEM



Inventory

1 Available



The Sennheiser G2 IEM (In-Ear Monitor) System is a professional-grade wireless monitoring solution designed for musicians, audio engineers, and production crews. This system provides clear, high-fidelity sound in live performance and production environments, ensuring that performers can monitor their audio with minimal latency and interference.

Operating in the UHF frequency range, the Sennheiser G2 IEM offers reliable and interference-free transmission. The system includes a bodypack receiver and a pair of in-ear monitors, delivering personalized monitoring for each user. The receiver features an easy-to-read display for monitoring signal strength, battery life, and channel selection, ensuring quick and easy setup even in fast-paced environments.

The G2 IEM system also features stereo and mono operation modes, allowing users to customize their mix to suit their needs. It is built for durability and long-lasting performance, making it a trusted choice for live concerts, theater performances, and broadcast productions.



Specifications

  • Model: Sennheiser G2 IEM
  • Type: Wireless In-Ear Monitor System
  • Frequency Range: UHF 626 - 668 MHz
  • Receiver Type: Bodypack
  • Operation Modes: Stereo and Mono
  • Output: 3.5mm jack for earphones
  • Power Source: 2x AA batteries
  • Battery Life: Up to 8 hours
  • Range: Up to 100 meters (328 feet)
  • Weight: 200g (7 oz) (receiver)


Key Features

  • Wireless UHF system for clear, interference-free monitoring
  • Bodypack receiver with easy-to-read display for quick setup
  • Stereo and mono operation modes for personalized sound monitoring
  • Range of up to 100 meters (328 feet) for live stage or production use
  • Includes high-quality in-ear monitors for clear audio
  • Durable and lightweight design, ideal for live performances and production work
  • Long battery life of up to 8 hours with standard AA batteries
