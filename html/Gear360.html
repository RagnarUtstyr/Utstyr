Samsung Gear 360



Inventory

1 Available



The Samsung Gear 360 is a compact 360-degree camera designed to capture immersive photos and videos. Equipped with dual-lens technology, it can seamlessly stitch together footage from both ultra-wide lenses to produce high-resolution 360° images and videos. Ideal for travelers, content creators, or anyone looking to record full panoramic experiences, the Gear 360 makes it easy to relive the moment from every angle.

With up to 4K video resolution and a user-friendly design, the Gear 360 integrates smoothly with compatible Samsung smartphones and other devices. Its built-in Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and USB-C connectivity allow for quick transfers and easy editing via the dedicated Gear 360 app. Thanks to its lightweight build and IP53-rated splash and dust resistance, this camera can accompany you on a variety of adventures.

Whether you’re filming a live event, documenting a trip, or shooting creative social media content, the Samsung Gear 360 offers an accessible entry point into 360-degree production. Its long battery life and removable memory card support ensure that you can capture everything without worrying about running out of space or power.



Specifications

  • Model: Samsung Gear 360
  • Lens: Dual 15 MP fisheye lenses
  • Photo Resolution: Up to 30 MP (combined)
  • Video Resolution: Up to 4K (4096 x 2048) at 24 fps
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, USB-C
  • Storage: microSD card (up to 256GB)
  • Battery: 1160 mAh removable battery
  • Water & Dust Resistance: IP53 rating
  • Weight: Approximately 152g


Key Features

  • Full 360° video and photo capture with dual-lens design
  • Up to 4K video resolution for immersive footage
  • Compact and lightweight form factor for easy portability
  • Seamless integration with compatible Samsung devices via Gear 360 app
  • Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and USB-C connectivity for fast transfers
  • Removable microSD card support up to 256GB
  • IP53 splash and dust resistance
  • Long battery life for extended shooting sessions
