The Arri Follow Focus Speed Crank is an accessory designed for use with Arri's follow focus systems, enabling faster and more dynamic focus pulls. Ideal for precise or rapid movements, the crank helps operators maintain control and speed when focusing during complex shots.

Crafted from high-quality materials, the speed crank includes a rotating handle with a bearing for smooth operation and reduced fatigue over long takes. It’s particularly useful for focus pullers in handheld, shoulder, or Steadicam configurations where standard focus knobs might be too slow or awkward.

The crank attaches easily to the focus knob and is compatible with most Arri follow focus units including the FF-4 and FF-5. Whether you’re working on high-speed tracking shots or need a quick rack focus, the Arri Speed Crank offers a compact, intuitive solution.
