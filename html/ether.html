The USB-C to Gigabit Ethernet Dongle provides fast, stable wired internet connectivity for USB-C-equipped devices such as laptops, tablets, and mobile production gear. Ideal for live streaming setups, low-latency remote work, or anywhere Wi-Fi isn't reliable, this compact dongle ensures seamless integration into your workflow.

Compatible with macOS, Windows, iPadOS, and many modern devices, this plug-and-play adapter supports full 10/100/1000 Mbps Ethernet speeds. It’s a must-have tool for broadcasters, editors, and content creators working in unpredictable network environments.
