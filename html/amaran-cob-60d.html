Amaran COB 60d S Daylight LED Spotlight



Inventory

2 Available



The Amaran COB 60d S is a 65W Daylight LED Spotlight designed to deliver high-quality, consistent light output with improved spectral performance. With a colour temperature of 5600 K, this LED light is perfect for daylight-balanced setups, and it comes with a Bowens Mount for attaching various accessories. It is dimmable in 1% increments and is fully compatible with the Sidus Link App for remote control.

The COB 60d S features 8 special lighting effects, including Paparazzi, Fireworks, Lightning, Faulty Bulb, TV, Pulse, Strobe, and Explosion, giving you creative flexibility for various lighting scenarios. Its quiet active cooling system ensures silent operation, and the central control knob and LC display make it easy to operate. The spotlight delivers 7195 lm of brightness, with a CRI of 96+ and TLCI of 99+, ensuring excellent color accuracy.

The included hyperreflector focuses the beam angle to 14°, while the wide-angle beam spreads up to 108° without the reflector. The Amaran COB 60d S can be powered via AC power or an optional V-mount battery. It is compact, lightweight, and portable, making it an ideal choice for filmmakers and photographers working in studios or on location.
