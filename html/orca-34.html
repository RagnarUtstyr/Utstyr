Orca OR-34 Soundbag



Inventory

1 Available



The Orca OR-34 Soundbag is designed for audio professionals who need a highly durable and functional solution for carrying and protecting sound equipment on location. Specifically crafted for medium to large-sized audio setups, the OR-34 offers a versatile design that fits field mixers and recorders, along with wireless systems, cables, and additional accessories.

Made with aluminum and durable thermoplastic, the Orca OR-34 provides a lightweight yet rigid structure that protects your equipment from damage while ensuring ease of access. The bag features clear TPU panels on the top and sides, allowing visibility of your gear while keeping it safe from dust and moisture. With side access panels and a detachable front pocket, it's easy to connect cables and adjust settings without removing your gear from the bag.

The Orca OR-34 also includes an internal honeycomb frame that offers reinforced protection and structure. A set of adjustable dividers allows for flexible compartmentalization, ensuring that your equipment is securely organized. The bag is designed to be comfortable for long-duration use, with padded shoulder straps and additional options for mounting to an Orca harness.



Specifications

  • Model: Orca OR-34
  • Type: Soundbag for field mixers, recorders, and accessories
  • Material: Aluminum and thermoplastic with clear TPU panels
  • Internal Frame: Honeycomb structure for extra protection
  • Dimensions (External): 34 x 22 x 17 cm
  • Weight: 1.5 kg (3.3 lbs)
  • Compatibility: Fits medium to large field mixers and recorders


Key Features

  • Durable soundbag designed for professional audio equipment
  • Clear TPU panels on top and sides for easy equipment visibility
  • Honeycomb internal frame for reinforced protection
  • Side access panels for easy cable management
  • Adjustable dividers for flexible compartmentalization
  • Comfortable shoulder straps and compatibility with Orca harness
  • Lightweight yet rigid design with high durability
