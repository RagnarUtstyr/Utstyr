Cinefoil



Inventory

1 Roll Available



Cinefoil is a flexible, heat-resistant, matte black aluminum foil designed to shape, block, or mask off unwanted light. Its non-reflective surface effectively absorbs light, making it easy to control spill and prevent glare on set. Quick to cut and mold, Cinefoil is a favorite among gaffers and cinematographers for making custom barn doors, flags, or gobos in a pinch.

Whether you're blacking out a window edge, wrapping a light to reduce flare, or shaping a beam for precise lighting effects, Cinefoil provides a portable, cost-effective solution. It’s especially handy for on-location shoots where speed and adaptability are critical, and packs neatly into most gear bags.



Specifications

  • Type: Matte black aluminum foil
  • Width: Approx. 12 inches (30 cm)
  • Length: Approx. 50 feet (15 m) per roll
  • Temperature Resistance: Heat-resistant for use near hot lights
  • Weight: ~1.5 kg per roll
  • Usage: Light masking, shaping, and spill control


Scope of Delivery

  • 1x Roll of Cinefoil


Key Features

  • Matte black surface minimizes reflections
  • Highly flexible and easy to shape or cut
  • Heat-resistant for use on or near hot fixtures
  • Lightweight and portable
  • Ideal for blocking light leaks, creating flags, or crafting quick DIY modifiers
