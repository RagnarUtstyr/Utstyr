Sennheiser ew 112P G4



Inventory

2 Available



The Sennheiser ew 112P G4 is a professional-grade wireless lavalier microphone system designed for film, broadcast, and field recording applications. This robust and versatile system provides high-quality audio transmission, making it perfect for documentary work, interviews, presentations, and on-the-go productions. The system includes a ME 2-II omnidirectional lavalier microphone, a bodypack transmitter, and a portable receiver.

Operating in the UHF range, the ew 112P G4 provides reliable transmission with up to 1680 tunable frequencies across a 42 MHz bandwidth to ensure clear, interference-free audio. The system has an operational range of up to 100 meters (328 feet), making it highly flexible for location shoots and large venues.

The receiver and transmitter feature easy-to-read LCD displays for intuitive control and monitoring of frequency, battery life, and signal strength. The system offers automatic frequency scan for quick setup and squelch control to minimize RF interference. With up to 8 hours of battery life, the ew 112P G4 is designed for long-duration shoots and productions.



Specifications

  • Model: Sennheiser ew 112P G4
  • Microphone Type: Lavalier (ME 2-II omnidirectional)
  • Frequency Range: UHF 566 - 608 MHz (G-Band)
  • Number of Channels: 1680 tunable frequencies
  • Bandwidth: 42 MHz
  • Range: Up to 100 meters (328 feet)
  • Receiver Type: Portable receiver
  • Battery Life: Up to 8 hours
  • Connector Type: 3.5mm TRS jack
  • Power Source: 2x AA batteries
  • Weight (Transmitter): 160g (5.6 oz)
  • Weight (Receiver): 200g (7 oz)


Key Features

  • Wireless lavalier microphone system designed for film, broadcast, and field recording
  • ME 2-II omnidirectional lavalier microphone for clear and natural sound
  • UHF wireless system with 1680 tunable frequencies for interference-free transmission
  • 42 MHz bandwidth with up to 12 compatible channels per frequency range
  • Automatic frequency scan and squelch control to minimize interference
  • Range of up to 100 meters (328 feet) for flexible, on-location shoots
  • LCD displays on both receiver and transmitter for easy monitoring
  • 8-hour battery life for long-duration production shoots
