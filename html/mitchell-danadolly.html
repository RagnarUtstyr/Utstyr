Mitchell MT Danadolly



Inventory

1 Available



The Mitchell MT Danadolly is a highly versatile camera dolly system designed to provide smooth and stable camera movements for professional film and video productions. Featuring a Mitchell Mount, the Danadolly supports heavy camera rigs and is compatible with a wide range of professional camera heads. This system is widely used in the industry for cinematic tracking shots, smooth panning, and dynamic camera movements.

The Mitchell MT Danadolly is built with durable aluminum and features high-precision wheels designed to run on standard speed rails or custom-made tracks. The dolly can handle a variety of camera setups, from small DSLR rigs to large cinema cameras, making it ideal for use in studio, indoor, and outdoor locations. Its compact design ensures easy portability, and it can be disassembled for quick transportation and storage.

Whether used for tracking shots, curved movements, or straight-line shots, the Mitchell MT Danadolly offers filmmakers and cinematographers a reliable solution for achieving smooth camera motion, enhancing the production quality of any shoot.



Specifications

  • Model: Mitchell MT Danadolly
  • Mount Type: Mitchell Mount
  • Material: High-grade aluminum
  • Track Compatibility: Standard speed rails or custom tracks
  • Weight Capacity: 150 kg (330 lbs)
  • Dimensions: 50 x 50 cm (19.7 x 19.7 inches)
  • Weight: 12 kg (26.5 lbs)


Key Features

  • Mitchell Mount compatible with professional camera heads
  • Smooth and stable camera movements for tracking and panning shots
  • Durable aluminum construction for heavy-duty use
  • High-precision wheels compatible with standard speed rails
  • Supports heavy camera rigs up to 150 kg (330 lbs)
  • Portable and easy to disassemble for transport
