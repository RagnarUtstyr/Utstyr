The Deity SRD-Mini RF Distribution is a compact and efficient RF distribution system designed to streamline wireless microphone and RF receiver setups in professional film production, broadcast, and live performance environments. This device allows users to connect multiple wireless receivers to a single set of antennas, minimizing interference and maximizing signal strength across multiple channels.

The SRD-Mini RF Distribution system supports both UHF and VHF frequency ranges, making it compatible with a wide range of wireless microphones and audio equipment. It ensures clean and stable signal transmission by reducing signal dropouts and RF congestion, providing clear audio capture in complex, high-density RF environments.

The device is powered by an external DC power source, and its compact size allows it to fit seamlessly into portable audio racks or setups. The Deity SRD-Mini RF Distribution is an essential tool for audio professionals who need to manage multiple wireless receivers on set or in live venues, offering reliability and ease of use.
