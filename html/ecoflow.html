EcoFlow Delta 2 Max Portable Power Station



Inventory

1 Available



The EcoFlow Delta 2 Max is a powerful and portable power station designed to keep your devices powered wherever you go. With a massive 2048Wh capacity and 2400W output (up to 4800W surge), the Delta 2 Max is capable of powering almost any device, from phones and laptops to larger appliances like refrigerators and power tools.

This power station features multiple output ports, including AC outlets, USB-A, USB-C, and car ports, providing versatile charging options for all your gear. Whether you’re camping, working remotely, or in need of backup power during an outage, the EcoFlow Delta 2 Max has you covered.

The Delta 2 Max also offers fast recharging capabilities, going from 0% to 80% in just 1 hour using EcoFlow's X-Stream technology, and can be recharged via AC, solar, or a car charger. Compact and portable, it’s the ideal power solution for both indoor and outdoor use.



Specifications

  • Model: EcoFlow Delta 2 Max
  • Battery Capacity: 2048Wh
  • AC Output: 2400W (Surge 4800W)
  • Charging Time: 1 hour to 80% via AC (X-Stream technology)
  • DC Outputs: 2x USB-A, 1x USB-C, 1x Car port
  • AC Outputs: 2x 230V AC outlets
  • Input: AC, Solar, Car Charging
  • Weight: 22.2 kg (48.9 lbs)
  • Dimensions: 42.8 x 27.5 x 31.0 cm (16.8 x 10.8 x 12.2 inches)
  • Battery Type: LiFePO4 (Lithium Iron Phosphate)
  • Cycle Life: 3000 cycles to 80% capacity
