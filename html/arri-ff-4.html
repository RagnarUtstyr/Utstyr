Arri FF-4 Follow Focus



Inventory

1 Available



The Arri FF-4 Follow Focus is a versatile and precise follow focus system, designed for use with a wide range of film and cinema lenses. This industry-standard follow focus is crafted from high-quality materials and provides smooth, reliable focus control for both professional and amateur filmmakers. The FF-4 is known for its modularity and ability to be quickly adapted to different shooting setups.

Built with a robust, modular design, the Arri FF-4 offers single-handed operation and includes interchangeable focus knobs to suit different shooting styles. The follow focus system is compatible with 15mm and 19mm rod systems, making it a flexible addition to any camera rig. The gear ratio provides precise control over focus adjustments, even when using long, fast lenses.

The Arri FF-4 supports both cine-style lenses and photography lenses, making it a versatile choice for filmmakers. It also includes a reversible gear drive, allowing you to switch between lenses that focus in opposite directions. With its reliable construction and durable materials, this follow focus system is designed to withstand the rigors of professional production.



Specifications

  • Model: Arri FF-4
  • Compatibility: 15mm and 19mm rod systems
  • Lens Compatibility: Cine-style and photography lenses
  • Gear Drive: Reversible for left and right focusing lenses
  • Material: Durable aluminum construction
  • Mounting Options: 15mm lightweight support and 19mm studio rod support
  • Adjustable Gear Ratio: Yes
  • Single-Handed Operation: Yes


Key Features

  • Professional-grade follow focus system for cinema and still lenses
  • Compatible with 15mm and 19mm rod systems
  • Reversible gear drive for lenses with opposite focusing directions
  • Single-handed operation for efficient focus pulling
  • Modular design with interchangeable focus knobs
  • Precision control for both short and long lenses
  • Durable, reliable construction for use in professional productions
