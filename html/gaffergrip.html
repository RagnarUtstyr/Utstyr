The Avenger Gaffer Grip is a strong and reliable clamp designed for quick mounting of lights and accessories to pipes, beams, tables, or other surfaces—without the need for stands or rigging.

Equipped with dual baby pins (5/8"), this clamp allows for horizontal or vertical mounting of small lights, flags, or other gear. It’s perfect for tight spaces, car rigs, table-top setups, or overhead mounting on set.

The jaws open wide and feature a textured grip surface for extra security. Its spring-loaded locking mechanism makes it quick to reposition or remove, saving time in fast-paced environments.



Specifications

  • Model:
  • Jaw Opening: 15mm to 80mm (0.59" to 3.14")
  • Mounts: 2x Baby 5/8" pins (one fixed, one removable)
  • Weight Capacity: Up to 10kg (22 lbs)
  • Material: Steel and aluminum
  • Weight: Approx. 1.3 kg (2.9 lbs)


Key Features

  • Quick-mount clamp for lights and accessories
  • Dual 5/8" pins for horizontal or vertical rigging
  • Wide jaw opening with strong grip and spring lock
  • Ideal for tabletop, rigging, or tight-space setups
  • Durable construction for professional use
