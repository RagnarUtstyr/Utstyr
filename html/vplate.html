V-Mount Plate

The V-Mount Plate allows you to securely mount standard V-Lock batteries to your camera rig, monitor setup, or field power solution. Compatible with most V-mount battery brands, it's an essential component for professional rigs requiring flexible and mobile power distribution.

The plate can be mounted directly to cages, cheese plates, or rails using a variety of mounting points. Some models include built-in outputs such as D-Tap, USB, or 2-pin LEMO, offering additional power options for accessories like monitors, wireless transmitters, or lights.

Built for durability in demanding environments, this V-Mount Plate is a key accessory for any professional video setup.



Specifications

  • Mount Type: V-Lock (V-Mount)
  • Compatibility: Standard V-Mount batteries
  • Outputs: May include D-Tap or USB (model-dependent)
  • Mounting Options: 1/4"-20, 3/8"-16, rail clamps or cheese plates
  • Material: Aluminum / ABS composite
  • Weight: Approx. 150–250g


Key Features

  • Securely mounts V-Lock batteries to rigs and accessories
  • Ideal for powering cameras, monitors, wireless systems, and more
  • May include accessory power outputs (D-Tap, USB, etc.)
  • Multiple mounting points for flexible rig integration
  • Compact and rugged design for professional use
