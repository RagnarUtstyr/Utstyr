Infocus HD Projector



Inventory

1 Available



The Infocus IN3138HD is a bright, Full HD projector designed for large venues, screenings, and business presentations. Delivering 1080p resolution and 4500 lumens of brightness, it ensures sharp, high-contrast images even in well-lit environments.

This projector features HDMI, VGA, and composite video inputs for flexible connectivity. With built-in 10W speakers and support for 3D content (via HDMI), it’s a great all-in-one option for portable cinema setups, conferences, or live event playback.

The IN3138HD also includes a powerful zoom lens, vertical keystone correction, and remote control for easy setup and adjustments. Its lightweight design makes it ideal for transport between locations.



Specifications

  • Model: Infocus IN3138HD
  • Resolution: 1920 x 1080 (Full HD 1080p)
  • Brightness: 4500 lumens
  • Contrast Ratio: 20,000:1
  • Inputs: 2x HDMI (1 with MHL), 1x VGA, Composite Video, 3.5mm Audio
  • Audio: Built-in 10W speaker, 3.5mm audio out
  • Throw Ratio: 1.39–2.09:1 (1.3x zoom)
  • 3D Support: Yes (HDMI-based 3D sources)
  • Keystone Correction: Vertical ±40°
  • Weight: 3.1 kg (6.8 lbs)


Key Features

  • Full HD 1080p native resolution for sharp image quality
  • Bright 4500 lumen output — great for daylight environments
  • Multiple input options including HDMI, VGA, and Composite
  • Built-in 10W speaker for simple plug-and-play setup
  • Compatible with 3D sources via HDMI
  • Portable and lightweight for easy transport
