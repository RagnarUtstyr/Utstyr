BUTTERFLY GRID 40° for 6×6 Butterfly Frame



Inventory

1 Available



The 40° Butterfly Grid is an essential accessory for controlling light spill on a 6x6 foot Butterfly frame. Manufactured by DoPchoice, this grid helps narrow the beam angle to 40°, giving photographers and filmmakers precise lighting control. It is ideal for both indoor and outdoor shoots, ensuring that light is focused where it's needed most.

The grid comes with a durable storage bag, making it easy to transport and store. When unfolded, the grid has a dimension of 172 x 172 x 12 cm and weighs 2.4 kg. In its storage bag, it measures 32 x 32 x 15 cm and weighs 2.9 kg.



Specifications

  • Beam Angle: 40°
  • Compatibility: 6x6 foot Butterfly frames
  • Unfolded Dimensions: 172 x 172 x 12 cm
  • Weight (Unfolded): 2.4 kg
  • Dimensions (In Bag): 32 x 32 x 15 cm
  • Weight (In Bag): 2.9 kg
  • Included: Butterfly Grid 40°, Storage Bag
  • Manufacturer: DoPchoice
