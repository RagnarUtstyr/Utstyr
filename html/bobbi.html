Bobbi Net



Inventory

2 Available



The Bobbi Net is a versatile lighting modifier commonly used by cinematographers and photographers to reduce or shape the intensity of a light source. Constructed from a durable mesh material, it softens and subtly cuts down light, helping control hotspots and manage contrast. It can be easily attached to barn doors, frames, or other light-control rigs on set.

Ideal for both studio and on-location shoots, the Bobbi Net offers a quick way to fine-tune lighting setups without having to move or swap out larger accessories. By using various degrees (from full to half net), you can precisely reduce light output to achieve the desired effect. Lightweight and compact, it travels easily and is a staple in most grip departments.



Specifications

  • Type: Single Bobbi Net (Medium Density)
  • Material: Durable nylon mesh
  • Size: Approx. 18" x 24" (varies by model)
  • Light Reduction: Approximately 1 stop
  • Compatibility: Common barn door frames and net holders
  • Weight: ~100 g
  • Usage: Ideal for subtle light control and highlight softening


Scope of Delivery

  • 1x Bobbi Net (Medium Density)
  • 1x Protective sleeve or pouch


Key Features

  • Reduces or shapes light without overly softening it
  • Durable nylon mesh construction
  • Quick and easy attachment to standard fixtures
  • Lightweight and compact, ideal for travel
  • Provides precise control over highlights and contrast
