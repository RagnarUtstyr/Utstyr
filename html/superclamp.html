Manfrotto Superclamp



Inventory

5 Available



The Manfrotto Superclamp is a versatile and essential grip tool for photographers, videographers, and lighting technicians. This strong, durable clamp is designed to securely grip various surfaces, such as pipes, tripods, lighting stands, and flat surfaces. With its easy-to-use locking mechanism, the Superclamp can quickly attach to round or flat objects ranging from 13mm to 55mm in thickness, making it ideal for securing lighting equipment, cameras, flags, and other accessories in studio or location environments.

The Manfrotto Superclamp is compatible with a wide variety of attachments, including spigots, arms, and other accessories, providing maximum flexibility on set. Made from durable materials, it is lightweight yet strong enough to support loads up to 15 kg (33 lbs), making it a reliable and essential tool for any professional or hobbyist looking to mount equipment securely.



Specifications

  • Model: Manfrotto Superclamp 035
  • Type: Universal clamp
  • Clamp Range: 13mm to 55mm
  • Load Capacity: 15 kg (33 lbs)
  • Material: Aluminum and steel
  • Weight: 410g (0.9 lbs)
  • Attachment Options: Compatible with spigots and accessory arms


Key Features

  • Versatile clamp for securing equipment to a variety of surfaces
  • Compatible with pipes, tripods, lighting stands, and flat surfaces
  • Clamps to objects ranging from 13mm to 55mm in thickness
  • Supports heavy loads up to 15 kg (33 lbs)
  • Durable, lightweight aluminum and steel construction
  • Compatible with a wide range of attachments for maximum flexibility
