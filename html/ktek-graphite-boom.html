K-Tek Graphite Boom Pole



Inventory

1 Available



The K-Tek Graphite Boom Pole is a premium lightweight boom pole designed for professional audio recording in film, television, and ENG (electronic news gathering) productions. Constructed from high-quality graphite, this boom pole offers an ideal balance of durability and lightweight performance, making it easier for boom operators to handle extended recording sessions with minimal fatigue.

The boom pole extends from 6 feet (1.83 meters) to 12 feet (3.66 meters), providing ample reach for capturing clean audio, even in wide shots. The smooth telescoping sections lock securely into place with a twist-lock system, ensuring that the boom remains stable during operation, while the internal coiled cable makes it easy to manage audio connections.

Designed with ergonomics and portability in mind, the K-Tek Graphite Boom Pole is the perfect solution for professional sound mixers and boom operators looking for a durable, easy-to-handle tool that can be used in various production environments.



Specifications

  • Model: K-Tek Graphite Boom Pole
  • Material: Graphite
  • Collapsed Length: 6 feet (1.83 meters)
  • Extended Length: 12 feet (3.66 meters)
  • Weight: 0.85 kg (1.9 lbs)
  • Sections: 5 telescoping sections with twist-lock system
  • Cable: Internal coiled XLR cable
  • Connector: Standard XLR


Key Features

  • High-quality graphite construction for durability and lightweight performance
  • Extends from 6 feet (1.83 meters) to 12 feet (3.66 meters) for maximum reach
  • Five smooth telescoping sections with a reliable twist-lock system
  • Internal coiled XLR cable for easy cable management
  • Ergonomic design to reduce fatigue during long shoots
  • Perfect for professional film, television, and ENG productions
