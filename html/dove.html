Dovetail Plate



Inventory

1 Available



The Dovetail Plate is a camera support accessory designed for quick and secure mounting of camera rigs to compatible baseplates or tripod heads. Commonly used with ARRI-standard or 2060-style shoulder mounts and bridge plates, this plate enables smooth forward/backward adjustment for balancing different rigs.

Built from precision-machined aluminum, it provides a rock-solid and play-free connection between camera and support gear. Ideal for professional cine setups, Steadicam rigs, or when working with accessories like follow focus, matte boxes, or heavy lens support.

The plate includes standard mounting slots for 1/4"-20 and 3/8"-16 screws, and is compatible with a wide range of professional camera systems.



Specifications

  • Type: ARRI-standard dovetail plate
  • Material: CNC-machined aluminum
  • Length: Approx. 12" (30 cm)
  • Mounting Slots: 1/4"-20 and 3/8"-16
  • Compatibility: ARRI bridge plates, 15mm and 19mm systems
  • Weight: Approx. 400g


Key Features

  • Compatible with industry-standard dovetail baseplates
  • Ideal for balancing heavy camera rigs and lens setups
  • Secure and smooth slide-in/lock system
  • Supports 15mm and 19mm rod configurations
  • Durable aluminum build for professional use
  • Essential for shoulder rigs, tripods, and Steadicam builds
