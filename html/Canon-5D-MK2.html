Canon 5D Mk2



Inventory

1 Available



The Canon 5D Mark II is a full-frame DSLR camera that revolutionized the world of photography and filmmaking. As the first DSLR capable of shooting Full HD 1080p video, the 5D Mk2 has been widely used by both photographers and videographers. It features a 21.1 MP full-frame CMOS sensor, offering excellent image quality with beautiful colors and detail, making it ideal for both professional photo shoots and video production.

The DIGIC 4 Image Processor allows for fast performance and great handling of high ISO settings, making it suitable for low-light conditions. The camera also boasts an ISO range of 100-6400 (expandable to 25,600), providing flexibility in various lighting environments. Its 9-point autofocus system ensures accurate focus in still photography, while its Full HD video capabilities make it a favorite among independent filmmakers and content creators.

The Canon 5D Mk2 also features a 3.0-inch Clear View LCD with 920,000 dots, providing a clear and detailed view for both shooting and reviewing your images and videos. It supports Live View and manual controls for video recording, offering complete control over exposure, aperture, and focus while filming. With CF card compatibility, the 5D Mk2 allows for ample storage, making it a reliable tool for professional work in any environment.



Specifications

  • Model: Canon EOS 5D Mark II
  • Sensor: 21.1 MP full-frame CMOS sensor
  • Image Processor: DIGIC 4
  • ISO Range: 100-6400 (expandable to 25,600)
  • Autofocus: 9-point AF system
  • Video: Full HD 1080p at 30fps
  • LCD: 3.0-inch Clear View LCD, 920,000 dots
  • Storage: CF card compatibility
  • Battery Life: Up to 850 shots per charge (CIPA standard)
  • Weight: 810g (body only)


Key Features

  • Full-frame 21.1 MP CMOS sensor for high-quality images and videos
  • Full HD 1080p video recording at 30fps
  • ISO range of 100-6400 (expandable to 25,600) for low-light shooting
  • 9-point autofocus system for sharp and accurate focus
  • 3.0-inch Clear View LCD for detailed image review and live view
  • Live View mode with manual controls for video recording

  • Durable body and weather sealing for tough shooting conditions
