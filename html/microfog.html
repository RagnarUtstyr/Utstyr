The MicroFogger 3 Lite by Vosentech is a compact, handheld fog machine capable of producing dense fog bursts in a matter of seconds. Designed for portability and ease of use, it’s perfect for small-scale practical effects, tabletop shoots, product videos, or on-location fog needs.

The device operates with a simple button press and uses replaceable e-liquid cartridges for clean, controlled fog output. Its rechargeable internal battery provides several hours of use on a full charge, depending on intensity and frequency of activation.

The MicroFogger 3 Lite is a lighter, simplified version of its big brother, yet it still delivers impressive results with minimal setup. Perfect for DPs, SFX artists, and content creators needing mobility and speed.
