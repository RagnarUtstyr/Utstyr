The K&F Concept PL-NEX Adapter allows you to mount professional PL-mount cinema lenses onto Sony E-mount (NEX) cameras. This adapter is ideal for filmmakers who want to use high-end PL glass on Sony mirrorless or cinema bodies such as the Sony FX series or Alpha line.

Constructed from precision-machined aluminum alloy and stainless steel, the adapter ensures a secure, tight fit with no wobble. It maintains accurate flange distance to preserve infinity focus and proper optical performance.

The adapter is fully mechanical with no electronic contacts, meaning aperture and focus are controlled manually on the lens itself. Its solid locking mechanism is designed to safely support heavier cinema lenses when properly supported with rods or lens support systems.
