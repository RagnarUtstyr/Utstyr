Tilta Nucleus-M2 Wireless Follow Focus

The Tilta Nucleus-M2 Wireless Follow Focus is an advanced follow focus system designed to offer precise and reliable focus control for cinematographers and camera operators. With wireless capabilities and a robust design, the Nucleus-M2 is ideal for multi-camera setups, gimbal rigs, and handheld shoots. The system is capable of controlling focus, iris, and zoom remotely, making it an essential tool for professional filmmakers and videographers.

The Nucleus-M2 utilizes high-torque brushless motors for smooth, accurate adjustments, with a wireless range of up to 1,000 feet (300 meters). It supports dual-channel control, allowing simultaneous control of two motors, which is perfect for multi-lens setups or controlling both focus and zoom on the same rig.

The wireless hand unit is powered by rechargeable 18650 batteries, offering long hours of operation. The system is designed to be compatible with a wide variety of cinema cameras, including DSLR, mirrorless, and high-end cinema cameras. The Nucleus-M2 also includes wireless handgrips for added flexibility and ergonomics, providing an ideal solution for focus pullers working on dynamic shots or handheld rigs.
