The **Tilta Nucleus-M2** is a next-gen wireless follow focus system that offers precise control over focus, iris, and zoom for professional cinematographers and camera assistants. With enhanced performance and features compared to its predecessor, the Nucleus-M2 provides seamless integration with various DSLR, mirrorless, and cinema cameras.

The **Ultimate Kit** includes everything needed to operate the system in complex shooting scenarios. The kit features the handwheel unit, two wireless handgrips, two high-torque motors, a zoom module, and all essential cables and accessories.

The Nucleus-M2 offers a range of up to 1,000 feet (300 meters) with dual-channel support, allowing simultaneous control of focus, iris, and zoom. The system uses NP-F550 batteries for the hand unit and grips, providing extended battery life. The kit also includes motor mounts for both 15mm and 19mm rod systems, offering maximum flexibility for your camera rig setup.
