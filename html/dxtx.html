The Deity DXTX Plug-On Transmitter is a professional-grade XLR transmitter designed for broadcast, film, and location audio. It features a locking Neutrik XLR input supporting condensers (48 V phantom) and electret mics, plus simultaneous 32-bit float recording to a microSD card while wirelessly transmitting. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

This unit operates across a wide UHF range (550 MHz – 960 MHz) with selectable RF power up to 100 mW for global use, offers dual AA battery operation for up to 14 hours of runtime, USB-C external power, and integrates remote control via Deity’s BitConnectify™ 2.0 Bluetooth system. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}



Specifications

  • Model: Deity DXTX Plug-On
  • Frequency Range: 550 MHz – 960 MHz (region dependent)
  • Max RF Output: Up to 100 mW software selectable
  • Recording Format: 32-bit float / 24-bit to microSD (≤ 128 GB)
  • Input Connector: Neutrik XLR-3 locking (48 V phantom, 5 V bias)
  • Battery Type: Dual AA batteries (up to ~14 hours)
  • External Power: USB-C input for power or firmware updates
  • Dimensions (HxWxD): ~119 × 44.8 × 42 mm
  • Weight: ~190 g (without battery & antenna)


Key Features

  • Simultaneous 32-bit float recording and wireless transmission
  • Wide UHF band with selectable RF power up to 100 mW

  • Up to ~14 hours runtime with dual AA batteries plus USB-C external power
