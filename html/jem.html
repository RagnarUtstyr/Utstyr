JEM ZR25 Smoke Machine



Inventory

1 Available



The JEM ZR25 is a compact, high-output smoke machine built for professional stage, studio, and on-location use. It delivers a powerful and consistent smoke output while remaining portable and easy to operate, making it ideal for both live productions and film environments.

Featuring fast heat-up times, intelligent temperature control, and robust build quality, the ZR25 is engineered for reliability under demanding conditions. It supports both manual and DMX operation and includes a detachable remote for flexible control options.

Designed to work with JEM Pro-Fog fluid, it produces dense, atmospheric smoke with long hang time, perfect for enhancing lighting effects or visual depth in a scene. A durable metal housing and internal fluid tank make it rugged and road-ready.



Specifications

  • Model: JEM ZR25
  • Control: DMX 512, manual, onboard timer, and remote control
  • Fluid Type: JEM Pro-Fog Fluid
  • Warm-up Time: Approx. 7 minutes
  • Output: 2000 m³/min
  • Tank Capacity: 2.5 L
  • Power Consumption: 1000W
  • Weight: 10.7 kg
  • Dimensions: 498 x 200 x 290 mm


Key Features

  • High-output smoke for stage, set, or studio use
  • Fast warm-up and ready-to-use in minutes
  • Supports DMX, manual, and remote control
  • Compact and portable with metal housing
  • Integrated fluid tank with 2.5L capacity
  • Produces thick, long-lasting atmospheric smoke
  • Compatible with JEM Pro-Fog fluid
