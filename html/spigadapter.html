This Junior 1-1/8" Stud to Baby 5/8" Stud adapter is a must-have piece of grip equipment for converting larger Junior receivers or stands to fit standard Baby-sized accessories. Ideal for mounting smaller lights, flags, or other gear onto stands or clamps that primarily accommodate 1-1/8" studs.

Engineered from durable steel with a protective finish, the adapter provides a secure, wobble-free connection. Simply slide the Junior stud end into your stand or receiver, and attach your Baby accessory onto the 5/8" top — no need to swap stands or compromise on stability.

From on-location shoots to studio setups, this versatile adapter ensures you can mount a wide range of equipment with a single stand or clamp, streamlining your workflow and saving valuable time.
