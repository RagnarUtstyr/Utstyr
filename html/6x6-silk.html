White China Silk 6x6



Inventory

1 Available



The White China Silk 6x6 is a diffusion fabric that is especially effective at softening light without significantly reducing its intensity. With only about 1 stop of light loss, it provides excellent diffusion, making it ideal for positioning between lights and talent during filming or photography sessions.

This fabric is designed to fit the Matthews Butterfly Frame 6x6 (180x180 cm), which is rented separately. It is perfect for outdoor shoots or studio setups where controlling the light's harshness is crucial.



Specifications

  • Material: China Silk
  • Dimensions: 180 cm x 180 cm (6x6 feet)
  • Light Loss: Approximately 1 stop
  • Compatibility: Fits 6x6 Matthews Butterfly Frame (rented separately)
  • Purpose: Diffusion fabric for softening light
