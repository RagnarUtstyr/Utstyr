CineEye 2S Wireless Video Transmitter



Inventory

1 Available



The CineEye 2S is a versatile wireless video transmitter with both SDI and HDMI inputs, making it compatible with a wide range of cameras. It boasts ultra-low latency of less than 60ms and can transmit video signals up to 500 feet (150 meters), ensuring a stable connection even in challenging environments. Whether you're shooting on open terrain or in more complex settings, CineEye 2S delivers smooth video and audio transmission.

Supporting 1080p/60fps video input and 44.1kHz/48kHz 16bit/24bit audio, CineEye 2S offers high-resolution transmission with low compression, providing a near-lossless monitoring experience. The device can hook up to four screens simultaneously, making it perfect for multi-person monitoring on iOS and Android devices. Its fanless design ensures quiet operation with low power consumption, making it an ideal tool for professional shoots.

Equipped with a 1.3-inch OLED display, the CineEye 2S shows key information such as input signal, battery level, connection status, and WiFi channel. It also features a foldable antenna for easy setup and portability. The unit can be powered by either Sony NP batteries or through a 7.4V-16.8V DC input, giving you flexible power options.

  • Video Input: SDI (1080p/60fps), HDMI (1080p/60fps)
  • Audio Input: 44.1kHz/48kHz 16bit/24bit PCM
  • Latency: <0.06 seconds
  • Transmission Range: 500 feet (150 meters)
