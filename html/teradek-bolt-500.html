Teradek Bolt 500 Wireless Video Transmitter



Inventory

3 Available



The Teradek Bolt 500 is a professional zero-delay wireless video transmission system designed for film production, broadcast, and live events. With the ability to transmit uncompressed 1080p video at distances up to 500 feet, the Bolt 500 offers reliable performance and low-latency streaming, ensuring that camera operators, directors, and crew can monitor video in real-time with no perceptible lag.

The Bolt 500 supports HD-SDI and HDMI inputs/outputs, making it compatible with a wide range of professional cameras, monitors, and recording devices. The system operates over the 5GHz frequency band and features AES-128 encryption for secure video transmission. It can pair with up to four receivers, allowing multiple people on set to view the transmitted signal on different devices.

The Teradek Bolt 500 is compact and lightweight, making it perfect for gimbal rigs, drone use, and other applications where size and weight are critical. Its robust design ensures durability in both indoor and outdoor environments. The unit is powered by DC or battery options, providing flexibility for use in a variety of settings.



Specifications

  • Model: Teradek Bolt 500
  • Range: Up to 500 feet (150 meters)
  • Resolution: Up to 1080p60
  • Inputs/Outputs: HD-SDI, HDMI
  • Frequency: 5GHz
  • Encryption: AES-128
  • Latency: < 1ms (zero delay)
  • Power Options: DC power or V-mount/Gold mount battery plates
  • Dimensions (Transmitter): 98mm x 61mm x 23mm
  • Weight (Transmitter): 190g (6.7 oz)


Key Features

  • Zero-delay wireless video transmission with a range of up to 500 feet
  • Supports 1080p60 uncompressed video transmission
  • HD-SDI and HDMI inputs/outputs for wide compatibility
  • Supports up to four receivers for multi-viewing on set
  • AES-128 encryption for secure video transmission
  • Compact and lightweight design ideal for gimbals and drones
  • Powered via DC or optional battery plates for flexibility on location
