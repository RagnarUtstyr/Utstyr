This 1/4"-20 Thread Eyebolt provides a quick and reliable solution for securing cameras, accessories, or rigging components that require a standard 1/4"-20 mount. It's commonly used in grip and rigging situations—everything from hanging small lights or cable organizers to safely tethering camera setups.

Constructed from sturdy steel with a smooth finish, this eyebolt ensures reliable fastening without damaging your gear. The open circular head allows for easy attachment of carabiners, safety tethers, or rigging ropes. Whether you're stabilizing equipment in the studio or looking for a secure way to mount accessories on location, this eyebolt is a versatile choice.

With a universal 1/4"-20 thread, it’s compatible with most camera cages, cheese plates, and other mounting points, ensuring you can integrate it seamlessly into a wide range of filmmaking setups.
