Tilta Nucleus-M Wireless Follow Focus



Inventory

1 Available



The Tilta Nucleus-M is a professional-grade wireless follow focus system designed for cinematographers and camera assistants working in film and video production. The Nucleus-M allows for precise control over a camera’s focus, iris, and zoom from a distance of up to 1,000 feet (300 meters), making it an ideal solution for complex and fast-paced shoots. Its dual-channel system enables simultaneous control of two motors, making it suitable for multi-lens setups.

The Nucleus-M is compatible with most DSLR, mirrorless, and cinema cameras, and it works with lenses that have either a mechanical or electronic focus. The wireless hand unit provides full control over focus, iris, and zoom, while two wireless handgrips can be mounted onto a rig or gimbal, offering a flexible and ergonomic setup for camera operators and focus pullers.

With its brushless motors, the Nucleus-M ensures smooth and accurate adjustments, while its strong wireless signal ensures reliable performance, even in challenging environments. The hand unit and grips are powered by 18650 rechargeable batteries, providing extended operation times. The system also supports power and communication through D-Tap cables.



Specifications

  • Model: Tilta Nucleus-M
  • Range: Up to 1,000 feet (300 meters)
  • Channels: Dual-channel wireless control (focus, iris, zoom)
  • Compatibility: DSLR, mirrorless, and cinema cameras
  • Power Source: 18650 rechargeable batteries (for hand unit and grips)
  • Motors: High-torque brushless motors
  • Signal Strength: Strong wireless signal with minimal latency
  • Weight (Hand Unit): 410g (14.46 oz)
  • Weight (Hand Grips): 290g each (10.23 oz)


Key Features

  • Wireless control of focus, iris, and zoom for up to 1,000 feet (300 meters)
  • Dual-channel control for use with multi-lens setups
  • Compatible with DSLR, mirrorless, and cinema cameras
  • Includes a wireless hand unit and two wireless handgrips
  • High-torque brushless motors for smooth and precise adjustments
  • Powered by 18650 rechargeable batteries for extended use
  • Reliable performance with minimal signal interference
