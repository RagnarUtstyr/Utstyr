The Sekonic L-858D Speedmaster is a professional light meter designed for both still photography and cinematography. Offering exceptionally accurate metering for ambient and flash lighting, this cutting-edge device can measure high-speed sync (HSS) flash and even flash duration up to 1/40,000 second, ensuring precise exposure control in complex lighting scenarios.

With a wide measurement range and advanced features like incident and reflected metering, the L-858D Speedmaster is ideal for studio, on-location shoots, and film sets. Its large 2.7-inch color touchscreen makes navigation straightforward, while optional wireless modules allow remote triggering of various popular flash systems.

The unit is powered by two AA batteries, offering an impressive battery life to keep you shooting longer. Its ergonomic design ensures comfortable handling, making the Sekonic L-858D a go-to solution for professionals seeking consistent and reliable metering capabilities in any lighting environment.



Specifications

  • Model: Sekonic L-858D Speedmaster
  • Metering Modes: Incident, Reflected, Flash, HSS
  • Measurement Range (Ambient): EV -5 to EV 22.9 (ISO 100)
  • Shutter Speed Range (Ambient): 30 min to 1/16,000 sec
