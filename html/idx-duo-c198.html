IDX DUO-C 198 V-Mount Battery



Inventory

8 Available



The IDX DUO-C 198 is a high-capacity V-Mount lithium-ion battery designed to provide reliable power for a wide range of cinema cameras, lights, and accessories. With a 198Wh capacity, it is a versatile power solution for production setups requiring longer run times without frequent battery changes. It is compatible with many high-draw devices including ARRI, RED, and Sony cameras, making it ideal for professional productions.

The DUO-C 198 features two D-Tap outputs for powering accessories like monitors, wireless transmitters, or lights, and also includes a 5V USB port to charge smaller devices such as smartphones and tablets. Its compact form factor and lightweight design make it easy to transport and use on location, while still delivering the power needed for intensive shoots.

This battery also includes a 5-LED battery level indicator, allowing you to quickly check the remaining charge on set. It is compatible with IDX’s V-Mount chargers for efficient charging and can be used in combination with other IDX batteries for extended shooting times.



Specifications

  • Model: IDX DUO-C 198
  • Battery Type: Lithium-ion
  • Capacity: 198Wh (14.4V, 13.8Ah)
  • Max Load: 14A/168W
  • Outputs: 2x D-Tap (unregulated 14.4V), 1x USB (5V, 2.3A)
  • Mount Type: V-Mount
  • Charging Time: Approximately 4 hours with IDX VL-2X charger
  • Weight: 1.1 kg (2.42 lbs)
  • Dimensions: 9.7 x 14.6 x 6.2 cm (3.8 x 5.7 x 2.4 inches)


Key Features

  • 198Wh capacity for longer shooting times
  • V-mount compatible with a wide range of cameras and lights
  • Two D-Tap outputs for powering accessories
  • USB port for charging small devices
  • Compact and lightweight design for portability
  • 5-LED battery level indicator for real-time status updates
  • Fast charging with IDX V-mount chargers
