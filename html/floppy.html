Floppy Flag 4x8



Inventory

4 Available



The Floppy Flag 4×8 is a professional-grade light control tool designed to help cinematographers and photographers precisely shape and block light on set. Its design allows you to use it at either 4×4 feet or “flop” open to its full 4×8 feet, providing maximum versatility in various shooting conditions.

Constructed with durable, high-quality black fabric, this floppy flag is ideal for blocking stray light, creating negative fill, or adding dramatic shadows. It is an essential piece of equipment for grip departments, allowing filmmakers to easily control spill and highlight just the areas they want lit.

The simple “flop-over” extension makes setup quick and efficient, while its sturdy metal frame ensures reliability throughout long shoot days. Whether you’re working in a professional studio or on location, the Floppy Flag 4×8 gives you the flexibility and coverage required to shape your lighting just right.

Below is a breakdown of how the Floppy Flag 4×8 can be utilized on set:

  • Standard 4×4 Mode: Use the flag folded up for tighter spaces or when you only need moderate light blocking or negative fill.
  • Extended 4×8 Mode: Flop the additional panel open for larger sets or when you need to cover a bigger area and block more light.
  • Versatile Positioning: Adjust the flag’s angle to shape light exactly where you need it, reducing unwanted spill or focusing on specific subjects.


Specifications

  • Dimensions: 4×4 ft (folded), 4×8 ft (extended)
  • Fabric Material: Heavy-duty black fabric for maximum light blocking
  • Frame Construction: Sturdy metal frame with “flop-over” extension mechanism
  • Approximate Weight: 4.5 kg


Key Features

  • Quickly switches between 4×4 and 4×8 coverage
  • Constructed from durable, light-blocking black fabric
  • Sturdy metal frame ensures stability in diverse shooting conditions
  • Ideal for both studio and on-location projects
  • Easy to transport and store thanks to foldable design
