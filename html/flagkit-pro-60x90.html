Flagkit Pro 60x90 cm



Inventory

2 Available



The Flagkit Pro 60x90 cm is an essential lighting control tool designed for professional film and photography setups. Flags, or cutters, are used to block, shape, and modify light, helping you create precise shadows and control spill from your lighting sources. The 60x90 cm size offers a large surface area for controlling light on set, making it ideal for both small and large productions.

This flag kit includes a durable aluminum frame and black fabric, which absorbs light and prevents reflections, ensuring accurate light control. The lightweight frame makes it easy to handle and mount on C-stands or other grip equipment, while the fabric is tensioned to prevent sagging or wrinkles, providing consistent performance on set.

The Flagkit Pro is perfect for creating defined shadows, cutting unwanted light from a subject, or flagging off areas of the set for more controlled lighting. Whether you're working in a studio or outdoor location, this flag kit offers reliable, professional-grade light shaping.



Specifications

  • Model: Flagkit Pro 60x90 cm
  • Frame Material: Aluminum
  • Fabric Material: Black, light-absorbing fabric
  • Dimensions: 60x90 cm (23.6 x 35.4 inches)
  • Weight: 1.5 kg (3.3 lbs)
  • Mounting: Compatible with C-stands and grip arms


Key Features

  • Light-blocking and shaping tool for precise lighting control
  • Durable aluminum frame with tensioned black fabric
  • 60x90 cm surface area, ideal for professional lighting setups
  • Lightweight and easy to mount on C-stands or grip equipment
  • Perfect for creating defined shadows and controlling light spill
