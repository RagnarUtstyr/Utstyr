Flagkit Pro 6090



Inventory

2 Available



The Flagkit Pro 6090 is a professional foldable and versatile scrim flag kit, designed for cinematographers and photographers who need precise control over their lighting. It features three-sided collapsible steel frames, which can be folded down to fit into a small carry case for easy transportation and hassle-free storage.

This kit includes 5 different types of fabric, allowing you to block, diffuse, or reduce the light hitting your subject, providing access to a comprehensive light control system. The open-ended frame ensures that no shadow is cast when used with the diffusion silks or net screens.

The SF6090 Professional Foldable & Versatile Flag Kit also comes with 2 small circular flags (Dot Flags) and 2 small rectangular flag panels (Finger Flags), ideal for fine-tuning the lighting on smaller subjects. The kit is perfect for professionals looking for a quick and easy solution for shaping, blocking, or diffusing the light.

Below is a breakdown of the different fabric covers included and their effects:

  • Black Single Net & Black Double Net Screens: Reduces the amount of light falling onto your subject without reducing specularity. Ideal for situations where you need to reduce light on one subject without affecting others.
  • 1-Stop & 2-Stop Silk Screens: Produces moderate diffusion. The 1-stop silk reduces contrast and specularity and opens up shadow areas. The 2-stop silk provides greater diffusion and further reduces contrast and specularity.
  • Solid Black Fabric: Blocks light completely or can be used to cast shadows for added mood and effect.

The Flagkit Pro 6090 follows the industry-standard color-coding scheme, enabling you to quickly select the correct flag on set:

  • Solid Black Fabric: Black border
  • Double-Net Screen: Red border
  • Single-Net Screen: Green border
  • Two-Stop Silk Screen: Yellow border
  • One-Stop Silk Screen: Grey border


Specifications

  • Flag Dimensions: 60 x 90 cm
  • Finger-Flag Dimensions: 10 x 35.5 cm
  • Dot Flag Diameter: 15 cm
  • Box Weight: 9.48 kg


Key Features

  • Foldable and versatile scrim flag kit for precise light control
  • Includes 5 different types of fabric for blocking, diffusing, or reducing light
  • Comes with additional dot and finger flags for smaller subjects
  • Open-ended frame to avoid casting shadows with diffusion silks or net screens
  • Easy to transport and store with foldable frames and small carry case
  • Industry-standard color coding for easy identification on set
