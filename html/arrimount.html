The Monitor Mount for Arri WCU-4 is a highly functional accessory designed to mount monitors securely to your camera rig, offering a stable solution for monitoring footage during production. Tailored specifically for use with the Arri WCU-4 wireless camera control system, this mount offers precision alignment and robust stability, ensuring a steady monitor setup during both handheld and tripod-based shooting scenarios.

Featuring a flexible design, the mount allows for easy attachment to 15mm or 19mm rod systems, giving you the versatility to integrate it into any professional camera rig. The mounting system is durable yet lightweight, ensuring it does not add unnecessary bulk while maintaining the monitor's secure position. Ideal for location shoots or studio setups, this monitor mount keeps your equipment stable and safe during extended filming sessions.

Its adjustable tilt and rotation features provide greater flexibility for achieving the perfect viewing angle, ensuring operators can have the best possible visibility of their shots, whether standing or sitting behind the monitor. Whether you're operating the Arri WCU-4 or other wireless control systems, this mount offers smooth operation and seamless integration with your setup.
