SmallHD 702 Bright On-Camera Monitor



Inventory

1 Available



The SmallHD 702 Bright On-Camera Monitor is an incredibly bright monitor with a 1000 cd/m² brightness and sharp Full HD 1080p display. Its 1000:1 contrast ratio and optically bonded glass with an anti-reflective coating provide superior daylight visibility and image clarity, even in challenging lighting conditions. The 7" screen size offers ample viewing space, making it ideal for on-set monitoring.

Housed in a durable aircraft-grade aluminum unibody, the 702 Bright also features a slim, modern design. The innovative dual battery plates support both Sony L-Series and Canon LP-E6 batteries on the same plate, giving users flexible power options. The monitor is packed with professional features, including HD-SDI and HDMI inputs/outputs, a headphone jack for audio monitoring, and a host of monitoring tools such as Waveform, Custom LUT support, and anamorphic de-squeeze. Powered by SmallHD's Page Builder OS, the 702 Bright allows users to create up to 12 customizable pages for quick access to frequently used features, improving on-set productivity.



Specifications

  • Brightness: 1000 cd/m²
  • Display Resolution: Full HD 1080p
  • Contrast Ratio: 1000:1
  • Viewing Angle: 179°
  • Material: Aircraft-grade aluminum unibody
  • Battery Support: Dual plates for Sony L-Series and Canon LP-E6
  • Inputs/Outputs: HD-SDI and HDMI
  • OS: SmallHD Page Builder OS with up to 12 customizable pages
  • Weight: Slim and lightweight design


Monitoring Features

  • Pixel Zoom: 2x and 4x with smooth pan via joystick button
  • Frame Guides
  • DSLR Scale (Canon, Nikon)
  • Custom Scale
  • Anamorphic De-Squeeze
  • Image Rotate (Manual/Automatic)
  • Image Flip (Manual/Automatic)
  • Cross Conversion
  • Focus Assist (Colors)
  • Peaking
  • False Color
  • Zebra (Multiple)
  • Waveform
  • RGB Parade
  • Histogram
  • Horizon Indicator
  • Image Capture (Individual and Time-Lapse)
  • Custom LUTS and LUT Import
  • LUT Downstream
  • Image Overlay
  • Audio Meters
