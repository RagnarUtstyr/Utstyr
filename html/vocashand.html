Vocas Leather Hand Grip Kit



Inventory

1 Available



The Vocas Leather Hand Grip Kit is designed to provide a secure, ergonomic grip for a variety of camera rigs. Built with high-quality leather and sturdy metal rosettes, this kit offers maximum comfort and control for extended handheld shooting. Suitable for use with DSLR and cinema cameras, it helps operators maintain steady shots and reduces fatigue during long filming sessions.

Each grip features an adjustable angle for better handling and can be easily mounted to standard ARRI-style rosettes, ensuring wide compatibility. The durable leather covering offers both a professional look and improved grip, while the metal core guarantees a solid, stable connection for confidence under rigorous shooting conditions.

Compact and lightweight, the Vocas Leather Hand Grip Kit is well-suited for documentary, commercial, and cinematic productions alike—ensuring every take is comfortable, steady, and well-supported.



Specifications

  • Model: Vocas Leather Hand Grip Kit
  • Mounting: ARRI-style rosettes
  • Materials: Leather grip with metal core
  • Adjustability: Grip angle can be changed via rosette
  • Kit Components: Pair of leather handgrips (left and right), rosette arms (if included)
  • Weight (approx.): 0.5 kg (1.1 lbs)


Key Features

  • Ergonomic leather grips for comfortable handheld operation
  • ARRI-style rosettes for secure, universal mounting
  • Adjustable grip angle for personalized comfort and control
  • Durable metal construction for long-term reliability
  • Lightweight design suited for extended shoots
