15mm Rod Clamp



Inventory

1 Available



The 15mm Rod Clamp is a compact, essential mounting accessory used to securely attach components to a 15mm rod system. Whether you're mounting a monitor arm, battery plate, EVF bracket, or wireless system, this clamp provides a strong and stable connection point.

Built from precision-machined aluminum, it fits seamlessly onto standard 15mm rods and tightens easily with a single locking screw or lever. Some versions include mounting threads (1/4"-20 or 3/8"-16) or cold shoe mounts for increased versatility.

Perfect for modular camera builds, the 15mm Rod Clamp is a must-have in any camera assistant’s toolkit, allowing flexible rigging in fast-paced environments.



Specifications

  • Rod Size: 15mm
  • Compatibility: Standard 15mm LWS rod systems
  • Material: Aluminum alloy
  • Mounting Options: May include 1/4"-20, 3/8"-16 threads or cold shoe (varies)
  • Locking Mechanism: Thumbscrew or quick-release lever
  • Weight: Approx. 80g


Key Features

  • Attaches securely to any 15mm rod
  • Great for mounting accessories like arms, plates, and brackets
  • Lightweight and compact for easy rig integration
  • Durable aluminum construction
  • Tool-less tightening with thumb lever or screw
