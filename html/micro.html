The DaVinci Resolve Micro Panel is a professional-grade hardware control surface designed to dramatically speed up color correction workflows in DaVinci Resolve. It provides tactile control over primary color grading tools, allowing editors and colorists to make faster and more precise adjustments compared to mouse and keyboard alone.

Built from aircraft-grade aluminum, the panel features three high-resolution weighted trackballs and 12 precision-machined control knobs for real-time access to Lift, Gamma, Gain, Contrast, Saturation, and more. It also includes dedicated buttons for playback, switching views, node navigation, and timeline control.

Compact and USB-C powered, the Micro Panel is ideal for on-set dailies, mobile grading suites, or small post-production studios seeking hands-on efficiency in color grading.
