Applebox Family (Full, Half, Quarter, and Pancake)



Inventory

8 Available (Family Set)



The Applebox Family set includes Full, Half, Quarter, and Pancake apple boxes, which are indispensable tools on any film, television, or photography set. These versatile wooden boxes are used to adjust the height of equipment, props, or talent, and can serve as supports, step stools, temporary seats, or even work platforms.

Each apple box in the family set is constructed from durable plywood, ensuring long-lasting performance even in tough production environments. The boxes are lightweight and easy to stack, making them convenient to transport and store on set. Their various sizes allow for precise height adjustments, whether you’re raising a light, positioning a camera, or creating a steady platform for talent to stand on.

The Applebox Family is a staple in any grip department, offering unmatched versatility. Whether you need to level out uneven ground, raise gear to eye level, or simply provide a quick seat, these boxes are built to support your needs in any situation.
