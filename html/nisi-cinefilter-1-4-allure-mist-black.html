NiSi 1/4 Allure Mist Filter



Inventory

1 Available



The NiSi 1/4 Allure Mist Filter is a cinematic diffusion filter designed to create a soft and ethereal effect by reducing contrast and diffusing highlights. It is commonly used in film and video production to achieve a more dream-like or romantic look while retaining the sharpness of the subject.

The 1/4 strength mist effect of this filter is perfect for softening wrinkles, blemishes, and harsh light while maintaining natural skin tones, making it ideal for portraits, close-ups, and cinematic scenes. It also helps to reduce the sharpness of digital images, giving them a more organic, filmic feel.

Constructed with high-quality optical glass, the NiSi Allure Mist filter ensures minimal loss of image quality. It is designed to fit into 4x5.65" matte boxes, making it compatible with a wide range of professional camera rigs and lenses.



Specifications

  • Model: NiSi 1/4 Allure Mist Filter
  • Filter Type: Diffusion filter
  • Effect Strength: 1/4 strength mist effect
  • Size: 4x5.65 inches
  • Material: High-quality optical glass
  • Compatibility: Fits 4x5.65" matte boxes
  • Weight: 0.2 kg (0.44 lbs)


Key Features

  • Ideal for creating a cinematic, dream-like look in film and video production
  • Maintains natural skin tones while softening imperfections
  • High-quality optical glass construction for minimal image quality loss
  • Compatible with 4x5.65" matte boxes
