77mm Soft 2 Filter

The 77mm Soft 2 Filter is a diffusion filter designed for photographers and filmmakers who want a stronger softening effect than the Soft 1 filter. This filter helps reduce contrast, softens highlights, and creates a dreamy, cinematic look in portraits and other scenes with high contrast lighting. The stronger diffusion effect makes it perfect for use in environments where you want to add an even more ethereal and artistic look.

The Soft 2 filter provides a stronger diffusion effect than its 1/4 counterpart, making it ideal for high-contrast lighting conditions, such as outdoor shoots or harsh artificial lighting. It creates a glowing, soft look while still preserving image clarity and detail, especially in portraits and close-up shots.

Crafted from high-quality optical glass, the 77mm Soft 2 Filter ensures no noticeable loss of sharpness or clarity, even with its diffusion effect. It fits lenses with a 77mm filter thread, making it a compatible and convenient addition to your camera gear for both photography and videography.
