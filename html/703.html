SmallHD 703 UltraBright On-Camera Monitor



Inventory

1 Available



The SmallHD 703 UltraBright On-Camera Monitor solves the challenge of outdoor monitoring with its 2200 cd/m² brightness, making it ideal for field use in bright sunlight. Its 1920 x 1080 resolution on a 7-inch screen ensures sharp image analysis and focus pulling. The monitor’s ability to preview HDR content adds further versatility, offering an extended dynamic range to better represent brightness levels in your footage.

Encased in a unibody aluminum exterior, the monitor is durable and lightweight. It features Gorilla Glass with optical bonding, reducing reflections and ensuring high contrast in direct sunlight. The dual SDI and HDMI inputs and outputs offer flexible connectivity, allowing for A/B comparison of different SDI signals or passing the signal downstream.

The PageBuilder OS allows users to organize monitoring tools (such as waveform, LUTs, and focus aids) into custom pages, accessible via a joystick. ColorFlow 3D LUT processing ensures consistent color from production to post-production, allowing for side-by-side comparisons of treated and untreated images. Additional features like HD scopes, focus assist, and frame guides make the 703 UltraBright a versatile tool for any production.
