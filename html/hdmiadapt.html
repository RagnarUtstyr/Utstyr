90° HDMI Adapter

This 90° HDMI adapter is the perfect solution for fitting HDMI cables into tight spaces, such as behind monitors, wall-mounted TVs, or small camera rigs. Its angled design relieves strain on HDMI ports and cables, helping reduce cable wear and potential damage.

Ideal for professional video setups and everyday home use alike, this adapter is designed to provide a secure, high-quality connection without sacrificing signal integrity. Simply connect the male end of the adapter to your device’s HDMI port, then plug your HDMI cable into the female side.

Built with durable materials and a compact form factor, it ensures a stable fit that won’t interfere with adjacent ports or mounting hardware. Whether you’re tidying up your cable management or accommodating a cramped setup, this 90° HDMI adapter can make all the difference.
