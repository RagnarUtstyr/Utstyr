Gaffers Control



Inventory

1 Available



The Gaffers Control system is a comprehensive lighting control interface designed for professional lighting technicians on set. It allows real-time control and fine adjustment of multiple light fixtures, enabling the gaffer to quickly make lighting adjustments from one central location. The system is compatible with a wide variety of DMX-controlled lights, including LED panels, HMI lights, and tungsten fixtures, making it a versatile solution for any type of production.

With an intuitive touch interface and wireless connectivity, the Gaffers Control gives you the flexibility to manage complex lighting setups without needing to walk back and forth between fixtures. The control unit features an easy-to-read display that shows real-time lighting parameters, including intensity, color temperature, and effects.

The system is built with rugged materials to withstand the rigors of on-set use and can be mounted on C-stands or carried around with its lightweight design. Gaffers Control allows for fast changes in lighting setups, making it ideal for productions where time is crucial, and multiple light sources need to be adjusted simultaneously.



Specifications

  • Model: Gaffers Control
  • Compatibility: DMX-controlled lights, including LED, HMI, and tungsten
  • Interface: Intuitive touch-screen control
  • Connectivity: Wireless (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth)
  • Mounting Options: C-stand compatible, portable design
  • Power Supply: Rechargeable battery, USB-C charging
  • Dimensions: 25 x 18 x 5 cm (9.8 x 7 x 2 inches)
  • Weight: 1 kg (2.2 lbs)

  • Compatible with a wide range of LED, HMI, and tungsten lights

  • Wireless connectivity for remote control of lighting setups
