This Elastic Safety Rope With Quickrelease Buckle is a must-have for camera operators seeking additional support and security on set. Designed with double-ended frog buckles and a flexible elastic core, it’s perfect for stabilizing your camera rig, tethering accessories, or providing a backup safety line when working at height.

The quick-release design allows you to attach or detach gear in seconds, while the heavy-duty elastic webbing accommodates movement without putting unnecessary strain on your equipment. Ideal for dynamic shooting scenarios where safety and rapid adjustments are paramount.

Lightweight yet robust, this sling easily packs into a grip bag or camera case. Whether you’re filming action sequences, running a handheld gimbal, or working on a busy set, this rope helps ensure your camera and accessories stay secure throughout the production.
