Cardellini End Jaw Clamp



Inventory

4 Available



The Cardellini End Jaw Clamp is a highly versatile, heavy-duty clamp designed for gripping and supporting various objects in film, television, and photography production. It features an adjustable jaw that opens from 0 to 2 inches (0 to 5 cm), making it suitable for clamping onto pipes, poles, tables, and other irregular surfaces. This rugged clamp is widely used to secure lights, flags, reflectors, cameras, and grip equipment with precision and stability.

Constructed from anodized aluminum and stainless steel, the Cardellini End Jaw Clamp provides strength and durability, ensuring a secure hold in demanding environments. Its end jaw design allows it to clamp onto objects at the very end of the jaws, making it ideal for use in tight spaces or on the edges of surfaces where traditional clamps cannot reach. The 5/8" baby pin integrated into the clamp allows for easy attachment of lights, modifiers, and other accessories, making it an indispensable tool for any grip department.

This clamp is well-suited for professional productions, offering a secure and adjustable grip that can adapt to a variety of surfaces and equipment. Its compact design makes it easy to transport and use on location, providing flexibility for rigging setups in both studio and outdoor environments.



Specifications

  • Model: Cardellini End Jaw Clamp
  • Jaw Opening: 0 to 2 inches (0 to 5 cm)
  • Material: Anodized aluminum and stainless steel
  • Spigot: 5/8" (16mm) baby pin
  • Weight: 0.9 kg (2 lbs)
  • Application: Grips onto pipes, poles, tables, and other surfaces


Key Features

  • Durable anodized aluminum and stainless steel construction
  • End jaw design for clamping onto edges and irregular surfaces
  • Adjustable jaw opens from 0 to 2 inches (0 to 5 cm)
  • 5/8" baby pin for mounting lights and accessories
  • Compact and portable design for easy transport
  • Ideal for professional grip, lighting, and rigging applications
