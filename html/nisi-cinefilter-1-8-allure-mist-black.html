NiSi 1/8 Allure Mist Filter



Inventory

1 Available



The NiSi 1/8 Allure Mist Filter is a subtle diffusion filter designed to soften highlights, reduce contrast, and enhance the cinematic feel of your footage. With its 1/8 strength mist effect, this filter offers a delicate softening of light without overwhelming the scene, making it perfect for filmmakers and photographers seeking to add a subtle, organic quality to their shots.

This filter is especially useful for portraits, close-ups, and cinematic scenes, where it helps to reduce harsh digital sharpness and smooth out skin textures while preserving natural skin tones. The NiSi 1/8 Allure Mist filter is made with high-quality optical glass, ensuring that there is no noticeable reduction in image quality while still delivering the desired diffusion effect.

The filter is compatible with 4x5.65" matte boxes, allowing it to be seamlessly integrated into a professional production setup. Whether used in film, television, or commercial photography, the NiSi Allure Mist 1/8 is an excellent tool for enhancing the visual aesthetics of your projects.



Specifications

  • Model: NiSi 1/8 Allure Mist Filter
  • Filter Type: Diffusion filter
  • Effect Strength: 1/8 strength mist effect
  • Size: 4x5.65 inches
  • Material: High-quality optical glass
  • Compatibility: Fits 4x5.65" matte boxes
  • Weight: 0.2 kg (0.44 lbs)

  • Ideal for creating a soft, cinematic look in film and video production
