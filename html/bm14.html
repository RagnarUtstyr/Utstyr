The 77mm Black Mist 1/4 Filter is a high-quality diffusion filter designed to soften highlights, add a subtle glow to bright areas, and reduce contrast. Ideal for filmmakers and photographers, this filter helps create a dreamy, cinematic look by softening harsh lighting while maintaining overall image sharpness and detail.

This Black Mist filter is perfect for portraiture, interviews, and any scene where you want to add a touch of softness to your subject, especially in high-contrast situations. The 1/4 strength offers a subtle effect, providing just the right amount of diffusion without overly softening the image.

Made from high-quality optical glass, the 77mm Black Mist 1/4 Filter ensures that there is no loss of clarity, while still producing a smooth and pleasing diffusion effect. It fits lenses with a 77mm filter thread, making it compatible with a wide range of professional camera setups.
