BNC → SMA Adapter



The BNC to SMA Adapter allows you to connect BNC-terminated coaxial cables to SMA connectors, typically found on RF gear, antennas, wireless transmitters, and timecode sync devices.

This adapter is particularly useful in camera or sound workflows where SDI video lines or timecode outputs need to interface with compact wireless or RF devices using SMA connections. It's built for signal clarity and long-term durability in field use.

Compatible with 75Ω and 50Ω systems, this adapter is a staple in RF, video assist, and wireless antenna routing kits.



Specifications

  • Type: BNC (female) to SMA (male)
  • Impedance: 50Ω (SMA) / compatible with 75Ω (BNC)
  • Application: RF, timecode, wireless video, antennas
  • Material: Nickel-plated body with gold-plated center pin
  • Connector Style: Straight


Key Features

  • Adapts BNC to SMA interface for wireless and RF applications
  • Used in timecode, antenna distribution, and camera RF workflows
  • Secure threaded SMA connection prevents accidental disconnects
  • Compact and durable design for field or studio use
  • Essential adapter for RF kits and sync systems
