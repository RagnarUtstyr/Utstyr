The Manfrotto Folding Mini Stand (012B) is a lightweight, highly portable lighting stand perfect for compact light fixtures, LED panels, or practical use in tight spaces. Its folding legs and compact size make it ideal for travel kits, table setups, or low-angle lighting on location or in studio environments.

Despite its small footprint, the Folding Mini Stand provides solid support for smaller gear and is crafted from black anodized aluminum for durability with a discreet, professional finish. Its 3-section riser allows flexible height adjustment, and the stand features a standard 5/8" spigot for compatibility with most lighting fixtures.

Whether you're lighting a product shot, tabletop scene, or hiding a practical light in-frame, the Folding Mini Stand offers quick deployment and sturdy performance in a minimal form factor.



Specifications

  • Height Range: 48 cm to 123 cm (18.9" to 48.4")
