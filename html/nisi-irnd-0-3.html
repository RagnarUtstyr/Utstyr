NiSi IRND 0.3 Filter



Inventory

1 Available



The NiSi IRND 0.3 Filter is a neutral density (ND) filter designed for professional cinematographers and photographers. With an optical density of 0.3, this filter reduces the light entering the lens by 1 stop, allowing for greater control over exposure settings in bright environments. The IR (Infrared) technology incorporated in the filter minimizes the potential color shifts caused by infrared light, ensuring accurate color rendition even when shooting in challenging lighting conditions.

The NiSi IRND 0.3 filter is ideal for controlling depth of field in bright conditions, enabling cinematographers and photographers to use wider apertures without overexposing their images. It is perfect for situations requiring a balanced exposure, such as outdoor shoots, landscape photography, or long-exposure photography.

Constructed from high-quality optical glass, the filter ensures no loss of image quality while providing consistent and even reduction of light across the frame. Designed to fit in 4x5.65" matte boxes, this filter is compatible with a wide range of professional cinema cameras and lenses, offering versatility and precision in light control.



Specifications

  • Model: NiSi IRND 0.3
  • Filter Type: Neutral Density (ND) with Infrared protection
  • Optical Density: 0.3 (1 stop)
  • Size: 4x5.65 inches
  • Material: High-quality optical glass
  • Compatibility: Fits 4x5.65" matte boxes
  • Weight: 0.2 kg (0.44 lbs)


Key Features

  • Neutral density filter with 0.3 density (1 stop reduction)
  • IR technology for minimizing color shifts due to infrared light
  • Ideal for outdoor, landscape, and long-exposure photography
  • Enables wider apertures in bright conditions for depth-of-field control
  • High-quality optical glass for sharpness and clarity
  • Compatible with 4x5.65" matte boxes for professional cinema setups
