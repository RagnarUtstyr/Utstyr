Shoot35 Cinefocus Pro Follow Focus



Inventory

1 Available



The Shoot35 Cinefocus Pro is a precision-engineered follow focus system designed for filmmakers who demand smooth and accurate focus control. It is compatible with a variety of DSLR and cinema lenses and built to work seamlessly with standard 15mm rod systems.

The Cinefocus Pro features a fluid-feel gearbox for responsive focus pulls, along with adjustable backlash control. It includes a removable marking disc and interchangeable focus gears, making it easy to customize your rig for different setups.

With durable construction and reliable performance, the Cinefocus Pro is ideal for independent productions, commercials, and run-and-gun scenarios. Whether you're working solo or with a focus puller, this follow focus system offers professional-grade functionality at an accessible size and weight.



Specifications

  • Model: Shoot35 Cinefocus Pro
  • Compatibility: 15mm rod systems
  • Lens Compatibility: DSLR and cine-style lenses
  • Gear Drive: Interchangeable and adjustable
  • Material: CNC-machined aluminum and stainless steel
  • Marking Disc: Removable and reusable
  • Backlash Adjustment: Yes
  • Weight: Approx. 0.9 kg (2 lbs)
