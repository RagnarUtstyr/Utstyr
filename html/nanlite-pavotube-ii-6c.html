The Nanlite PavoTube II 6C is a portable, battery-powered RGBWW LED tube light, perfect for filmmakers, photographers, and content creators. Compact yet powerful, this 10-inch tube light is designed for creative lighting setups with full control over color, intensity, and lighting effects. With a CCT range of 2700K to 7500K, the PavoTube II 6C provides both warm tungsten and cool daylight balance, making it versatile for any shooting environment.

The PavoTube II 6C features built-in RGB mode, which allows users to choose from over 360 colors and adjust saturation and hue, providing endless creative possibilities for color lighting. It also includes 15 special effects such as police car, candlelight, lightning, and fireworks, adding dynamic motion to any shoot. The built-in rechargeable battery lasts up to 65 minutes at full power and can be recharged via USB-C.

This lightweight tube is ideal for on-the-go lighting setups and can be easily mounted using the 1/4"-20 mounting points or the built-in magnets on either end, allowing for quick attachment to metal surfaces. With its compact size and creative lighting options, the Nanlite PavoTube II 6C is perfect for run-and-gun shooting, product photography, or adding vibrant colors to your scene.
