Rubber Box Power Distro 32A Input with 4x16A Output



Inventory

2 Available



The Rubber Box Power Distro is a rugged and reliable power distribution unit, designed for use in professional environments such as film sets, live events, and industrial settings. This unit features a 32A input with 4x 16A outputs, all running on 230V, providing safe and efficient power distribution for a wide range of electrical equipment.

Built with durability in mind, the Rubber Box Power Distro is housed in a tough rubberized enclosure, making it resistant to physical damage and harsh environmental conditions. It is equipped with circuit breakers for each output, ensuring safety and protection against electrical overloads. This distribution box is a trusted solution for professionals who need reliable power on demanding sets.



Specifications

  • Model: Rubber Box Power Distro
  • Input: 32A, 230V
  • Outputs: 4x 16A, 230V
  • Enclosure: Rugged rubberized housing
  • Protection: Individual circuit breakers for each output
  • Weight: Approx. 4 kg
  • Use Cases: Film production, live events, industrial applications
