Sennheiser ME66 Shotgun Microphone



Inventory

1 Available



The Sennheiser ME66 is a highly directional short shotgun microphone module designed for broadcast, film, television, and location recording. It is ideal for capturing dialogue, sound effects, and location audio in challenging environments where background noise is an issue. The ME66 capsule offers a supercardioid/lobar polar pattern, which ensures excellent off-axis rejection and focuses on the sound source in front of the mic, minimizing unwanted ambient noise.

Paired with the Sennheiser K6 powering module (sold separately), the ME66 provides a high-output signal with low self-noise, making it perfect for use in documentary work, ENG (electronic news gathering), interviews, and film production. Its rugged, metal construction ensures long-term durability, even in demanding environments.

The Sennheiser ME66 also benefits from a wide frequency response of 40Hz to 20kHz, capturing sound with clarity and detail. With its compact size and lightweight design, this microphone is easy to mount on a boom pole, camera, or handheld grip, offering flexibility and mobility on set.



Specifications

  • Model: Sennheiser ME66
  • Type: Short shotgun microphone
  • Polar Pattern: Supercardioid/lobar
  • Frequency Response: 40Hz - 20kHz
  • Sensitivity: 50 mV/Pa +/- 2.5 dB
  • Maximum SPL: 125 dB SPL
  • Output Connector: XLR-3M (requires K6 powering module)
  • Dimensions: 22.5 x 0.8 cm (8.85 x 0.78 inches)
  • Weight: 65 g (2.3 oz)


Key Features

  • Highly directional shotgun microphone with supercardioid/lobar polar pattern
  • Designed for professional broadcast, film, and location recording
  • Excellent off-axis rejection for minimizing background noise
  • Compatible with Sennheiser K6 powering module
  • Wide frequency response of 40Hz to 20kHz for detailed audio capture
  • Compact and lightweight for easy mounting on boom poles and cameras
  • Durable metal construction for long-term use
