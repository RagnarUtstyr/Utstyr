Tilta MB T-12 Matte Box



Inventory

1 Available



The Tilta MB T-12 Matte Box is a high-quality, lightweight matte box designed for professional filmmaking and cinematography. It is built to accommodate 4x5.65" filters and can be easily attached to a wide variety of camera setups, offering superior light control and versatile filter usage. Whether shooting indoors or outdoors, the Tilta MB T-12 helps filmmakers reduce glare and control reflections for enhanced image clarity.

The MB T-12 features a clamp-on design that makes it quick and easy to mount on lenses of various sizes, ranging from 80mm to 114mm, without the need for additional tools. It supports up to three 4x5.65" filters simultaneously, allowing you to use ND filters, polarizers, and diffusion filters together for ultimate creative control over your footage.

Constructed from durable materials, the Tilta MB T-12 is built to withstand the rigors of professional use on set. The matte box is designed to minimize internal reflections, improving the overall quality of the captured footage. The adjustable top flag and side wings offer additional protection from unwanted light flares, further enhancing the flexibility of this versatile tool.



Specifications

  • Model: Tilta MB T-12
  • Material: Aluminum and high-quality plastics
  • Filter Compatibility: 4x5.65" filters (up to 3)
  • Lens Compatibility: 80mm to 114mm diameter lenses
  • Mounting Type: Clamp-on design
  • Weight: 0.8 kg (1.76 lbs)


Key Features

  • Clamp-on matte box for quick and easy mounting
  • Supports up to three 4x5.65" filters simultaneously
  • Compatible with lens diameters ranging from 80mm to 114mm
  • Durable construction with aluminum and high-quality plastics
  • Top flag and side wings for extra light control
  • Perfect for reducing glare and controlling reflections
