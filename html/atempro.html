Blackmagic ATEM Mini Pro



Inventory

1 Available



The Blackmagic ATEM Mini Pro is a compact yet powerful live production switcher designed for multi-camera live streaming. With four HDMI inputs, an integrated hardware encoder for direct streaming, and a versatile control panel, you can seamlessly switch between sources, add effects, and broadcast content to platforms like YouTube, Facebook Live, Twitch, and more.

Thanks to built-in multi-view functionality, you can monitor all video sources, audio levels, and recording status in real time. Its USB output acts as a webcam source, making integration with popular streaming software simple and intuitive. Whether you’re switching a live event, creating online tutorials, or producing a professional broadcast, the ATEM Mini Pro provides a robust, all-in-one solution.



Specifications

  • Video Inputs: 4x HDMI (up to 1080p60)
  • Video Output: 1x HDMI (Program/Multiview), USB Webcam Out, Ethernet for direct streaming
  • Audio Inputs: 2x 3.5mm stereo mini jack, embedded HDMI audio
  • Streaming Engine: Hardware-based H.264 encoder for live streaming
  • Control: Front panel with buttons and integrated LCD, plus ATEM Software Control over USB or Ethernet
  • Multiview: Single-screen preview of all four cameras, audio levels, recording status, and streaming info
  • Power: External 12V DC power supply


Key Features

  • 4 HDMI Inputs: Seamlessly switch between four video sources in up to 1080p resolution.
  • Integrated Streaming: Hardware encoder for direct streaming via Ethernet to popular platforms.
  • USB Output: Appears as a standard webcam source, simplifying connection with Zoom, Skype, or streaming apps.
  • Multiview Monitoring: View up to four cameras on a single monitor along with audio meters and status indicators.
  • Onboard Control Panel: Quick access buttons for transitions, audio mixing, picture-in-picture, and more.
  • ATEM Software Control: Free application for advanced settings, macro creation, media pool updates, and remote switching.
