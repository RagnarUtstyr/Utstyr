C-Stand 40



Inventory

9 Available



The C-Stand 40 is a versatile, robust grip stand designed for supporting lights, modifiers, reflectors, and other gear in film, television, and photography production. The 40-inch arm provides ample reach and precise placement, making it an essential tool for rigging in studios or on-location shoots. Built from high-quality chrome-plated steel, the C-Stand 40 offers strength, durability, and long-lasting performance.

This stand includes a 3-section riser and a detachable turtle base, making it easy to transport and set up. The grip head and arm allow for precise positioning of flags, nets, and other modifiers to shape and control the light. The turtle base folds flat for easy storage and transport, and the stand’s design offers maximum flexibility for mounting equipment in tight or hard-to-reach spaces.

The C-Stand 40 is widely used in professional productions due to its ability to support heavier gear while maintaining stability. Its wide base ensures secure support, while the 40-inch arm enables the extension and positioning of accessories exactly where needed. This stand is ideal for mounting larger lighting setups, providing both height and reach for various rigging scenarios.



Specifications

  • Model: C-Stand 40
  • Material: Black or Chrome-plated steel
  • Height Range: 1.2 m to 3.1 m (4 feet to 10.5 feet)
  • Risers: 3-section riser
  • Base Type: Detachable turtle base
  • Weight Capacity: 10 kg (22 lbs)
  • Weight: 5.3 kg (11.7 lbs)
  • Arm Length: 102 cm (40 inches)

  • Grip head and 40-inch arm for precise adjustments
