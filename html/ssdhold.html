SmallRig SSD Mount for Camera



Inventory

1 Available



The SmallRig SSD Mount is a secure, adjustable holder designed to attach an external SSD to your camera cage or rig setup. Ideal for shooters who record directly to portable SSDs for higher data rates and quick file transfers, it offers reliable protection while keeping your drive easily accessible on set.

Engineered from durable aluminum alloy, the mount features adjustable brackets to fit a range of SSDs, including popular models like the Samsung T5. Thanks to an integrated NATO rail clamp and convenient thumb screws, installation and repositioning are fast and hassle-free.

Whether you're working on a DSLR, mirrorless, or cinema camera rig, this SSD mount helps you maintain a compact layout, protect against accidental drops or cable pulls, and ensure a clean, organized workflow.

  • Brand: SmallRig
  • Model: SSD Mount for Camera
  • Material: Aluminum Alloy
  • Compatibility: Fits common 2.5" or portable SSDs (e.g., Samsung T5)
  • Mounting: NATO rail clamp, 1/4"-20 threaded holes
