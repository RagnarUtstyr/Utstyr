Aputure LS 600d Pro LED Light



Inventory

1 Available



The Aputure LS 600d Pro is a powerful 600W daylight-balanced LED fixture designed to deliver intense brightness and performance for high-end film and video production. With a 5600K color temperature and CRI/TLCI ratings of 96+, the LS 600d Pro provides incredible color accuracy, making it ideal for both studio and location shoots. It produces up to 100,000 lux at 1 meter with the included F10 Fresnel, delivering more than enough power for challenging lighting scenarios.

This flagship model in the Aputure Light Storm series features a Bowens Mount, allowing for compatibility with a wide range of modifiers such as softboxes, reflectors, and barndoors. It also supports wireless control via the Sidus Link App and DMX512 for professional integration into larger lighting setups. The LS 600d Pro is weather-resistant, making it perfect for outdoor shoots even in harsh conditions.

With multiple power options, the LS 600d Pro can be powered via AC or battery using V-Mount or Gold Mount batteries. It also features battery hot-swap capability, allowing users to change power sources without interrupting the light's operation. The advanced control box provides intuitive controls and can also be remotely operated for maximum flexibility on set.
