Lilliput BM280-4KS – 28″ 4K Monitor with 3D LUTS and HDR



Inventory

1 Available



The Lilliput BM280-4KS is a professional-grade 28″ 4K monitor designed for broadcast, cinematography, and post-production. With a UHD 4K resolution of 3840x2160, this monitor delivers razor-sharp images and high-definition clarity, making it perfect for detailed image review and color grading.

The BM280-4KS supports HDR (High Dynamic Range) for enhanced contrast and vivid color reproduction, providing a more realistic and immersive viewing experience. It is also equipped with 3D LUT functionality, allowing users to load custom LUTs for accurate color grading and visual effects monitoring.

With 12G-SDI, HDMI 2.0, and DisplayPort inputs, the BM280-4KS is compatible with a wide range of professional cameras and production gear. Its rugged aluminum chassis makes it ideal for both studio and field use, while its high brightness ensures excellent visibility in outdoor environments.

  • Display Size: 28 inches

  • Aspect Ratio: 16:9
  • Inputs: 12G-SDI, HDMI 2.0, DisplayPort
