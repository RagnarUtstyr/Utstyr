IDX iMicro 98 V-Mount Battery



Inventory

6 Available



The IDX iMicro 98 V-Mount Battery is a compact, high-performance V-mount lithium-ion battery designed for lightweight rigs and portable setups where space and weight are critical. With a 98Wh capacity, the iMicro 98 is ideal for powering small cameras, monitors, and accessories without adding bulk or weight to your equipment.

Despite its compact size, the iMicro 98 features two D-Tap outputs for powering additional accessories. It offers uninterrupted power in a form factor that is suitable for gimbal work, drone shooting, or handheld setups.

The battery comes equipped with a 5-LED status indicator, allowing users to monitor the remaining battery life at a glance. The iMicro 98 is also compatible with IDX's V-Mount chargers for fast and efficient recharging, making it a reliable solution for extended shooting times in the field.



Specifications

  • Model: IDX iMicro 98
  • Battery Type: Lithium-ion
  • Capacity: 98Wh (14.5V, 6.8Ah)
  • Max Load: 10A
  • Outputs: 2x D-Tap (unregulated 14.5V)
  • Mount Type: V-Mount
  • Charging Time: Approximately 2.5 hours with IDX VL-2X charger
  • Weight: 500g (1.1 lbs)
  • Dimensions: 9 x 7.5 x 5.3 cm (3.5 x 2.9 x 2.1 inches)


Key Features

  • Compact design, ideal for lightweight rigs and gimbal setups
  • 98Wh capacity for powering cameras and accessories
  • Two D-Tap outputs for powering additional accessories
  • USB port for charging mobile devices
  • 5-LED battery status indicator for real-time power updates
  • Compatible with IDX V-mount chargers for fast charging
  • Perfect for gimbal work, drone shooting, or portable setups
