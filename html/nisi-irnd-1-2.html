NiSi IRND 1.2 Filter



Inventory

1 Available



The NiSi IRND 1.2 Filter is a neutral density (ND) filter designed for professional filmmakers and photographers. It reduces light entering the lens by 4 stops, allowing greater control over exposure settings in bright conditions. This makes it perfect for outdoor shoots, landscape photography, and cinematic production where you need to maintain control over depth of field and shutter speed.

The IR (Infrared) technology integrated into the filter helps prevent color shifts that can be caused by infrared light, ensuring accurate color rendition and a natural look in your footage or stills. The NiSi IRND 1.2 is especially useful when you need to shoot at wide apertures in bright daylight or capture long exposures for creative effects like motion blur.

Constructed with high-quality optical glass, this filter provides excellent sharpness and clarity, ensuring that your image quality remains intact while controlling the light levels. Designed to fit into 4x5.65" matte boxes, this filter integrates seamlessly with a variety of professional cinema cameras and lenses.



Specifications

  • Model: NiSi IRND 1.2
  • Filter Type: Neutral Density (ND) with Infrared protection
  • Optical Density: 1.2 (4 stops)
  • Size: 4x5.65 inches
  • Material: High-quality optical glass
  • Compatibility: Fits 4x5.65" matte boxes
  • Weight: 0.2 kg (0.44 lbs)


Key Features

  • Neutral density filter with 1.2 density (4-stop reduction)
  • IR technology to prevent color shifts caused by infrared light
  • Ideal for outdoor shoots, long exposures, and cinematic productions
  • Allows for slower shutter speeds or wider apertures in bright conditions
  • High-quality optical glass for superior sharpness and clarity
  • Compatible with 4x5.65" matte boxes for professional cinema setups
