Schuko to PowerCON Cable



Inventory

4 Available



This Schuko to PowerCON adapter cable allows for seamless connection between standard European Schuko outlets and devices requiring a PowerCON input. It’s perfect for powering professional audio, video, or lighting equipment in studio, event, or touring environments.

Constructed with high-grade connectors and flexible rubber-insulated cable, this lead ensures reliable power delivery and secure locking with the PowerCON blue connector. Ideal for use with LED fixtures, hazers, switchers, and other stage equipment.

Its compact and durable build makes it ideal for rental setups where quick deployment and rugged reliability are key. Color-coded plugs ensure easy identification during setup and breakdown.



Specifications

  • Connector A: Schuko (CEE 7/7) Male
  • Connector B: PowerCON Blue (Neutrik NAC3FCA)
  • Voltage Rating: 250V AC
  • Current Rating: 16A max
  • Cable Type: H07RN-F 3G1.5mm² rubber cable
  • Length: 1.5 m
  • Insulation: Weather-resistant flexible rubber
  • Color: Black with blue PowerCON plug


Key Features

  • Connects standard Schuko outlet to PowerCON-equipped devices
  • Heavy-duty cable with professional locking connector
  • Ideal for stage, studio, and AV equipment
  • Safe and secure connection with high power rating
  • Rugged and portable design for repeated use
