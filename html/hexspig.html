This Baby Pin Spigot with 5/8" Hex Stud is a versatile mounting adapter commonly used in film, video, and photography production. The 5/8" "Baby" pin fits into standard grip heads, clamps, or stands, allowing you to mount lights, modifiers, or other accessories securely. The hexagonal shape provides extra grip to prevent rotation under load.

Made of high-quality steel with a corrosion-resistant finish, it delivers reliable performance on set, even in challenging environments. Its compact size makes it easy to pack and carry, while the universal design ensures you can seamlessly integrate it into your existing grip and support equipment.

Whether you need a simple spigot for your LED lights or a sturdy point of attachment for rigging small accessories, this baby pin with hex stud is a must-have component in any professional grip kit.
