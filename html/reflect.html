Foldable Reflector



Inventory

2 Available



This versatile Foldable Reflector is an essential tool for shaping and bouncing light during photo or video shoots. It typically features multiple surfaces—such as silver, gold, white, black, and translucent—so you can adapt to a variety of lighting scenarios. Ideal for softening harsh direct light, filling shadows, or adding warmth, this reflector is both lightweight and easy to set up, whether on location or in the studio.

Designed to collapse into a compact carrying case, it’s conveniently portable and can easily fit into most gear bags. The sturdy but flexible frame pops open in seconds, and the different covers or surfaces can be zipped on and off as needed, giving you maximum control over your lighting setup.



Specifications

  • Diameter (Opened): 110 cm (approx.)
  • Surfaces: Silver, Gold, White, Black, Translucent (5-in-1)
  • Folded Size: ~40 cm diameter
  • Weight: ~500 g
  • Material: Durable fabric with a spring steel frame
  • Usage: Fill light, bounce light, control shadows, add warmth or contrast


Scope of Delivery

  • 1x Foldable Reflector (5-in-1 surfaces)
  • 1x Carrying Case


Key Features

  • Multiple reflective surfaces in one compact design
  • Lightweight and easy to fold for portability
  • Quick setup: pops open for immediate use
  • Durable frame and fabric for long-term reliability
  • Great for both indoor and outdoor shooting environments
