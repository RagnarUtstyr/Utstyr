The DJI Mavic Mini (1st Gen) is an ultra-lightweight drone designed for creators seeking smooth, high-quality footage with minimal setup. Weighing under 250 grams, it requires no special registration in many regions while still delivering stable flights and clear 2.7K video.

Its 3-axis motorized gimbal ensures cinematic stabilization, and the convenient folding design makes it travel-friendly and easy to store. The included remote control boasts a low-latency video feed, enabling precise piloting and reliable distance control.

With up to 30 minutes of flight time on a single charge, plus intuitive features like QuickShots and simplified editing through the DJI Fly app, the Mavic Mini transforms everyday scenes into extraordinary visual stories.



Specifications

  • Model: DJI Mavic Mini (1st Gen)
  • Camera: 2.7K video at 30 fps, 12 MP photos
  • Flight Time: Up to 30 minutes (per battery)
  • Weight: < 250 g (0.55 lbs)
  • Max Range: Approx. 4 km (2.5 miles) in FCC mode
  • Gimbal: 3-axis mechanical stabilization
  • App: DJI Fly (Android/iOS)
