RED Epic Dragon 6K



Inventory

1 Available



The RED Epic Dragon 6K is a professional-grade digital cinema camera designed to deliver unparalleled image quality. With a 6K Dragon sensor, this camera captures stunning visuals with an extraordinary level of detail and dynamic range, making it ideal for high-end filmmaking and commercial productions. Its modular design offers flexibility for various shooting scenarios, allowing users to configure the camera with the necessary accessories for both studio and location work.

Capable of capturing up to 75 fps at 6K full frame, the RED Epic Dragon offers more than just resolution — it provides an impressive 16.5 stops of dynamic range, delivering rich, cinematic images in a wide variety of lighting conditions. Its ability to shoot in REDCODE RAW ensures you get the most out of each shot in post-production, giving filmmakers exceptional control over exposure, color grading, and detail recovery.

The Dragon sensor is known for its versatility, performing excellently in both daylight and low-light conditions. The camera also supports a variety of lenses with interchangeable mounts, including PL, EF, and other third-party options, making it adaptable to any production environment. Compact and lightweight for a cinema camera, the RED Epic Dragon is an industry favorite for commercials, feature films, and high-end TV productions.



Specifications

  • Model: RED Epic Dragon 6K
  • Sensor: 19 MP Super 35mm Dragon CMOS
  • Resolution: 6K (up to 6144 x 3160)
  • Frame Rates: 1-75 fps at 6K, 1-100 fps at 5K, 1-150 fps at 4K
  • Dynamic Range: 16.5+ stops
  • Recording Format: REDCODE RAW, Apple ProRes, Avid DNxHD
  • Lens Mount: Interchangeable PL, EF, Nikon, Leica-M mounts
  • ISO Range: 200-2000 (Expandable)
  • Weight: 2.26 kg (5 lbs, body only)


Key Features

  • 6K Dragon sensor with 19 MP resolution for stunning image quality
  • 16.5+ stops of dynamic range for detailed highlights and shadows
  • Capable of 75 fps in 6K full frame and up to 150 fps in 4K
  • Supports REDCODE RAW, Apple ProRes, and Avid DNxHD recording formats
  • Interchangeable lens mounts for PL, EF, and other third-party lenses
  • Modular design for flexible configurations, perfect for studio or on-location shoots
