The Shure SM58 is a legendary dynamic vocal microphone that has set the industry standard for decades. Known for its durability, warmth, and clarity, the SM58 is trusted by vocalists, broadcasters, and live performers around the world.

Its tailored frequency response emphasizes vocals while reducing background noise, and the cardioid pickup pattern isolates the main sound source effectively. The built-in spherical wind and pop filter helps minimize breath noise and plosives.

With rugged construction, proven performance, and a timeless sound signature, the Shure SM58 is a go-to choice for both stage and studio environments.



Specifications

  • Model: Shure SM58
  • Type: Dynamic microphone
  • Polar Pattern: Cardioid
  • Frequency Response: 50Hz – 15kHz
  • Sensitivity: -54.5 dBV/Pa (1.85 mV)
  • Impedance: 150 ohms (actual)
  • Connector: XLR-3M
  • Weight: 298 g (10.5 oz)
