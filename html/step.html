Step Up/Down Filter Rings



Inventory

Multiple Sizes Available



Step Up and Step Down Filter Rings are essential accessories for adapting different thread sizes between your camera lenses and screw-on filters. Whether you're trying to mount a 77mm ND filter onto a 67mm lens, or vice versa, these rings ensure compatibility across your gear without needing duplicates of the same filter.

Constructed from lightweight aluminum alloy with precision threading, these rings offer a secure fit with minimal bulk. They’re perfect for photographers and cinematographers looking to reduce kit weight and increase flexibility on set.

Sets typically include common thread conversions like 52mm → 58mm, 67mm → 77mm (step-up), or 82mm → 77mm (step-down), allowing you to cover multiple lens setups with fewer filters.



Specifications

  • Type: Step Up & Step Down Filter Adapter Rings
  • Material: Lightweight anodized aluminum
  • Available Sizes: 52mm, 58mm, 67mm, 72mm, 77mm, 82mm
  • Thread Pitch: Standard filter threads (0.75mm pitch)
  • Finish: Matte black to reduce reflections


Key Features

  • Adapts filters to different lens thread sizes
  • Minimizes the need to own multiple filters
  • Durable and lightweight design for field use
  • Precise fit with secure threading
  • Compatible with UV, ND, CPL, and other circular filters
