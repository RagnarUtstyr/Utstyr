Overview

A 100mm bowl high hat is a compact and sturdy mounting solution for low-angle shots or tight spaces where standard tripods may not fit. Built with durable metal and fitted with a 100mm bowl, this accessory accommodates many professional fluid heads, ensuring a stable base for your camera rig on the floor, mounted to a board, or secured in otherwise challenging environments.



Key Features

  • 100mm Bowl Compatibility: Accepts most 100mm fluid heads for professional cameras.
  • Robust Metal Construction: Ensures stability and confidence when shooting on uneven surfaces or at extreme angles.
  • Compact Design: Ideal for low-angle setups or close-quarter filming scenarios.
  • Versatile Mounting: Can be secured to a wooden board, stairs, or other surfaces to capture unique perspectives.
  • Portable & Lightweight: Easy to carry and deploy quickly on set.


Typical Usage Scenarios

  • Low-Angle Shots: Capture dramatic, ground-level perspectives for creative storytelling.
  • Close Quarters: Perfect for working in tight spaces where a normal tripod footprint won’t fit.
  • Unconventional Mounting: Easily attach to various surfaces (e.g., plywood, apple boxes) for custom rigging solutions.


Advantages of a 100mm Bowl System

  • Professional Fluid Head Support: 100mm bowl is widely used in film and video production, ensuring compatibility.
  • Stable & Secure: Provides firm support for mid-to-large cameras without excessive wobble.


Considerations

  • Surface Condition: For best results, ensure the high hat is placed on a stable, level surface or is firmly secured.
  • Load Capacity: Always check the maximum supported weight when pairing with heavier camera setups.
  • Accessories: You may need additional hardware (e.g., screws, wooden boards) to safely attach the high hat in unconventional setups.
