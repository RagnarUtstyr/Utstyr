Gravity LS Vari-Pole 01 B (2.1m - 3.7m)



Inventory

1 Available



The Gravity LS Vari-Pole 01 B is an adjustable tension pole designed for mounting lights, backdrops, and other production equipment. With an adjustable height range of 2.1m to 3.7m (6.9 to 12.1 ft), this versatile pole is ideal for studio setups, photo shoots, and on-location work where traditional stands may not be feasible.

Featuring a spring-loaded mechanism, the Gravity Vari-Pole can be easily installed between floors and ceilings without the need for permanent mounting hardware, making it a great solution for temporary setups. The heavy-duty aluminum construction ensures stability and durability, even under the weight of larger lights or other equipment.

The non-slip rubber end caps provide added grip and protect surfaces from damage, while the easy-to-use tension lock allows for quick adjustments and secure positioning. Whether you’re in a tight studio space or shooting in a location where you can’t mount gear to walls or ceilings, the Gravity LS Vari-Pole offers a flexible and reliable solution.



Specifications

  • Model: Gravity LS Vari-Pole 01 B
  • Height Range: 2.1m to 3.7m (6.9 ft to 12.1 ft)
  • Material: Heavy-duty aluminum
  • Mounting Mechanism: Spring-loaded tension system
  • End Caps: Non-slip rubber for secure grip
  • Weight Capacity: Supports up to 25 kg (55 lbs)
  • Weight: 3.5 kg (7.7 lbs)
