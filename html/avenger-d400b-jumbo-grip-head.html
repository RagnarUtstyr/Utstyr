Avenger D400B Jumbo Grip Head



Inventory

2 Available



The Avenger D400B Jumbo Grip Head is a highly durable and versatile grip accessory designed for supporting large, heavy-duty lighting setups and equipment in film and photography production. The D400B is built with a 4.5-inch grip head and allows for precise and secure positioning of flags, scrims, and other light modifiers, even under demanding conditions.

Made from heavy-duty black anodized aluminum, the D400B is designed to withstand tough shooting environments. Its oversized T-handle provides extra leverage for secure tightening and ensures that even heavy equipment stays in place. The grip head is compatible with 16mm (5/8") and 28mm (1 1/8") spigots, making it compatible with a variety of stands and rigs used in professional film and photography setups.

The Jumbo Grip Head is ideal for mounting large light control tools like butterflies, overhead frames, and large reflectors. Its adjustable mechanism allows for flexible positioning and precise control over the angle and tension of modifiers. With its reliable build quality, the Avenger D400B is a staple in any grip department.
