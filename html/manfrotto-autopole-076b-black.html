The Manfrotto Autopole 076B Black is a versatile, adjustable tension pole designed for mounting lights, backdrops, and other production equipment in studios, locations, or tight spaces. With a height range of 1.5m to 2.7m (4.9 ft to 8.8 ft), this Autopole is ideal for photographers and videographers who need a flexible, portable solution for lighting rigs or set building.

The Autopole's spring-loaded locking mechanism allows for quick installation between the floor and ceiling without the need for permanent fixtures, making it perfect for temporary setups. Constructed from durable aluminum, the 076B Autopole is designed to withstand heavy-duty use while remaining lightweight and easy to transport.

The black anodized finish not only adds to its durability but also minimizes reflections during shoots, making it ideal for professional photography and video production. Whether you are suspending lights or backdrops, the Manfrotto Autopole 076B is an essential tool for any production setup.
