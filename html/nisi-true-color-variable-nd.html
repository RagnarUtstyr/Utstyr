NISI Cine Filter True Color Variable ND 1-5 Stops (ND 0.3 - 1.5) 12mm



Inventory

1 Available



The NISI Cine Filter True Color Variable ND 1-5 Stops (ND 0.3 - 1.5) 12mm is a high-quality variable neutral density (ND) filter designed for cinematographers and photographers who need versatile control over exposure. This True Color ND filter offers a variable light reduction from 1 to 5 stops (ND 0.3 to ND 1.5), making it perfect for managing exposure in various lighting conditions, from bright daylight to controlled studio environments.

The True Color technology in this filter ensures that there are no unwanted color shifts while adjusting the ND level, maintaining accurate and natural color rendition throughout your shoot. This makes it ideal for applications where color accuracy is crucial, such as commercial shoots, documentaries, and cinematic productions.

With a 12mm thickness, the NISI Cine Variable ND is robust and designed to fit into standard 4x5.65" matte boxes, making it compatible with a wide range of professional cinema cameras and lenses. The variable ND mechanism allows for smooth transitions between different ND levels, providing flexibility when changing lighting setups or shooting different scenes without having to switch out filters.

  • Model: NISI Cine Filter True Color Variable ND 1-5 Stops
  • Filter Type: Variable Neutral Density (ND)
  • ND Range: 0.3 - 1.5 (1-5 stops)
  • Size: 4x5.65 inches
  • Material: High-quality optical glass
  • Thickness: 12mm
  • Compatibility: Fits 4x5.65" matte boxes
  • Weight: 0.3 kg (0.66 lbs)

  • Ideal for a variety of lighting conditions from bright daylight to controlled studio settings
