Manfrotto C461 Tube Clamp with Junior Pin



Inventory

2 Available



The Manfrotto C461 Tube Clamp with Junior Pin is a robust clamp designed for heavy-duty applications. It fits tubes with a diameter of 48-52mm and is ideal for securely mounting lights and other equipment to studio setups. The clamp comes equipped with a Junior Pin for additional versatility, ensuring it works with a variety of industry-standard accessories.

With a Safe Working Load (SWL) of up to 500 kg, this clamp is built to withstand the rigors of professional production environments. Its durable construction and easy-to-use design make it a staple in the grip equipment arsenal for both photography and filmmaking studios.



Specifications

  • Model: C461
  • Clamp Type: Tube Clamp with Junior Pin
  • Tube Diameter: 48mm - 52mm
  • Material: High-grade aluminum
  • Weight: 1.1 kg
  • Safe Working Load (SWL): 500 kg
