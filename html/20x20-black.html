This 20x20 black fabric is designed to block or reduce light, making it ideal for large-scale set-ups requiring negative fill or a darker environment. Whether you are filming or photographing, this large fabric helps control spill light and shape shadows precisely.

It fits a standard 20x20 (approx. 600 cm x 600 cm) frame (rented separately) and is durable enough for outdoor or studio applications. By placing this fabric behind or alongside the subject, you can eliminate unwanted light and enhance contrast in your shots.



Specifications

  • Material: Opaque Black Fabric
  • Dimensions: ~600 cm x 600 cm (20x20 feet)
  • Function: Light-blocking / negative fill
  • Compatibility: Fits standard 20x20 frames (rented separately)
  • Purpose: Control spill light and create deeper shadows
