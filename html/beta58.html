The Shure Beta 58A is a high-output supercardioid dynamic vocal microphone designed for professional sound reinforcement and studio recording. Known for its tailored frequency response, durability, and feedback rejection, it's a top choice for vocalists on stage and in broadcast environments.

With a brightened midrange and bass roll-off, the Beta 58A delivers excellent clarity and presence for vocals while maintaining low handling noise. Its rugged steel mesh grille and hardened construction make it road-ready and reliable for touring or rental use.

Whether used as a lead vocal mic or for spoken word, the Shure Beta 58A stands out with its tight pickup pattern and proven performance in live sound environments.



Specifications

  • Model: Shure Beta 58A
  • Type: Dynamic microphone
  • Polar Pattern: Supercardioid
  • Frequency Response: 50Hz – 16kHz
  • Sensitivity: -51.5 dBV/Pa (2.6 mV)
  • Impedance: 150 ohms (actual)
  • Connector: XLR-3M
  • Weight: 278 g (9.8 oz)


Key Features

  • Tailored frequency response for vocals with brightened midrange
  • Supercardioid pattern isolates sound source and minimizes background noise
  • Hardened steel mesh grille resists wear and abuse
  • Neodymium magnet for high signal-to-noise output
  • Shock-mounted cartridge for low handling noise
  • Legendary Shure durability, ideal for stage use
