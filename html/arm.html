The Grip Arm is a versatile and essential piece of grip equipment used to extend the reach of a grip head for mounting flags, reflectors, scrims, or small lights. Commonly paired with a C-stand, it offers flexible rigging and fine-tuned positioning on set.

This 40-inch (102 cm) steel arm features a 5/8" baby pin on the end for mounting compatible gear, and is built to support medium-weight modifiers without bending or flexing. The arm includes knurled sections for better grip with flags and gels, and works seamlessly with most standard 2.5" grip heads.

Whether you're cutting light, bouncing fill, or flying a silk, the grip arm gives you the control and reach needed to rig safely and creatively. A must-have in any grip package.



Specifications

  • Model: Standard 40" Grip Arm
  • Length: 40 inches (102 cm)
  • Material: Steel or aluminum (varies by model)
  • End Pin: 5/8" baby pin
  • Compatibility: Fits standard 2.5" grip heads
  • Knurled Sections: Yes, for secure flag/gobo placement
  • Weight: Approx. 1.4 kg

  • Features 5/8" baby pin on end for additional gear
  • Durable construction for long-term use on set
  • Essential rigging tool for light shaping and control
