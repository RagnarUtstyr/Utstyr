The Canon EF 24-70mm f/2.8L II USM is a versatile, professional-grade zoom lens that offers exceptional image quality and performance for both photography and video applications. Featuring a constant f/2.8 aperture throughout the zoom range, this lens is ideal for capturing stunning images in a variety of lighting conditions, from landscapes and portraits to fast-moving action and low-light environments.

The 24-70mm focal range makes it highly adaptable for a wide range of shooting scenarios, providing excellent sharpness, contrast, and color reproduction. The lens incorporates Canon's Ultra Sonic Motor (USM) technology for fast and quiet autofocus, making it suitable for both still photography and video production. Its rugged build quality, weather-sealing, and high-grade optics ensure that this lens is designed to withstand professional use in challenging environments.

With multiple aspherical and Ultra-low Dispersion (UD) elements, the Canon 24-70mm f/2.8 minimizes chromatic aberrations and distortions, delivering images with remarkable clarity and precision. The lens also includes a circular 9-blade aperture for beautiful bokeh and smooth background defocus, making it a top choice for portrait photographers and filmmakers alike.



Specifications

  • Model: Canon EF 24-70mm f/2.8L II USM
  • Focal Length: 24-70mm
  • Maximum Aperture: f/2.8
  • Lens Mount: Canon EF
  • Autofocus Motor: Ultrasonic Motor (USM)
  • Minimum Focus Distance: 38 cm (15 inches)
  • Filter Size: 82mm
  • Lens Construction: 18 elements in 13 groups, including 1 Super UD and 2 UD elements
  • Aperture Blades: 9, circular diaphragm
  • Weight: 805g (1.77 lbs)
  • Dimensions: 88.5mm x 113mm (Diameter x Length)


Key Features

  • Constant f/2.8 maximum aperture throughout the zoom range
  • Ultrasonic Motor (USM) for fast, silent autofocus
  • Advanced optical design with Super UD and UD elements for high image clarity
  • Weather-sealed construction for professional use in harsh conditions
  • Circular 9-blade aperture for smooth bokeh
  • 24-70mm focal range for versatile shooting, from wide-angle to portrait
  • Designed for both full-frame and APS-C Canon cameras
