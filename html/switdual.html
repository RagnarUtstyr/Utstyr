SWIT Dual V-mount Charger

The SWIT Dual V-mount Charger is a professional battery charger designed to charge two V-mount batteries simultaneously. It supports both standard V-mount and Gold-mount batteries, providing flexibility for various camera setups. The charger ensures efficient charging, allowing for quick turnaround times on set and preventing downtime due to low battery power.

This charger is designed for use in high-demand environments like film sets, providing rapid, safe charging for your V-mount batteries. With features like multiple output channels and intelligent charging, the SWIT Dual V-mount Charger is perfect for camera operators, lighting technicians, and other professionals in the film industry who need reliable power for their equipment.

Equipped with an intelligent charging system, it automatically detects the battery type and adjusts the charging speed to prevent overcharging, ensuring longevity and safety. The charger also provides clear LED indicators for battery status, allowing you to monitor the charging process at a glance.



Specifications

  • Model: SWIT Dual V-mount Charger
  • Input: 100-240V AC, 50/60Hz
  • Output: 2x V-mount or Gold-mount batteries
  • Charging Current: 2.5A per channel

  • LED Indicators: Shows battery charging status
  • Weight: 1.6 kg (3.5 lbs)
  • Dimensions: 195mm x 120mm x 95mm (7.7 x 4.7 x 3.7 inches)
