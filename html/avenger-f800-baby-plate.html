The Avenger F800 Baby Plate is a versatile mounting solution featuring a 16mm (5/8") spigot designed for securely holding lights and other equipment on flat surfaces. This baby plate can be easily screwed or nailed onto walls, floors, ceilings, or other structures, providing a solid mounting base for lighting fixtures, flags, and modifiers. Its low-profile design and steel construction ensure durability and reliability, even in demanding production environments.

The 16mm spigot is compatible with most industry-standard baby pin mounts, making it an essential tool for rigging in both film and photography applications. The Avenger F800 is commonly used when conventional stands are not practical, offering flexibility for mounting lights in tight spaces or unconventional angles. The 5-hole base allows for multiple attachment points, ensuring secure installation on a variety of surfaces.

The Avenger F800 is lightweight, portable, and built to withstand the rigors of professional use. Its compact design makes it an excellent tool for on-location shooting, allowing filmmakers and photographers to mount lights quickly and securely.
