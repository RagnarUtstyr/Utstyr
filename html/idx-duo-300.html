IDX DUO-C 300 V-Mount Battery

The IDX DUO-C 300 is a high-capacity V-mount lithium-ion battery designed to power cinema cameras, lights, and accessories that require long-lasting power in the field or on set. With a capacity of 293Wh, the DUO-C 300 offers extended runtime and reliable performance, making it perfect for high-power draw equipment, including ARRI, RED, and Sony cinema cameras.

The DUO-C 300 features two D-Tap outputs , allowing you to power additional accessories like monitors and transmitters while running your main equipment.

The battery is equipped with an integrated 5-LED power status indicator, providing real-time updates on remaining battery life. Additionally, the DUO-C 300 is compatible with IDX’s V-mount chargers for fast and efficient recharging.



Specifications

  • Model: IDX DUO-C 300
  • Battery Type: Lithium-ion
  • Capacity: 293Wh (14.4V, 20.4Ah)
  • Max Load: 14A/168W
  • Outputs: 2x D-Tap (unregulated 14.4V), 1x USB (5V, 2.3A)
  • Mount Type: V-Mount
  • Charging Time: Approximately 5 hours with IDX VL-2X charger
  • Weight: 1.5 kg (3.3 lbs)
  • Dimensions: 16.6 x 10.1 x 6.9 cm (6.5 x 4 x 2.7 inches)


Key Features

  • High-capacity 293Wh battery for extended runtime
  • V-mount compatible with a wide range of cinema cameras and lights
  • Two D-Tap outputs for powering additional accessories
  • Compact and lightweight design for portability
