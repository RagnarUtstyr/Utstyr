Shoot35 Cinebox 4x4 Matte Box



Inventory

The Shoot35 Cinebox 4x4 is a robust and professional matte box system designed to accommodate standard 4x4" filters. It offers flexible mounting options and superior light control, making it an excellent choice for both run-and-gun shooters and studio setups.

This matte box features a swing-away arm for quick lens changes and comes with multiple filter trays, enabling easy stacking of filters. The adjustable top flag and side wings help block unwanted flares and give cinematographers more control over lighting conditions.

Built with durability in mind, the Cinebox is made from a combination of high-quality materials for use in demanding production environments. Its modular design allows compatibility with a wide range of lens diameters through adapter rings and support rods.



Specifications

  • Model: Shoot35 Cinebox 4x4
  • Material: Aluminum and ABS plastic
  • Filter Compatibility: 4x4" filters (up to 2 slots)
  • Lens Compatibility: 58mm to 114mm (via adapter rings)
  • Mounting Type: Rod-based and swing-away
  • Weight: Approx. 1.2 kg (2.65 lbs)


Key Features

  • Accepts 4x4" filters (rotating and fixed)
  • Swing-away arm for fast lens swaps
  • Adjustable top flag and side wings included
  • Strong yet lightweight aluminum and ABS construction
  • Compatible with a wide range of lenses via adapter rings
  • Rod-mounting system for added stability
