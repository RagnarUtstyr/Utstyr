USB-C to USB-A Adapter



Inventory

1 Available



This USB-C to USB-A Adapter helps bridge modern USB-C ports with older USB-A peripherals, giving you the best of both worlds. Plug the USB-C male connector into your laptop, smartphone, or other Type-C device, and instantly connect traditional USB-A accessories such as flash drives, keyboards, or card readers.

Lightweight and portable, this adapter supports high-speed data transfer and can also handle charging duties (depending on your device’s capabilities). Perfect for on-the-go use, in-office setups, or film productions requiring a variety of different gear, it offers a seamless way to expand compatibility without replacing existing equipment.
