NiSi Pola 4x5.65 Filter



Inventory

1 Available



The NiSi Pola 4x5.65 is a high-quality polarizing filter designed for professional cinematographers and photographers. Polarizing filters are essential for reducing reflections, glare, and haze from non-metallic surfaces, such as water, glass, or leaves, while improving the overall color saturation and contrast of an image.

The NiSi Pola 4x5.65 ensures that natural colors are preserved without introducing unwanted color shifts, making it ideal for scenes with water, sky, or reflective surfaces. The filter also enhances the depth and richness of blue skies, green foliage, and cloud textures, resulting in more vivid and engaging visuals.

Crafted from high-quality optical glass, the NiSi Pola 4x5.65 provides excellent image sharpness and clarity, without causing any noticeable loss in image quality. Designed to fit into 4x5.65" matte boxes, this polarizer integrates easily into professional cinema camera setups, offering superior light control for creative applications.



Specifications

  • Model: NiSi Pola 4x5.65
  • Filter Type: Polarizing Filter (Pola)
  • Size: 4x5.65 inches
  • Material: High-quality optical glass
  • Effect: Reduces reflections and glare, enhances color and contrast
  • Compatibility: Fits 4x5.65" matte boxes
  • Weight: 0.2 kg (0.44 lbs)


Key Features

  • Reduces reflections and glare from non-metallic surfaces
  • Enhances color saturation and contrast for vivid visuals
  • Perfect for outdoor, landscape, and water scenes
  • Preserves natural color without introducing color shifts
  • High-quality optical glass for sharp, clear images
  • Compatible with 4x5.65" matte boxes for professional setups
