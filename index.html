Welcome to Ragnar Bøe Equipment Rental



Ragnar Bøe is a filmworker based in Bergen, Norway, with experience working on both large and small productions. He also runs an equipment rental service, conveniently located just outside the city center of Bergen, providing a range of gear for local film projects. With a practical approach and a focus on supporting the local film community, Ragnar’s services help ensure productions have the tools they need to run smoothly.
